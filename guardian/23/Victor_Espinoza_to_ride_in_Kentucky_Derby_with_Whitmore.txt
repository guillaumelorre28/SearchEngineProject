

Victor Espinoza will ride Whitmore in the Kentucky Derby 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/kentucky-derby>, the gelding’s co-owner 
Harry Rosenblum said on Wednesday.

The Mexican jockey will take aim at a third straight victory in America’s most 
famous thoroughbred race after winning it onAmerican Pharoah 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/american-pharoah> last year and California 
Chrome in 2014.

Whitmore, the son of Pleasantly Perfect, is coming off a third-place finish in 
the Grade I Arkansas Derby and second-place runs in the Grade II Rebel Stakes 
and Grade III Southwest Stakes, all at Oaklawn Park.

Jockey Irad Ortiz Jr, who rode Whitmore in his last two starts, will ride Blue 
Grass Stakes runner-up My Man Sam in the 7 May race at Churchill Downs, 
clearing the way for Espinoza.

The 43-year-old soared to celebrity after riding American Pharoah to the first 
Triple Crown in 37 years 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2015/jun/06/american-pharoah-triple-crown-belmont-stakes>
, becoming the oldest jockey to complete what’s been called the one of the 
toughest achievements in all of sport. Four months later, Espizona rode the bay 
colt tovictory in the Breeders’ Cup Classic 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2015/oct/31/american-pharoah-breeders-cup-classic-keeneland-found-golden-horn>
 as he became the first horse to complete the so-called grand slam.

Since then Espinoza, who also won the Derby aboard War Emblem in 2002, resumed 
with California Chrome and in Marchrode him to victory in the $10m Dubai World 
Cup 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2016/mar/26/california-chrome-stunning-win-meydan-dubai-world-cup-horse-racing>
, the world’s richest race. Espinoza also won the Derby aboard War Emblem in 
2002.

Undefeated Nyquist has emerged from prep season as the Derby favorite, a 
status confirmed with hiswin in the Grade I Florida Derby 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2016/apr/02/nyquist-takes-florida-derby-showdown-to-emerge-as-kentucky-derby-favorite>
 over the also-unbeaten Mohaymen at Gulfstream Park earlier this month.
