
Bryan Cranston is set to reprise his stage role as President Lyndon B Johnson 
in theTony-winning play All the Way 
<https://www.theguardian.com/stage/2014/jun/09/bryan-cranston-scoops-tony-award-for-best-actor-in-broadway-debut>
. The biopic will follow Johnson during his first year in office after the 
assassination of John F Kennedy, and also his campaign for re-election and the 
passage of the Civil Rights Act of 1964.
