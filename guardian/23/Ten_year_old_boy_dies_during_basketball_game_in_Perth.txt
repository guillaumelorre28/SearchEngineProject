
A 10-year-old boy has collapsed and died while playing at a basketball 
tournament in Perth.

Paramedics were called to the Warwick Stadium in Perth at 10.50am on Saturday 
after the boy collapsed during a break in a game at the under-11 state 
championships.

St John Ambulance paramedics tried to revive the boy but he died in Joondalup 
hospital. It’s believed the collapse was related to an existing medical 
condition.The ABC reported 
<http://www.abc.net.au/news/2016-12-03/boy-collapses-suspected-heart-attack-at-perth-basketball-game/8090042>
 it was a suspected heart attack.
