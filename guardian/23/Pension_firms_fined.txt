
The Financial Services Authority has fined three companies £57,500 plus costs 
for rule breaches relating to the review of pensions mis-selling.

Britannia Independent, of Stoke, Staffordshire, was fined £25,000, Berry Birch 
& Noble Financial Services, of West Wickham, Kent, has been fined £17,500, and 
Horsham Financial Services, of Horsham, Sussex, was fined £15,000.
