It’s tough at the top, eh? Venezuela 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/venezuela> played really well in patches, 
and created several quality chances, but they made too many mistakes in 
defence, and couldn’t handle Lionel Messi. But who can, really? He’s just 
preposterously good at football, isn’t he? He was magnificent tonight: quick, 
busy, inventive, precise – and took his goal expertly to draw level with 
Gabriel Batistuta on 54 international goals.

Serious question: with Messi in form like this, how can USA possibly win on 
Tuesday?
