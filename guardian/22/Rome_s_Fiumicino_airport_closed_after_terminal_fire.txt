
Rome’s main airport was closed on Thursday after a fire broke out in a baggage 
storage area but there were no serious injuries, officials said.


 The airport would remain closed until 2 p.m. (1200 GMT), they said.

 The fire broke out shortly after midnight (2200 GMT) in an underground 
baggage area of Fiumicino airport’s Terminal 3, which is used for international 
flights.
