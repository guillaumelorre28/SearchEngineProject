
Born in Germany, Heidi Klum 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2015/aug/18/heidi-klum-donald-trump-video>
, 42, has been a model since the age of 19. She is the Emmy award-winning host 
ofProject Runway <http://www.bravotv.com/project-runway>, a judge on America’s 
Got Talent <http://www.nbc.com/americas-got-talent> while her business empire 
includes the lingerie rangeHeidi Klum Intimates 
<https://www.heidiklumintimates.com/>. She is divorced from the singer Seal 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/2010/sep/16/seal-interview-pop>, has four 
children, dates the art dealerVito Schnabel 
<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vito_Schnabel> and lives in Los Angeles.

When were you happiest?
The first time I saw each of my four children’s faces.

What is your greatest fear?
That anything should happen to those four cute faces.

What is your earliest memory?
Falling down our basement staircase in Germany when I was about four.

What is the trait you most deplore in yourself?
I get distracted very easily.

What is the trait you most deplore in others?
Intolerance.

Property aside, what’s the most expensive thing you’ve bought?
I have many beautiful paintings that I treasure. My accountant is probably not 
as enthusiastic about them.

What makes you unhappy?
When I am not with my loved ones.

What do you most dislike about your appearance?
I am not complaining.

If you could bring something extinct back to life, what would you choose?
A 1980s hair scrunchie.

Who would play you in the film of your life?
Amy Poehler <https://www.theguardian.com/culture/amy-poehler>: someone funny.

What is your most unappealing habit?
Falling asleep at 8pm.

Which book changed your life?
The German-to-English dictionary.

What is your guiltiest pleasure?
Sex, champagne and chocolate – especially in that order.

What did you want to be when you were growing up?
A dancer: I danced for 12 years – tap, ballet, jazz, ballroom, belly dancing. 
Then a fashion designer.

What is top of your bucket list? 
More sleep.

To whom would you most like to say sorry, and why?
When I was little, this boy would bully my friend and me all the time. One 
day, on our way home, we held him, put him to the ground and pushed his face 
full on into this big dog poo, and I’d like to say sorry.

What was the best kiss of your life?
The last one.

Which living person do you most despise and why?
All murderers.

Which words or phrases do you most overuse?
“Amazing.”

If you could go back in time, where would you go?
To Studio 54 
<https://www.theguardian.com/artanddesign/2015/mar/14/studio-54-ultimate-den-of-vice-photographs-hasse-persson>
 in 1978, because I love an amazing dance party.

When did you last cry, and why?
Three days ago, when my makeup artist poked me in the eye.

How often do you have sex?
A day or per hour?

What single thing would improve the quality of your life?
An eighth day in the week – just for me, no one else.

What keeps you awake at night?
My boyfriend.
