
Former One Nation senator Rod Culleton was treated for a suspected broken 
wrist after allegedly being assaulted outside aPerth 
<https://www.theguardian.com/australia-news/perth-news> court.

Culleton had been in court seeking a restraining order against two men when he 
was approached outside of court and allegedly assaulted on Tuesday, a 
spokeswoman said. She said he fell after being chased along the street.

A spokesman for Western Australian police said they were called to an assault 
outside of the Kings hotel, near Perth magistrates court, at 10.40am but when 
they arrived they were told the victim had taken himself to hospital.
