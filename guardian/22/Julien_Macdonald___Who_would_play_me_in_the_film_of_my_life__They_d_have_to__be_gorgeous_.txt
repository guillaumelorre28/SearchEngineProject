
Born in Merthyr Tydfil, Julien Macdonald 
<https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2010/jul/18/julien-macdonald-five-things-know-style>
, 45, studied knitwear before doing an MA at the Royal College of Art. After 
graduating in 1996, he was spotted byKarl Lagerfeld 
<https://www.theguardian.com/fashion/karl-lagerfeld> and appointed Chanel’s 
head knitwear designer. In 1998, he launchedhis own label 
<http://www.julienmacdonald.com>, and in 2001 was made creative director of 
Givenchy. He recently kicked off London fashion week with an installation of 
his signature prints. He lives in London.

When were you happiest?
With my family in Wales.

What is your earliest memory?
My two older sisters, Lesley and Beverley, dressing me up as a girl and taking 
me to our local corner shop.

Which living person do you most admire?
Karl Lagerfeld.

What is the trait you most deplore in yourself?
My sweet tooth.

What is the trait you most deplore in others?
Bad breath.

What was your most embarrassing moment?
When I dyed my hair pink.

Property aside, what’s the most expensive thing you’ve bought?
A very expensive watch. I can’t tell you what it is – I’ll get robbed.

What is your most treasured possession?
My miniature Doberman, Jake.

What makes you unhappy?
When people cry.

What do you most dislike about your appearance?
I wish I was taller: I am 5ft 6½in. I wear boots with a chunky heel.

What is your favourite word or phrase?
“Absolutely fabulous.”

What did you want to be when you were growing up?
A pop star.

What is the worst thing anyone’s said to you?
That I looked fat.

What is your guiltiest pleasure?
Cupcakes.

If you could bring something or someone back to life, what would you choose?
Marilyn Monroe – I dress her every day in my dreams.

Who would play you in the film of your life?
They’d have to be gorgeous.

What is your most unappealing habit?
You don’t want to see me do a downward dog in power yoga.

Who or what is the greatest love of your life?
My mother, Joan; she’s the oldest swinger in town. She’s kind of a Bet Lynch 
<http://coronationstreet.wikia.com/wiki/Bet_Lynch> character.

Have you ever said ‘I love you’ and not meant it?
Many times.

Who would you invite to your dream dinner party?
Liza Minnelli, Madonna, Beyoncé, Michael Jackson, Prince, David Bowie, James 
Dean and, at the head of the table, Marilyn Monroe.

What is the worst job you’ve done?
Working in a betting office on Grand National day. I was 17 and my hair was 
scrunched likeMick Hucknall <https://www.theguardian.com/music/mick-hucknall>’s.

How often do you have sex?
I had it two days ago.

What is the closest you’ve come to death?
When I climbed Mount Kilimanjaro for charity 
<http://www.vogue.co.uk/gallery/julien-macdonald-mount-kilimanjaro-climb>. It 
was horrendous.

What keeps you awake at night?
Thinking about the next day.

What song would you like played at your funeral?
Always Look On The Bright Side Of Life 
<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jHPOzQzk9Qo>.

Tell us a secret
I’ve told you loads already.
