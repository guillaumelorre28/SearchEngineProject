
A woman has died after reportedly being stabbed in a courthouse in the 
northern suburbs of Perth,Western Australia 
<https://www.theguardian.com/australia-news/western-australia>.

The woman, in her 30s, was apparently stabbed in one of the mediation rooms at 
Joondalup magistrates court about 10am on Tuesday,the ABC has reported 
<http://www.abc.net.au/news/2016-12-20/woman-attacked-in-joondalup-courthouse-mediation-room/8135832>
. She was taken to nearby Joondalup health campus but later died.

A 40-year-old man, who a police spokesman said was known to the woman, was 
taken into custody.

The court was closed to allow a forensic investigation to take place. Court 
cases scheduled for Tuesday would be relisted, a court spokeswoman said.
