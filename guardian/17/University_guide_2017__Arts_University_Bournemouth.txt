Fees £9,000 for home and EU full-time students; £13,995 for international 
students. Figures quoted are for the academic year 2016-17, checkaub.ac.uk/fees 
<http://aub.ac.uk/fees> for updates.

Bursaries and scholarships There are a mix of awards on offer, including 
funding for care leavers and for students from under-represented groups who 
have a household income below £25,000. Visitaub.ac.uk/plan-visit-apply/funding 
<http://aub.ac.uk/plan-visit-apply/funding> for full details.


Accommodation The university has halls of residence on campus and in 
Bournemouth <https://www.theguardian.com/uk/bournemouth> town centre. For the 
year 2016-17, prices range from £115-158 a week, including bills.
