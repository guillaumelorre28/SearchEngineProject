Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. £16,250 for 
international students.

Bursaries/Scholarships SOAS offers grants based on financial need and related 
to academic work. For full details visitsoas.ac.uk/registry/scholarships 
<https://www.soas.ac.uk/registry/scholarships/ug/>.

Accommodation Dinwiddy House, is available exclusively to Soas 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/school-of-oriental-and-african-studies> 
undergraduate students and is located a 20-minute walk away from the main 
campus. Hall accommodation cost approximately £150. This weekly amount includes 
utilities such as heating, lighting and hot water.
