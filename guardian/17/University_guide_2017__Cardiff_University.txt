Fees £9,000 for full-time home and EU undergraduates in 2016-17. Part-time 
fees vary by course. International fees range from £14,500 to £32,250. Fees 
quoted refer to the academic year 2016-17.


 Welsh and EU students are entitled to a tuition fee grant of up to £5,100 
which means that eligible Welsh and EU students will actually borrow £3,900 
(not £9,000) per year in 2016-17.

Bursaries and scholarships Cardiff has a range of scholarships including 
means-tested and subject specific awards. Cardiff University scholarships, 
worth £3,000 in cash payments, are available to students who achieve AAA at A 
level and who accept the offer of a place at Cardiff on a firm basis by 31 July 
2016. Visitwww.cardiff.ac.uk/scholarships 
<http://www.cardiff.ac.uk/scholarships> for more information.

Accommodation All first year students are guaranteed one of the university’s 
single study bedrooms, provided they are applying through the normal UCAS 
admissions cycle. Prices range from £93 and £120 per week for self-catered 
accommodation and around £127 per week for catered accommodation. Figures 
quoted refer to 2015-16 prices, for updates visitcardiff.ac.uk/accommodation 
<http://www.cardiff.ac.uk/for/prospective/accommodation.html>
