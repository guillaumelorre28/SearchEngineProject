Fees £1,820 per year for full-time Scottish/EU/EEA students, or £215 per 20 
credit module for part-time students in 2016-17. In most cases, Scottish and EU 
domiciled students have their fees paid for them by the Student Awards Agency 
for Scotland (SAAS).

£8,000 for students from the rest of the UK (England, Wales and N. Ireland). 

Fees for international students range from £10,000 to £11,000 for on-site 
courses.

Bursaries A range of scholarships and discretionary funds are available. Visit 
uhi.ac.uk/bursaries-and-special-funds 
<http://www.uhi.ac.uk/en/studying-at-uhi/first-steps/how-much-will-it-cost/funding/bursaries-and-special-funds>
 for more information.

Accommodation Some of the university campuses offer dedicated student 
accommodation, such as NAFC Marine Centre UHI, Scottish Association for Marine 
Science UHI, Sabhal Mòr Ostaig UHI, Lews Castle College UHI, and Perth College 
UHI.

There will be additional accommodation available this year at two further 
campuses from September 2016 at Inverness College UHI campus and at West 
Highland College UHI. The average price for campus owned/sponsored student 
accommodation is £572 a month and this typically includes all utilities and 
Wi-Fi. Some campuses also offer on-site catering to students, while others have 
shared kitchen facilities.
