Fees £9,000 for undergraduate first-degree home/EU students in 2016-17. 


For international students, £10,000 per year for most courses. Occupational 
therapy and physiotherapy are £11,500 for international students and usually 
funded by the local education and training board, NHS England for UK and EU 
students.

Scholarships The York St John bursary provides financial support (£500) to UK 
and EU families with a household income below £25,000 per anum.

Accommodation All first-years who want to can live in student accommodation. A 
range of catered and self-catered accommodation is on offer, ranging from £96 
to£123 a week.
