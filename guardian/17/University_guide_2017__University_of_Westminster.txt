Fees £9,000 for new full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. For part-time 
students, £1,500 per 20 credit module.


International fees are £12,500 for full-time courses and £2,040 per 20 credit 
module for part-time courses.

Bursaries/Scholarships Many scholarship offers will be made conditional on 
students achieving required grades, which are usually of a higher standard than 
the entry requirements for the undergraduate courses. Visit
westminster.ac.uk/scholarships/ 
<https://www.westminster.ac.uk/study/prospective-students/fees-and-funding/scholarships/uk-eu-undergraduate-scholarships>
 for more details.

Accommodation Prices for 2016-17 range from £167.65 per week for a standard 
single room with en-suite at the Harrow campus, to £269.50 per week for 
penthouse rooms at the Marylebone Campus.
