Fees £9,000 for full-time undergraduates in 2015-16. International fees vary 
but range from £13,360 to £28,320.

Bursaries Around a third of home undergraduate students are likely to be 
eligible for the University’s means-tested core bursary, which offers up to 
£2,000 for each year of undergraduate study for students who household income 
is £0-£35,000.

Nottingham potential bursaries of £1,000 are also offered to those who meet 
certain criteria. Visitnottingham.ac.uk/bursariesandscholarships 
<https://nottingham.ac.uk/studentservices/financialsupport/bursariesandscholarships/2015guaranteedbursaries.aspx>
 for more information. Figures quoted are for 2016.

Accommodation Most first years live in university accommodation. Prices vary, 
but in 2015-16 rents start at £4,136 per year. Fees for on-campus accommodation 
in catered halls starts at £169 a week for a standard room, with self-catered 
accommodation near the University Park and Jubilee Campuses starting at £98 a 
week for a standard room. Each self-catered site has a variety of rooms to 
choose from and fees will vary depending on site and room type.
