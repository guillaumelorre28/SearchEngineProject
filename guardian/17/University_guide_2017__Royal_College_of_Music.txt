Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. £21,500 for 
international students.

Bursaries Over 40% of Royal College of Music 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/royalcollegeofmusic> students benefit 
from financial support.

Any student who auditions in person for a place at the RCM will automatically 
be considered for a scholarship or study award. Scholarships are offered purely 
on the merits of performance at audition. For more information visit
rcm.ac.uk/fees-funding <http://www.rcm.ac.uk/fees-funding>.

Accommodation RCM offers accommodation in Prince Consort Village, where a 
standard room costs around £154.00. (Prices quoted are for the year 2016-17).
