Fees £9,000 for home/EU students studying degrees full-time, or £2,250 per 30 
credits for part-time students. Foundation year fees are £6,000. International 
student fees are from £11,800-12,650.

Bursaries A range of awards are on offer, including £1,000 per year for UK 
full-time undergraduate degree and foundation year students who have a 
household income of £25,000 or under.

An academic achievement bursary of £1,500 is available to new UK/EU students 
commencing year one of their three or four-year degree who have gained AAB at A 
Level (or equivalent) and GCSE grade B minimum in both Maths and English. Visit
mmu.ac.uk/study/money-matters 
<http://www2.mmu.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/money-matters> for more information.

Accommodation Room rents range from £94 per week to £143 per week in 
Manchester, and 85 per week to £95 per week in Cheshire. In total, 66% of rooms 
are en-suite. All are self-catered. The majority of halls are reserved for 
first-year students.
