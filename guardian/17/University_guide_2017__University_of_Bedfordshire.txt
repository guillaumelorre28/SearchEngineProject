Fees £9,000 for full-time home and EU undergraduates in 2016-17 or £12,000 for 
international students.

Bursaries/Scholarships The university offers care leavers’ bursaries worth 
£5,250 and sports scholarships worth up to £1,000.


Accommodation There are halls of residence on site at Luton and Bedford, 
featuring communal games areas, on-site laundry facilities, study spaces and 
gyms. Weekly accommodation prices for 2016-2017 range from £93-158 (Luton) and 
£86-164 (Bedford), and include free Wi-Fi, all utilities and 24 hour security. 
All first year students who wish to live in university accommodation can expect 
to be offered a room.
