Fees £9,000 for full-time undergraduates in 2016-17. International student 
fees are between £15,295 and £22,515.

Bursaries/Scholarships If you are a home or EU student from a lower-income 
household (less than £42,620), you will be eligible to receive an annual 
non-repayable Oxford Bursary to help with living costs of up to £4,500. Figures 
quoted are for the year 2016.

Accommodation Most students live in accommodation provided by their college. 
All colleges house first-years; many house students for subsequent years, often 
for the entirety of their course. Standard accommodation in most colleges is 
between £469-667 a month, including utilities.
