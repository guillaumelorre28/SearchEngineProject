Fees £9,000 per year for home/EU students on all courses in 2016-17, excluding 
the four year BA degree courses, where the foundation year will be charged at 
£7,500.

Bursaries A range of scholarships are available, including a £1,000 cash 
bursary per year for students from lower income household. Excellence 
scholarships, paid in cash, are also available to applicants who demonstrate 
exceptional potential at audition. Visit
lcm.ac.uk/courses/undergraduate/scholarships 
<http://www.lcm.ac.uk/courses/undergraduate/scholarships> for more details.
