Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. £18,500 for 
international students.

Bursaries/Scholarships
A number of bursaries are available to undergraduates, postgraduates and 
international applicants, made on the basis of need and merit. There are 
scholarships available to applicants who demonstrate outstanding ability and 
potential at audition. All applicants are automatically considered for an 
entrance scholarship. Visitrncm.ac.uk/fees/ 
<http://www.rncm.ac.uk/study-here/how-to-apply/fees/> for more information.

Accommodation
The college has a hall of residence two minutes’ walk away with places for 316 
RNCM students. Fees for the year 2015-16 were £147 per week on a 42 week 
contract.
