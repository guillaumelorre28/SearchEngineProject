Fees £9,000 for full-time undergraduates in 2016-17. The undergraduate tuition 
fee for home/EU part-time students is usually up to £4,500.

Tuition fees vary for international students depending on the type of course. 
Full fee information can be found atwww.le.ac.uk/fees <http://www.le.ac.uk/fees>

Bursaries/Scholarships The University of Leicester offers of scholarships 
based on academic performance and household income. There are also music and 
sports scholarships. More information about scholarships can be found at 
www.le.ac.uk/fees <http://www.le.ac.uk/fees>

Accommodation The cheapest room is £85.40 per week for a single room, 
self-catered with shared bathroom facilities. The most expensive room is 
£192.50 per week for a one-bedroom flat that is catered.

 All first years are guaranteed a place in University of Leicester-managed 
accommodation if they apply for it by September of their year of entry. More 
information about accommodation can be found at www.le.ac.uk/accommodation 
<http://www.le.ac.uk/accommodation>
