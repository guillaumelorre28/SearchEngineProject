
Rather than refusing entry to Andrei Lugovoi, Dmitry Kovtun and others on the 
Magnitsky list (Litvinenko ‘poisoners’ on banned Russian list 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2017/jan/10/litvinenko-suspects-added-to-us-russia-sanctions-list>
, 11 January), western immigration officers should be waiting to welcome them – 
and hand them straight to the police for prosecution under the principles of
universal jurisdiction <https://www.theguardian.com/law/universal-jurisdiction>.
Michael Ellman (solicitor)
London
