Fees £9,000 for full-time undergraduates in 2016-17. International fees are to 
be confirmed, visituwtsd.ac.uk/finance/tuition-fees/  
<http://www.uwtsd.ac.uk/finance/tuition-fees/>for more information.

 Welsh and EU students are entitled to a tuition fee grant of up to £5,100 
which means that eligible Welsh and EU students will actually borrow £3,900 
(not £9,000) per year in 2016-17.

Bursaries/Scholarships A range of bursaries available, including care leavers’ 
awards, departmental scholarships, subject-specific bursaries and childcare 
bursaries. Visituwtsd.ac.uk/bursaries <http://www.uwtsd.ac.uk/bursaries/> for 
full information.

Accommodation The University has a variety of rooms available, with 
first-years given priority. Weekly charges vary from £63 to £105 a week, 
depending on facilities and catering options chosen by the student.
