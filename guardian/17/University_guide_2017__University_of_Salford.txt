Fees £9,000 for home/EU full-time undergraduate students for 2016-17. For 
part-time home/EU students, fees are charged at £1,500 per 20 credits. 
International fees are £11,500 or between £1,908-£2,217 per 20 credits.

Bursaries A range of scholarships are offered. The vice-chancellor’s 
excellence scholarship is available to students who achieve ABB at A-level or 
equivalent, and is worth up to a £2,000 cash for the first year of their 
undergraduate course. The Salford student bursary is worth £3,000 for students 
on an undergraduate course who are from Greater Manchester and have a household 
income of less than £25,000.

The bursaries and scholarships listed above are for the 2016-17 academic year 
are subject to change for the 2017-18 academic year.

Accommodation Accommodation is located on campus or a short walk away. Prices 
range from £75 per week to £139 with bills included.
