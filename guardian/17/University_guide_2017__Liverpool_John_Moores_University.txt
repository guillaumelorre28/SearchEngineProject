Fees £9,000 per year for home/EU students taking full-time undergraduate 
courses, £6,750 for part-time courses. £6,000 for foundation year fees.

International fees are charged at £9,270 a year for foundation/HND programmes, 
£11,330 a year for classroom-based programmes and £12,360 for lab-based 
programmes. Figures quoted are for the year 2016-17.

Bursaries and scholarships A mix of scholarships are on offer. The LJMU 
progression bursary, worth £500 a year, is paid to full-time, home students 
with household incomes below £25,000. The bursary is worth £500 per year and 
students do not need to apply.

The LJMU academic excellence scholarship is worth £1,000 per year and is paid 
to students who attain at least 360 Ucas points from three subjects.Students 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/students> need to apply for an academic 
excellence scholarship and applications will be considered by a committee.

Accommodation Prices range from £89 for a single study bedroom in a 6 person 
self-catering flat with two bathrooms to £157 per week for a large en-ensuite 
study bedroom with a double bed. All new students can expect to receive a room 
owned by one of the university’s official accommodation partners.
