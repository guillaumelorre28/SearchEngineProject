Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. For international 
students, fees range from £15,680 to £28,000.

Bursaries/Scholarships For students with residual household incomes below 
£25,000, a York bursary of £2,400 is available in the first year then £1,800 in 
subsequent years of study provided the residual household income remains at 
£25,000 per annum or less.

For students with residual household incomes between £25,001 and £35,000 a 
York bursary of £1,000 is available in each year of study.

The York opportunity awards provide support to talented students who have 
overcome significant barriers to their educational goals. Each award is worth a 
total of £3,300.

Visit york.ac.uk/scholarships 
<http://www.york.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/fees-funding/uk-eu/scholarships/> 
for more information. Figures quoted are for the year 2016.

Accommodation All first year students are guaranteed a room, whether through 
university-managed or nominated accommodation. Prices range from £85.12 to £173 
per week.
