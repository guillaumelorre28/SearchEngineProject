Fees All bachelors and foundation degrees will cost £8,830 annually in the 
2016-17 academic year, with part-time undergraduate degrees priced at £4,300 
per annum. Fees for international students are £9,600 per year.

Bursaries A range of awards offered, including academic excellence scholarship 
and sporting excellence scholarships. Visit
ucb.ac.uk/scholarships-and-fee-waivers 
<http://www.ucb.ac.uk/learning-and-support-services/financial-support/scholarships-and-fee-waivers.aspx>
for more information.

Accommodation Prices start at £85, inclusive of heating, lighting, wifi and 
contents insurance cover. Visitucb.ac.uk/student-life/accommodation 
<http://www.ucb.ac.uk/student-life/accommodation/accommodation.aspx> for more 
information.
