
The government has unveiled a £20m fund and a new review board 
<https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2016/sep/01/southern-rail-crisis-20m-fund-announced-to-tackle-problems>
 to improve Southern rail services and restore confidence in the franchise 
following a summer of misery for passengers. The announcement came as the RMT 
union said a planned 24-hour strike by Southern rail workers next week in a 
dispute over ticket office closures had been suspended – but a 48-hour walkout 
by guards next Wednesday and Thursday would go ahead.

Though Southern trains has been in the news
