Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates starting in 2017-18. Fees for 
international students are still to be confirmed, visit
surrey.ac.uk/undergraduate/fees <http://www.surrey.ac.uk/undergraduate/fees> 
for updates.

Bursaries/Scholarships The university offers a range of financial support 
including scholarships, fee waivers and bursaries. Currently theFull Surrey 
Award 
<http://www.surrey.ac.uk/discover/undergraduate-tuition-fees-bursaries-scholarships-2016-entry>
 is targeted at students living in households with an income of less than 
£25,000 or in areas where fewer people go to university.

Sports and academic excellence awards are also available. Visit 
surrey.ac.uk/bursaries-scholarships 
<http://www.surrey.ac.uk/discover/undergraduate-tuition-fees-bursaries-scholarships-2016-entry>
 for updates and more information. Figures quoted are for the year 2016.

Accommodation All first-years are guaranteed university accommodation if they 
apply by the deadline. There is a wide range available. Single-room university 
accommodation costs ranged from £69 to £137.00. All accommodation is 
self-catered.
