Accommodation Most first year undergraduate students can expect to get a room 
at the Guildhall School’s hall of residence, Sundial Court, which is located 
around the corner from the main building and has 176 bedrooms in 39 flats.

Weekly cost in 2016-17 is £154 per week for the self-catered option, which 
includes a single room and utility bills including water, gas, electric, wifi.
