Fees £9,000 for most full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. For 
international students, £13,000 for most courses.

Bursaries/scholarships In 2016-17 the university will offer a bursary of 
£1,000 per year to new students whose household income is assessed at £25,000 
or less. The university will also offer a bursary of £500 per year to new 
entrants if household income is between £25,001 to £42,875.

Accommodation Students can rent accommodation for 46-47 weeks (from September 
to July) and the rents are payable in three instalments. The rents are between 
£99 and £150 a week, depending on size and are inclusive of all bills including 
standard internet.
