Fees UK and EU student fees are from £7,650 to £9,000 for full-time 
undergraduate Courses. International student fees are £10,000 - £12,380 for 
full-time undergraduate courses. Visitsolent.ac.uk/tuition-fees/ 
<http://www.solent.ac.uk/studying/fees/tuition-fees/what-to-pay.aspx> for more 
information.

Bursaries The university offers a range of financial support, including an 
award for care leavers worth £1,500 a year and a fee waiver. A fee waiver 
scheme for foundation level students from low income families (a whole year’s 
fees is waived, plus £1,000 cash offered)

 Academic merit scholarships (fee discounts worth up to £2,000) are available 
for international students.

Visit solent.ac.uk/studying/fees/financial-support 
<http://www.solent.ac.uk/studying/fees/financial-support/bursaries/university-bursary.aspx>
 for more details.

Accommodation For 2016/17, prices range from £86.24 to £119.14 for non 
en-suite rooms and £130.83 to £140.42 for en-suite rooms. Fully self-catered 
and single and twin study bedrooms are available.
