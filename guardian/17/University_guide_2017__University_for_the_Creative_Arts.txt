Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. Full-time home/EU 
undergraduates taking four-year degree option are charged £4,050 for the 
additional year and £9,000 per year for the next three years.

£340 per 10-credit unit for part-time home/UK undergraduates.


£12,010 for international undergraduates.

Bursaries There are UCA bursaries, UCA creative scholarships and internal 
progression bursaries available. For full details visit
uca.ac.uk/life-at-uca/fees <http://www.uca.ac.uk/life-at-uca/fees/>.

Accommodation All halls of residence are self-catering and UCA have a mixture 
of en-suite and shared bathroom facilities available.Fees are around £370-£535 
per month.
