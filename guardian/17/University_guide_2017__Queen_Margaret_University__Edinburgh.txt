Fees £1,820 for home full-time students. Part-time fees are £280 per 10 credit 
module. If you are a first degree student and ordinarily resident inScotland 
<https://www.theguardian.com/uk/scotland>, you are eligible for your tuition 
fees to be covered by the Scottish government.

£7,000 for full-time students from England, Wales & Northern Ireland, 
including the Channel Islands and the Isle of Man.


 If you are from elsewhere in the EU you are entitled to be charged the same 
level of tuition fees as Scottish students.

For full-time international (non-EU) students, £11,000 per year.

Bursaries and scholarships Undergraduate students from England, Wales and 
Northern Ireland who are studying a course that attracts a tuition fee of 
£7,000 per annum could be entitled to a bursary of up to £2,000 if their 
household income is less than £42,600. Visitqmu.ac.uk/FundingUGRestofUK 
<http://www.qmu.ac.uk/welcome/FundingUGRestofUK.htm> for more information.

Accommodation Prices range from £99 per week for a 40 week contract or £118 
per week for a premium room on a 50 week contract.
