Fees £9,000 for home/EU students studying undergraduate courses in 2016-17. 
Students <https://www.theguardian.com/education/students> from the Isle of Man 
or the Channel Islands also pay the £9,000 tuition fee. £4,500 for part time 
home/EU students.

For international students, fees are charged at £10,800 per year for 
undergraduate courses.

Bursaries A range of awards on offer, including excellence scholarships worth 
£3,000 for new students from 2016-17 who have a minimum of AAA at A-level, or 
equivalent.

Music scholarships are also available, as are foundation scholarship, open to 
students from partner church schools and colleges, Visithope.ac.uk/scholarships 
<https://www.hope.ac.uk/undergraduate/feesandfunding/scholarships/> for more 
information.

Accommodation All first-years are guaranteed accommodation at either Hope 
Park, Creative Campus or Aigburth Park. Cost range from £77–£123 per week (36 
week contract, 2016–17) for a single study room depending on which halls you 
choose.

All halls have internet access, shared kitchen and dining areas and common 
areas. A number of rooms are adapted for disabled students.
