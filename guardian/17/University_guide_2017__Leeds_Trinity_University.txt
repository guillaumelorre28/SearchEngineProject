Fees £9,000 a year in 2016–17 for home/EU students taking three-year or 
two-year undergraduate degree courses. Tuition fees for foundation degree 
courses are £5,000 a year in 2016–17. Part-time tuition fees are paid pro rata 
according to the number of credits students are studying.

For international students, £11,000 a year for a three year undergraduate 
course, £12,500 a year for a two year undergraduate course and £11,000 a year 
for undergraduate top up courses. Tuition fees may rise with inflation in 
future years.

Bursaries A range of scholarships are available, including £1,000 for second 
year students from low-income backgrounds. The university also offers a 
progress bursary for students graduating from a foundation degree in July 2016, 
and progressing straight to a top-up course with the university in September 
2016. Visitleedstrinity.ac.uk/student-finance 
<http://www.leedstrinity.ac.uk/student-life/student-finance/undergraduate-and-foundation-uk-and-eu-student-finance>
 for full details and updates on 2017 entry.


Accommodation Costs range from £91 a week for self-catered, standard 
accommodation, to £126/week for part-catered, en-suite accommodation (which 
includes a £30 per week catering allowance). All accommodation on campus is 
university-owned with single bedrooms. Most first year students are guaranteed 
a room.
