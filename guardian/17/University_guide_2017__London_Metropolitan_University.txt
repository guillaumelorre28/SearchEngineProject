Fees £9,000 for home/EU full-time students taking undergraduate courses or 
£2,250 per 30 credit module. £10,950 per year for international students.

Bursaries and scholarships £1,000 bursary each year of study for all students 
who qualify for the full state maintenance loan. The university also offers 
care leavers bursaries and extended degree bursaries. Full information can be 
found at:londonmet.ac.uk/funding-your-studies 
<http://londonmet.ac.uk/applying/funding-your-studies>

Accommodation London Met does not own any student accommodation, but works 
with external student accommodation providers through its accommodation bureau.

For the 2016-17 session the cheapest hall of residence advertised is £120.51 
per week for a twin room in Helen Graham House (owned by Viridian), or £140 per 
week for a single room at Claredale House.

The most expensive is a Platinum studio at Chapter Spitalfields which is £729 
per week. All of these prices are inclusive of bills and non catered. The 
average price of halls on offer is £200 per week.
