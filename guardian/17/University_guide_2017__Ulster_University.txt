Fees £3,805 for full-time undergraduates who are domiciled in Northern 
Ireland, the Republic of Ireland and other EU countries in 2016-17.

£6,000 for full-time undergraduates who are domiciled in England, Wales or 
Scotland.

£12,800 for full-time international undergraduates. 

Bursaries/Scholarships Means-tested bursaries, funds for care leavers and 
subject-specific awards are available. Visit
ulster.ac.uk/apply/fees-and-finance/scholarships 
<http://www.ulster.ac.uk/apply/fees-and-finance/scholarships> for more 
information.

Accommodation Fees start at £2,183 for a 38 week contract in university 
accommodation in Jordanstown.
