
“There’s the open Pacific Ocean in front of us, and old-growth temperate 
rainforest behind us. The wonders of nature right outside our door.” This is 
how Charles McDiarmid sums up the allure of Tofino, where he runs the 
world-class hotel the Wickaninnish inn.

In this video, chef and YouTuber John Quilter needs little encouragement to 
dive right in – both into the surf, and into the local cuisine scene, where 
gigantic salmon are delivered straight from the water to the kitchen.
