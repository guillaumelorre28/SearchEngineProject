Fees £9,000 for all full-time undergraduates from the UK and EU/EEA in 
2016-17. The average fee for a full-time international students in 2016-17 is 
£14,000.00

Bursaries In 2016 all new fee paying students in receipt of a tuition fee loan 
from the Student Loan Company who meet the full support threshold of household 
income up to £25,000 will receive a £2,000 financial support package in their 
first year.

Students <https://www.theguardian.com/education/students> who are care leavers 
will also receive a full £2,000 support package, regardless of household income.

Students with a household income of between £25,000 and £42,6201 in receipt of 
a maintenance grant from the SLC will receive a cash award bursary for year one 
and continuing eligible years.


Accommodation The halls of residence are located a 15 minute walk from campus 
and are priced at £156-166 a week.
