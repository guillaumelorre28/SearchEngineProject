Fees £9,000 for home/EU students studying full-time, or £17,712 for 
international students in the year 2016-17.

Bursaries and scholarships A maximum bursary of £4,000 a year is offered to 
UK/EU students with the lowest household income. Other forms of financial 
support are available, including scholarships for UK, EU and overseas students.

For more information visit lse.ac.uk/financialsupport/scholarshipsLSE/ 
<http://www.lse.ac.uk/intranet/students/moneyMatters/financialSupport/ScholarshipsLSE/scholarshipsForStudyatLSE.aspx>

Accommodation LSE guarantees a place for all first-year undergraduates. Prices 
range from £100.80 per week for a shared room to £339 per week for a 
self-contained studio.
