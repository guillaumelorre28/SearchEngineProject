Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. £11,350 for 
international students. £75 per credit for part-time undergraduates in 2016-17. 
Fees for part-time students are £750 per 10 credit module.

Scholarships and bursaries A range of scholarships are on offer, including 
high achiever scholarships – £1,000 paid over the first year of study for 
undergraduates who achieve at least 360 Ucas tariff points.

Chancellor’s scholarships, worth £2,000, are also awarded to students who are 
nominated for making a contribution to the university community. Visit
edgehill.ac.uk/scholarships <https://www.edgehill.ac.uk/scholarships/> for more 
information.

Accommodation Prices range from £65-115 per week for self-catered 
accommodation for the year 2016-17. The most expensive accommodation includes a 
self-catered ensuite single room. The majority of first year undergraduates can 
expect a university room.
