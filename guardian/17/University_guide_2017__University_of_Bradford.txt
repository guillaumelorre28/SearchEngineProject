Fees £9,000 for full-time undergraduates in 2016-17. 

Fees for international students are £15,420 per year for lab-based courses 
(including engineering, informatics and life sciences), £12,950 a year for 
classroom-based courses, £1,300-£1,550 for sandwich course placement years.

Scholarships Up to £3,500 over the duration of a three-year degree, to all 
home/EU students who achieve ABB or higher in their A-levels (or equivalent). 
£500 per year if your family income is assessed as being under £30,000 per year.


For information on other scholarships, visit bradford.ac.uk/scholarships 
<http://www.brad.ac.uk/fees-and-financial-support/university-scholarships-and-support/>
.

Accommodation Weekly rent is £104.30 for a standard room in a townhouse, or 
£112.84 for an en-suite room in a flat. Rent includes internet/WiFi, insurance 
for personal belongings, 24 hr security and out-of- hours wardens. All students 
who apply on time are guaranteed a room in The Green, the university’s 
sustainable student village on campus.
