Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. Fees and funding 
options for international (non-EU) students will vary, visit
plymouth.ac.uk/international <http://www.plymouth.ac.uk/international> for more 
information.

Bursaries The university offers means-tested bursaries and financial support 
services. For further details visitplymouth.ac.uk/money 
<http://www.plymouth.ac.uk/money>

Accommodation Weekly rent in halls of residence ranges from £97.30 to £165 per 
week and are all are within a 20 minute walk of the campus and city centre. 
Prices for university approved student housing ranges from £70 to £135 per 
week. (prices quoted are for 2016-17).
