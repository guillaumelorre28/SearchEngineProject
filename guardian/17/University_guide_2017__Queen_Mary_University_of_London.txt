Fees £9,000 for full-time undergraduates in 2016-17. For international 
students, fees are £14,100 for arts/classroom-based programmes, £16,950 for 
laboratory-based programmes, £16,950 for intercalated programmes for 
BSc/BMedSci.

Bursaries The university offers a range of bursaries, including awards that 
are means-tested and subject-specific. For further details and links to 
school-specific scholarships, visitqmul.ac.uk/bursaries 
<http://www.qmul.ac.uk/undergraduate/feesandfunding/bursaries/index.html>.

The Queen Mary University of London 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/university-of-london> bursary is worth 
£1,571 per year for students whose household income is £25,000 or less, £1,256 
per year for students whose household income is between £25,000 and £42,600.

Science and engineering excellence scholarships worth £3,000 per year are also 
available to AAA students across the Faculty of Science and Engineering. These 
awards cover biological and chemical sciences, materials, engineering – 
including electronic, mechanical and aerospace – computer science, maths and 
physics.

Accommodation Fees for the academic year 2015-16 ranged from £89.95 to £108.78 
per week for a twin room at the Whitechapel campus to £165.20 per week for a 
single premium en-suite at the Mile End campus.
