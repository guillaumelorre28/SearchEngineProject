Fees £1,820 for Scottish/EU full-time undergraduates and £9,000 for rest of UK 
(RUK) full-time undergraduates for 2016-17. International (non-EU) students 
undergraduate fees are £15,135

Bursaries/Scholarships On an annual basis, RCS disburses circa £1.3m in 
scholarships to incoming and continuing students on the basis of merit and 
financial need. As part of this total, RCS has established a specific fund for 
incoming rest of the UK students on a means-tested basis. Visit
rcs.ac.uk/feesandfunding <https://www.rcs.ac.uk/studyhere/feesandfunding/> for 
more details.

Accommodation RCS aims to offer all first-year students a place in its 
contracted hall of residence, Liberty House, in the centre of Glasgow and 
within easy walking distance of RCS. Rooms are allocated on a first come, first 
served basis. Liberty House provides comfortable city-centre living; 
accommodation consists of en-suite study bedrooms sharing well-equipped kitchen 
and dining areas. Prices for Liberty House start at £139.50 per week (based on 
40 weeks).
