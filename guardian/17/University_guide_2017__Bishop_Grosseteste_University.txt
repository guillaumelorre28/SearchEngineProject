Fees £6,750 for home/EU students studying undergraduates 2016-17. Part-time 
fees are £1,500 per 20 credit module.

For international students, fees are £11,500.

Bursaries The university offers a range of bursaries. Visit 
bishopg.ac.uk/bursary 
<http://www.bishopg.ac.uk/student/fees/funding/Pages/bursary.aspx> for full 
details.

Accommodation Prices (fully inclusive of utilities and wifi) are approximately 
£4,232 for an en-suite study bedroom and £3320 for a non en-suite study bedroom.
