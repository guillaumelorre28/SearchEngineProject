Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. £19,500 for 
international students.

Bursaries/Scholarships A range of scholarships on offer. Visit 
trinitylaban.ac.uk/financial-awards 
<http://www.trinitylaban.ac.uk/study/fees-and-finance/financial-awards> for 
updates and more information.

Accommodation The university has halls of residence and provides guidance on 
renting in the private sector. Prices for studio rooms range from £195.11 to 
£204.11 per week, while prices for cluster rooms range from £149.77 to £152.61 
per week.
