Fees £9,000 for full-time home and EU undergraduates in 2017. Fees for 
international students range from £17,478-19,468.

Bursaries
The school offers a range of bursaries and scholarships. Details of those 
available to undergraduates can be found at cssd.ac.uk/scholarships 
<http://www.cssd.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/scholarships>. 

Accommodation There is limited provision for students at the University of 
London’s intercollegiate halls. Student services support finding accommodation. 
Halls cost between £123.91 and £470.02 per week.
