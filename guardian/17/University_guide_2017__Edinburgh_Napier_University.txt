Fees  £1,820 for full-time Scottish students, £305 per 20 credits for 
part-time courses. If you are a first degree student and ordinarily resident in
Scotland <https://www.theguardian.com/uk/scotland>, you are eligible for your 
tuition fees to be covered by the Scottish government.


 £9,000 for full-time students from the rest of the UK (England, Wales and 
Northern Ireland), £1,125 for part-time courses. If you are from elsewhere in 
the EU you are entitled to be charged the same level of tuition fees as 
Scottish students.

For international (non-EU) students, fees are £11,840 per year for full-time 
courses or £1,975 for part-time courses.


Bursaries A range of awards available. Visit 
napier.ac.uk/study-with-us/bursaries 
<http://www.napier.ac.uk/study-with-us/bursaries>for more details.

Accommodation Prices range from £107 per week for a single room with shared 
shower and toilet facilities, or £143 per week for a double bed room with a 
private shower room and toilet.
