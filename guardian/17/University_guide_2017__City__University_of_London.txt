Fees £9,000 for home and EU students in 2016-17. International fees vary 
according to the course but range from £13,500 to £16,000.

Bursaries/Scholarships The university offers a range of bursaries, including 
support for care leavers, mature students, academic prizes and more. For more 
information visitcity.ac.uk/scholarships-and-bursaries 
<https://www.city.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/funding-and-financial-support/scholarships-and-bursaries>
.

Accommodation All first-year undergraduate students are guaranteed a place in 
halls-of-residence. City’s halls of residence are managed by third party 
housing providers.

Prices range from £140 (a single room in Liberty Court) to £200 per week 
(premium en-suite bedroom with a double bed in East Central House).
