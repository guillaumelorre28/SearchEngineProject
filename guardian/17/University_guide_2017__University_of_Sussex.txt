Fees £9,000 per year for home/EU students in 2016-17.

For international students, £14,800 for foundation year courses. For 
undergraduate degree courses, fees range from £14,800 to £18,300, or £27,405 
for a BM BS in medicine.

Bursaries The university offers a range of awards including bursaries for 
undergraduates who family income is less than £42,875 per annum. The award is 
worth a £1,000 cash award and £2,000 rent reduction/fee waiver in first year, 
plus a further £1,000 in subsequent years.

Academic excellence scholarships for students with high grades are also 
available. Figures quoted are 2016-17, for more information visit
sussex.ac.uk/bursaries 
<http://www.sussex.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/fees-and-scholarships>.
