Fees Scottish and EU students can apply to the Students 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/students> Award Agency for Scotland to 
have their fees paid by the Scottish government.

Tuition fees for rest of UK (RUK) students in 2016-17 are £9,000 per year for 
three years of a four year degree (or four years of a five year integrated 
Masters). This means students only pay a maximum of £27,000 (or £36,000 for a 
five year MEng or MSci). There are a few exceptions to this, such as MBChB 
medicine, BDS dentistry and MArch architecture, where the £9,000 is charged for 
each year of the course.

Tuition fees for undergraduate international students in 2016-17 are generally 
between £12,950 and £15,950 and are guaranteed to stay the same for each year 
of the course. The only exceptions to this are MBChB medicine, BDS dentistry 
and BSc medical sciences degrees which charge a different tuition fee for the 
clinical years.

Bursaries/Scholarships There are a number of bursaries and scholarships 
available to rest of UK (RUK) and international applicants. Visit
dundee.ac.uk/study/ug/scholarships-fees/ruk-scholarships 
<http://www.dundee.ac.uk/study/ug/scholarships-fees/ruk-scholarships/> for more 
information.

Accommodation Guaranteed for applications received by mid-July of the year of 
entry. Prices range from £118.51 to £139.86 per week in 2016.
