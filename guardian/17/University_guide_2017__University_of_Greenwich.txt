Fees £9,000 for home/EU full-time students in 2016-17. £10,850 for

Bursaries A range of bursaries are available. Greenwich scholarship programme 
provides eligible undergraduate honours degree students in the first year of 
study only, a £1,000 fee waiver and up to £1,000 credited to a card to be spent 
on certain learning resources.

The aspire@Greenwich bursary is available to eligible new full-time home and 
EU undergraduate students who are starting certain programmes in the first 
academic year of study in 2016–17 only. Successful students will be provided 
with £200, in the first year of study only.


The high achievement scholarship is available for students achieving ABB or 
higher in A-level or certain other equivalent qualifications. Successful 
students will be awarded a bursary of £2,000 for a maximum of the three years 
of study.


Bursaries and Scholarships for students starting programmes of study in the 
2017-18 academic year are currently being reviewed and will be published on the 
university website.


For more informtion and updates visit gre.ac.uk/study/finance/scholarships 
<http://www2.gre.ac.uk/study/finance/undergraduate/scholarships>


Accommodation All first year, full-time students who apply by 30 June are 
guaranteed places in halls and it is expected that all first year full-time 
students who apply after this date will also be offered rooms in 2016-17.


Although there are a small number of twin rooms and studios available, the 
majority of rooms in halls are single bedrooms with either shared or en-suite 
shower/toilet facilities.


Based on 2016-17, prices range between £111.93 per week (some shared 
facilities) to £194.04 per week (extra-large room with en-suite facilities).


For further information visit gre.ac.uk/study/accommodation 
<http://www2.gre.ac.uk/study/accommodation>.
