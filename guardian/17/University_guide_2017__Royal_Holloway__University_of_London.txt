Fees £9,000 for full-time UK/EU undergraduates in 2016-17, with a different 
fee for a year spent abroad or working in industry. Fees for international 
students at all levels vary depending on the degree. Visit
royalholloway.ac.uk/feesandfunding 
<http://www.royalholloway.ac.uk/feesandfunding> for full and up-to-date details.

Bursaries Royal Holloway offers a scholarships and bursaries for academic, 
sporting or musical success, plus particular awards for academic disciplines 
and for international students. Some are competitive, and merit-based, and 
others offer automatic entitlement. For the year 2016, income-related bursaries 
are available for students from England, ranging from £500-1,500 per year, 
depending on family income. Bursaries are also available for mature students 
and care leavers. Visitroyalholloway.ac.uk/feesandfunding 
<http://www.royalholloway.ac.uk/feesandfunding> for full and up-to-date details.

Accommodation Guaranteed for firm choice first-year undergraduates who apply 
by the closing date. Prices range from about £80 to £175 a week, with a choice 
of self-catering or catered pay-as-you-go subsidised meals.
