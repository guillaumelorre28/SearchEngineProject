Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. International 
student fees range from £17,000 to £38,000.

Bursaries A range of awards, including means-tested bursaries and study abroad 
packages. Visitmanchester.ac.uk/manchester-scholarships-bursaries 
<http://www.manchester.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/student-finance/2016/uk/funding/manchester-scholarships-bursaries/>
 for more details.

Accommodation Prices range from £94.70 per week (self-catered hall and 
standard bedroom) to £179.68 per week (catered en-suite accommodation).
