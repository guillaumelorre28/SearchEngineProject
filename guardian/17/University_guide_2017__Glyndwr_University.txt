Fees £9,000 for home/EU students studying full-time undergraduate courses, or 
£4,500 for part-time courses.


 Welsh and EU students are entitled to a tuition fee grant of up to £5,100 
which means that eligible Welsh and EU students will actually borrow £3,900 
(not £9,000) per year in 2016-17.

For international students, fees are £10,500.

Bursaries/Scholarships Glyndwr University offers a range of scholarships and 
bursaries. Visitglyndwr.ac.uk/en/feesandstudentfinance/ 
<http://www.glyndwr.ac.uk/en/feesandstudentfinance/> for more information.

Accommodation Prices range from £83.75 per week for a single room in 
self-catered accommodation to £96.50 for a shared room with en-suite bathroom. 
All first year students are guaranteed accommodation.
