
The University of Cambridge 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/cambridgeuniversity> is at the top of 
the league table, retaining first place for the sixth year running, followed by
Oxford <https://www.theguardian.com/education/oxforduniversity> and St Andrews 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/2009/may/10/universityguide-uni-st-andrews>
 in the Guardian’s undergraduate university league table.

Loughborough 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/2009/may/10/universityguide-loughborough-uni>
 is this year’s newcomer in the top 10, coming in at fourth place, up from 11th 
place last year.Bath 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/2009/may/10/universityguide-uni-bath> 
has slipped from number five to number 10 this year.

Lancaster 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/2009/may/10/universityguide-lancaster-uni>
 gained two, to number eight, andWarwick 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/2009/may/10/universityguide-uni-warwick> 
lost three positions and is now at number nine. Surrey, Durham and Imperial 
College stayed in the same places as last year.


Exeter is the only school knocked out of the top 10, going from ninth to 11th 
place. The biggest drop isLiverpool John Moores University 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/2009/may/10/universityguide-liverpool-john-moores-uni>
, down 26 places from 58 to 84.Queen Margaret University 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/2009/may/10/universityguide-queen-margaret-uni-edinburgh>
 drops 25 places out of the top 100 to place 101 and Brighton falls 24 places 
to 91st place.


City University 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/2009/may/10/universityguide-city-uni-london>
 climbs 19 places and gets into the top 20 at position 18. The biggest climber 
is Sunderland, up 30 places, going from 109th to 79th place.

Liverpool Hope <https://www.theguardian.com/education/liverpoolhopeuniversity> 
appears in the top 100, going from 104th place to 79th and Derby regained the 
position it had in 2014, by going from 73rd to 48th. Also Bradford and Anglia 
Ruskin gained more than 20 places to rise into 52nd and 66th place respectively.
