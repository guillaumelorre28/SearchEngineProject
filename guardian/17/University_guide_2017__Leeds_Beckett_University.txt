Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU students in 2016-17. Fees for part-time home 
students are £75 per credit (to a maximum of £6,750). £9,700 for international 
students.

Bursaries A range of financial awards are available, including a higher 
achievers’ scholarship worth £1,000 to full-time home/EU undergraduates 
starting in the 2016-17 academic year who hold three A level grades at ABB+ or 
equivalent qualifications.

Sports scholarships, care leavers’ bursaries and hardship funds are also 
available. Visitleedsbeckett.ac.uk/scholarships 
<http://www.leedsbeckett.ac.uk/undergraduate/scholarships/> for more details.

Accommodation Prices for 2017-18 are not yet confirmed, but current 
accommodation fees range from £87.50 to £170 a week, including utilities in 
most cases. Visitleedsbeckett.ac.uk/accommodation 
<http://www.leedsbeckett.ac.uk/accommodation/your-contract/rent-prices/> for 
full information.
