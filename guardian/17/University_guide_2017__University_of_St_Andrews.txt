Fees Scottish and EU students from outside the UK pay no tuition fees. For 
other UK students, tuition fee of £9,000 for 2016-17. International students 
pay between £17,890-£25,200.

Bursaries There are a number of bursaries and scholarships available, designed 
to provide assistance to help students support themselves during their time at 
university and also aim to reward academic excellence. For more information, 
visitst-andrews.ac.uk/bursariesandscholarships 
<https://www.st-andrews.ac.uk/students/money/bursariesandscholarships/>.

Accommodation Around half of all students live in university accommodation. 
Fees start at £5,640 per academic year.
