Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17, £16,800 for 
international students. Part-time fees vary according to course.

Bursaries The college offers a cash bursary of £1,800 to students with a 
household income below £25,000. This is operated through the Student Loans 
Company and no application is required.

There are also a number of subject specific awards on offer. For full details 
visitbruford.ac.uk/bursaries 
<https://www.bruford.ac.uk/study/starting-here/bursaries/>.

Accommodation There are limited places available for first-years in halls 
approximately two miles from the college. Accommodation costs between £85 – 
£120 per week.
