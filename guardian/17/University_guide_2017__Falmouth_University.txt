Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates starting in the year 2017-18. 
£15,000 for international students. Part-time fees vary.

Bursaries A range of bursaries are available, including: the Cornwall award 
(£2,200 to applicants who live in Cornwall to help towards equipment, 
internships and the funding of end-of-year shows), a materials’ award (cash 
grants for UK and EU students whose household income is £25,000 or under) and 
care leaver’s and dependants’ bursaries. Visitfalmouth.ac.uk/falmouth-bursaries 
<http://www.falmouth.ac.uk/student-funding/undergraduate/falmouth-bursaries-16-17>
 for more information.

Accommodation Weekly rents range from £77.28 to £96.60 a week for shared 
rooms; £111.79 to £131.88 a week for single occupancy rooms and £140.66 
to£172.27 per week for studio apartments. Options and prices are subject to 
change for the 2017-18 academic year.
