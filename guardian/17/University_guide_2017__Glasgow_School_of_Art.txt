Fees £1,820 for Scottish/EU full-time undergraduates in 2016-17. £9,000 for 
full-time students from the rest of the UK.


 If you are a first degree student and ordinarily resident in Scotland, you 
are eligible for your tuition fees to be covered by the Scottish government. If 
you are from elsewhere in the EU you are entitled to be charged the same level 
of tuition fees as Scottish students.


£13,800 for international students. For more information visit 
www.gsa.ac.uk/finance. <http://www.gsa.ac.uk/finance>


Bursaries A ‘GSA RUK welcome scholarship’ worth £2,000 per year for up to four 
years is available to students who are paying the full £9,000 tuition fees and 
have been awarded the maximum maintenance student loan/tuition fee loan and 
whose total household income is between £0-45,000

The GSA also offers a range of subject-specific scholarships and a hardship 
fund. Visitgsa.ac.uk/scholarships  
<http://www.gsa.ac.uk/life/fees,-funding-finance/scholarships>for more details.

Accommodation Two halls of residence Blythswood House (from £135 per week), 
and Margaret Macdonald House (from £110 per week) are open to undergraduate and 
postgraduate students. Both sites are located in the city centre, close to 
amenities and travel links, and within five minutes’ walk of the main GSA 
Garnethill campus.
