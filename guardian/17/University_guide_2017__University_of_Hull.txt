Fees £9,000 is the average course cost for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 
2016-17. Part time fees are £4,500 for most courses.

For international students, £12,500 for arts subjects (£8,350 for a foundation 
year), £15,000 for science and engineering subjects (£10,450 for a foundation 
year).

Bursaries/Scholarships A range of awards on offer, such as studentship awards 
of £2,100 for the first year of study to home/EU students, available for 
students with an annual residual household income of less than £25,000.

Merit Scholarships, worth £2,000, are also available for the first year of 
study to home/EU students, achieving BBB at A Level, or equivalent.
www.hull.ac.uk/meritscholarship <http://www.hull.ac.uk/meritscholarship> 

Achievement Scholarship, £4,000 in total, are awarded to home/EU students 
achieving A*AA at A Level, or equivalent.

Visit hull.ac.uk/achievementscholarship 
<http://www.hull.ac.uk/achievementscholarship> for more information.

Accommodation All unaccompanied first year students are guaranteed a room in 
University-owned, managed or directed accommodation with utility bills and 
insurance included in the rent. Prices range from £72.95-112 per week.
