Fees £9,000 for most full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. 
International fees are £11,400.

Scholarships and bursaries The University is currently reviewing its 
scholarship package. Checkworcester.ac.uk/your-home/scholarships 
<http://www.worcester.ac.uk/your-home/scholarships.html> for updates.

Accommodation If you’re a first year undergraduate student who has accepted a 
conditional or unconditional offer, you are guaranteed a place in 
university-managed accommodation if you apply for accommodation before May 
31st. Places are allocated on a first-come-first-served basis. Prices begin at 
£91 a week and go up to £149 a week for en-suite rooms (2016-17 figures).
