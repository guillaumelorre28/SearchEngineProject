Fees £9,000 for home/EU students for the year 2016-17. Overseas tuition fees 
for countries not in the EU are £16,250 for classroom-based course, £21,750 for 
lab-based courses and £19,000 for MPharm.

Bursaries and scholarships A range of scholarships and bursaries are 
available, including income dependent and subject specific awards. For the year 
2016-17, students whose household income is £25,000 are entitled to a £1,500 
bursary. Visitkcl.ac.uk/scholarships-and-bursaries 
<http://www.kcl.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/fees-and-funding/student-funding/scholarships-and-bursaries/bursaries/kings-living-bursary.aspx>
 for more information.

Accommodation All first year undergraduates are guaranteed accommodation, 
provided they meet the application deadline which is published on the 
university website each year. Prices range from £140 per person, per week (zone 
1, single room with shared bathroom and kitchen facilities) to £282 per person, 
per week (zone 1, studio with bathroom and kitchenette facilities).
