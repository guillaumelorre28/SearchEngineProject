Fees £9,000 for full-time home and EU undergraduates for the year 2016-17. 
£16,800 for international students.

Bursaries and scholarships Courtauld offers £3,000 to students who qualify for 
the full maintenance grant (i.e. have a household income of £25,000 or less) 
and come from a group that is under-represented in higher education, such as 
disabled students.Students <https://www.theguardian.com/education/students> may 
elect to use £2,000 as a fee waiver and take £1,000 as a cash bursary for 
maintenance costs.

Accommodation
The Courtauld provides rooms in its recently refurbished residence at Duchy 
House and a small number of rooms at the University of London’s intercollegiate 
halls, which cost between £123.91 and £470.02 per week.
