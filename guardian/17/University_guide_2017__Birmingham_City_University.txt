Fees £9,000 for home and EU full-time students; £12,000 per year for 
international student fees. Prices listed are for academic year 2016-17, check
bcu.ac.uk/fees-costs-and-scholarships 
<http://www.bcu.ac.uk/international/fees-costs-and-scholarships> for updates.


Bursaries and scholarships There are a mix of bursaries on offer, including 
awards of up to £4,000 in grants or scholarships for students who meet a range 
of criteria (such as achieving or being predicted 320 Ucas tariff points). Visit
bcu.ac.uk/financial-support 
<http://www.bcu.ac.uk/student-info/finance-and-money-matters/financial-support> 
for more information.

Accommodation For the year 2016-17, prices ranged from £90 per week for a 
single room in six-bedroom, self-catered flat to £134 per week for a single 
room in two-bedroom, self-catered flat.
