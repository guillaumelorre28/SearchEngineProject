Fees For home and EU students, £6,000 for a full-time foundation degree and 
£9,000 for a full-time bachelor’s degree. For home and EU students on part-time 
courses, fees are charged at half of the full-time rate for each level of study.


For international students, fees for full-time courses range from £9,500 for a 
foundation degree to £13,500 for an undergraduate degree. Figures quoted are 
for 2016-17, checkbournemouth.ac.uk/fees-funding 
<https://www1.bournemouth.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/fees-funding/uk-eu-student-finance>
 for updates.

Bursaries and scholarships There are a range of awards on offer, including 
academic excellence scholarships worth £1,500 for students who achieve AAA or 
above at A-level (or equivalent). A maintenance bursary and care leavers 
bursary are also available. Visitbournemouth.ac.uk/fees-funding/bursaries 
<https://www1.bournemouth.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/fees-funding/uk-eu-student-finance/bursaries>
 for more information.

Accommodation Guaranteed to all first year students who select Bournemouth 
<https://www.theguardian.com/uk/bournemouth> as their firm choice and who 
submitted their Ucas application by 15 January (UK and EU) or 30 June (non-EU 
students). Prices range from £100-120 per week for a single room in a 
self-catered, shared house to £134 for a room with an en-suite shower room and 
a shared kitchen and living area. All prices include bills.
