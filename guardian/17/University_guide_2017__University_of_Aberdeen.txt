Fees If you are a first degree student and ordinarily resident in Scotland, 
you are eligible for your tuition fees to be covered by the Scottish 
government. For other UK students, annual tuition fees are £9,000 in 2016-17.

If you are from elsewhere in the EU you are entitled to be charged the same 
level of tuition fees as Scottish students. For international (non-EU) 
students, £13,000.

Bursaries There are a number of scholarships available to students and 
applicants, including access and merit scholarships, sports awards, and funds 
for students who are returning to education. Visit
abdn.ac.uk/scholarships-and-funding 
<http://www.abdn.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/scholarships-and-funding-286.php> 
for details.

Accommodation
Guaranteed for all new first-year students. Weekly prices range £99 to £169.
