Fees £9,000 for home/EU full-time students in 2017-18.

For international students, £10,900 for classroom-based courses, £11,190 for 
resource-intensive courses, £11,725 for occupational therapy.


Bursaries and scholarships A means-tested bursary for students with a 
household income below £36,592 (2015 level). Eligible students could receive up 
to £1,000 (including a £100 study resource card for Blackwell’s bookshop). 
Scholarships are also available for talented athletes and aspiring coaches.

Visit derby.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/fees/bursary/ 
<http://www.derby.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/fees/bursary/> for more information.


Accommodation University of Derby 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/universityofderby> halls of residence 
are purpose-built, self-contained flats with a choice of rooms ranging from 
double en-suite rooms (£137.76 per week) to budget rooms (£76.58 per week). 
Special accommodation is also offered for disabled students.
