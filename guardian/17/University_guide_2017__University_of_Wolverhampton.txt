Fees £9,000 is the maximum fee for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17 
or £7,570 for foundation degrees. Sandwich years are free. Part-time fees are a 
maximum of £2,780.

For international students, fees are £11,250.

Bursaries/Scholarships A variety of funds are on offer including access 
bursaries worth £2,000 available to students paying £6,000 tuition or more who 
have been declared eligible for disabled student allowance prior to enrolment 
or who have previously been in care.

Distinction scholarships and sports achievement scholarships are also 
available. Visit

For full details of the scholarships on offer, visit 
wlv.ac.uk/money-matters/financial-support 
<http://www.wlv.ac.uk/study-here/money-matters/financial-support/> for more 
information.

Accommodation Prices range from £77-99. All accommodation at Wolverhampton 
City, Walsall and Telford campuses is university-owned.Students 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/students> can choose from economy, 
standard or en-suite accommodation. (Prices quoted for 2016-17)
