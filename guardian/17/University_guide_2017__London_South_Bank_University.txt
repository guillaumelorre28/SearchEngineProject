Fees £9,000 for full-time home and EU undergraduates in 2016-17. Part-time 
fees vary by course. Fees for undergraduate study for international students 
started at £11,600.

Bursaries LSBU offers several types of fee reduction through scholarships and 
bursaries, including an access bursary worth £2,000 for full-time home 
undergraduate students from lower-income families.

Additionally, all full-time undergraduate students starting in September 2016 
have the opportunity to apply for one of seven £10,000 scholarships, awarded on 
academic merit. Talented sportsmen and women can also apply for LSBU’s elite 
sports programme which provides a bursary of up to £1,500.

Accommodation Weekly rents for 2016-17 range from £118.50-143.20 Accommodation 
is guaranteed for full-time undergraduate students starting in September 2016 
who live in homes outside the M25.
