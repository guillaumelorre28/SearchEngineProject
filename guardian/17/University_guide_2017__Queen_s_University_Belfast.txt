Fees For 2016-17 entry, students from Northern Ireland and the non-UK EU will 
be charged £3,925 per year. Full-time first degree students from the rest of 
the UK (RUK) pay £9.000 per year.

International fees range from £13,945 to £34,830. Fees for 2017-18 have not 
been confirmed, visitqub.ac.uk/InternationalStudentTuitionFees 
<http://www.qub.ac.uk/home/TuitionFeesandStudentSupportArrangements2016-17/InternationalStudentTuitionFees2016-17/>
 for updates.

Bursaries/Scholarships For students enrolling in 2016-17 awards include:

Undergraduate scholarships for students from England, Scotland, Wales, the 
Channel Islands and the Isle of Man. Eligible students who satisfy the 
conditions of their offer will qualify for a £1,250 scholarship per year. This 
amount will increase to £1,750 per year for those who achieve ABB at A-level or 
the equivalent in other qualifications, rising to £2,500 per year for those 
achieving AAB at A-level or the equivalent in other qualifications.


 All students entering the university for the first time to undertake a 
primary degree, and who have achieved at least three A grades at A-level 
(including AVCEs), are eligible to enter the university’s annual A-level 
entrance scholarship competition. A number of scholarships are available 
varying from £200 to £4,000.

Means-tested bursaries and awards for international students are also 
available. Visitqub.ac.uk/Study/Scholarships-and-Finance/ 
<http://www.qub.ac.uk/Study/Scholarships-and-Finance/> for updates and more 
information.

Accommodation The current weekly costs for 2015-16 range from £69.00 sharing a 
twin room to £119.00 for a premium double room and £155.00 for a one bed 
apartment in Willow Walk.

Elms Village is a purpose built self-catered accommodation village for over 
1,900 undergraduate students. It provides a range of room types to suit all 
budgets and the price is all inclusive (ie, heat and hot water, electricity, 
30Mb Wi-Fi).

Located on the periphery of Elms Village, Willow Walk offers exclusive 
accommodation to international undergraduate and postgraduate students in 
modern contemporary apartments. There is a mix of studio, one, two, three, and 
four bedroom apartments.Students 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/students> can also choose to live in 
Single Sex, Quiet Living or No Alcohol blocks.

Prices for the years 2016-17 and 2017-18 are not yet available. 
stayatqueens.com/ApplyforAccommodation 
<http://www.stayatqueens.com/sites/StayAtQueens/ApplyforAccommodation/> for 
updates.
