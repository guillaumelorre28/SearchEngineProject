Fees £9,000 for full-time home, EU and Channel Island undergraduates in 
2016-17.

For full-time international students, fees are £14,700 (art students) and 
£18,500 for laboratory subjects.

Bursaries UK students with a household income of £22,000 or below may be 
eligible for a bursary of £3,000 per year of study (including unpaid placements 
and study abroad periods).

The Lloyds Scholars programme offers bursaries of £1,000 towards living costs 
and study materials to students from lower income households, as well as paid 
internships, awards for good grades, mentoring support and access to workshops 
through the Lloyds Banking Group.

For a full list of the awards on offer visit bath.ac.uk/ug-applicants/bursaries
 <http://www.bath.ac.uk/ug-applicants/funding/bursaries/index.html>.

Accommodation Weekly prices start at £98 for a single, self-catered room or 
£65 for a self-catered, shared single. Large, self-catered rooms with an 
en-suite show cost £160. There are also small double flats available for 
couples at £216 per week.
