Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. International 
fees cost up to £12,240.

Bursaries Means-tested bursaries are available to students with a household 
income of less than £42,000. A bursary programme for care leavers also offers 
£1,000 a year.
For up-to-date information, visit chi.ac.uk/our-financial-support-package 
<http://www.chi.ac.uk/study-us/fees-finance/our-financial-support-package>.

Accommodation Halls of residence – both catered and self-catered - are 
available on both Chichester and Bognor Regis campuses, and prices for 2014-15 
start from £96.18 per week.
