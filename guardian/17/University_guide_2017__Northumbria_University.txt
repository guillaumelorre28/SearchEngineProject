Fees £9,000 for home/EU full-time undergraduates in 2016-17. Fees for 
part-time courses vary according to their length. International student fees 
range from £12,000-£14,000

Bursaries Northumbria has a number of scholarships and bursaries on offer, 
including academic awards and sports awards. Visit
northumbria.ac.uk/study-at-northumbria/fees-and-scholarships 
<https://www.northumbria.ac.uk/study-at-northumbria/fees-and-scholarships/> for 
more information.

Accommodation Rents range from £69.30 (Claude Gibb accommodation) to £164.50 
per week (studio Trinity Square accommodation). Prices include utilities, 
internet and contents insurance and free 100Mb wi-fi.
