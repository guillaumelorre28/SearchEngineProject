Fees £9,000 for UK and EU full-time students on undergraduate degree courses 
in 2016-17. Foundation degrees are £7,500.

Fees for international students on undergraduate programmes are £11,750 per 
year.

Bursaries/Scholarships The university offers a range of bursaries, including 
support for care leavers, music scholarships, sports excellence funds and 
emergency financial assistance. Visitglos.ac.uk/life/finance/funding 
<http://www.glos.ac.uk/life/finance/pages/funding.aspx> for more information.

Accommodation Halls of residence are from £91–£155 per a week for a 40 week 
contract. All rooms are for single occupancy and are self-catered. Rent 
includes wireless internet access, heating, water, electricity and basic 
contents insurance (our premium rooms also feature TVs in each room and 
communal area and washing machine in each kitchen.)
