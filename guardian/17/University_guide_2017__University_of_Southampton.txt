Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17.

Fees for international student vary. Programmes (including foundation year) in 
arts, humanities, law, social sciences and mathematics (excluding geography and 
psychology) are £16,054 per year; programmes (excluding foundation year) in 
engineering, science, first- and second-year medicine, health and life 
sciences, geography and psychology are £19,725 per year; third-, fourth- and 
fifth-year medicine is £40,230 per year; engineering and science foundation 
year are £16,800 per year.

Bursaries/Scholarships A range of awards are offered, including means-tested 
bursaries, subject-specific funds and sports awards. For updates and more 
information visitsouthampton.ac.uk/ugp/scholarships 
<http://www.southampton.ac.uk/ugp/scholarships>.

A number of subject-specific scholarships and bursaries are available to 
non-EU students, while many international organisations offer financial 
assistance. Visitsouthampton.ac.uk/international/scholarships 
<http://www.southampton.ac.uk/ugp/international/scholarships> for details.

Accommodation Prices for 2016-17 start at £93 per week for a self-catered 
non-en suite room, going up to £187 for a self-contained studio flat. 96% of 
accommodation is university-owned and managed. All new, full-time first year 
undergraduates who are coming to study unaccompanied and who apply for 
accommodation by 1August are guaranteed a room.
