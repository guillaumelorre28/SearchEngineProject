Fees £9,000 for home/EU students studying full-time courses. Fees for 
international students range from £14,950 to £18,950. Fees are reduced during 
placement year.


Bursaries A mix of awards are offered, including the Loughborough scholarship 
programme, which provides undergraduates with up to £6,000 over three years. 
Support includes bursaries and tuition fee waivers – the value and combination 
of support is dependent on household income.


Development trust scholarships, worth £9,000 over three years, are also 
available to support to a number of UK undergraduates from neighbourhoods where 
fewer people go to university. Sports scholarships are also offered. Visit
lboro.ac.uk/finance <http://www.lboro.ac.uk/study/finance/ug2016/> for full 
details.

Accommodation Prices for 2015-16 range from £4,947-£6,747 per year for catered 
accommodation and £3,186-6,174 for self-catered accommodation.Students 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/students> are offered 14 different halls 
to choose from, the vast majority of which are on campus and all are within 
walking distance.
