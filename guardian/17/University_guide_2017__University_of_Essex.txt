Bursaries/Scholarships
Essex offers a comprehensive package of bursaries and scholarships. This 
includes bursaries for students from lower-income households, care leavers, 
awards for academic excellence, and sports scholarships.

For full details, visit the university website 
<http://www.essex.ac.uk/studentfinance/ug/scholarships_and_bursaries.aspx>.

A range of packages on offer to support students, including those from lower 
income households. Bursaries also available to care leavers, students with 
refugee status, and students from lower income households studying aborad or 
doing an industrial or voluntary placement. Visitessex.ac.uk/scholarships 
<https://www.essex.ac.uk/fees-and-funding/ug/scholarships/> for full 
information. (Figures quoted are for the year 2016-17)

Accommodation Prices for Colchester accommodation range from £76.93 per week 
for a single room to £140.98 for an en suite single room.

Southend accommodation ranged from £135.25 for an en suite single-study room 
to £166.82 for a studio flat with kitchenette. All students are guaranteed 
university-provided accommodation for their first year of study, as long as 
they have had their place at the university confirmed, their accommodation 
application has been received by the published deadline, and they accept their 
offer of study atEssex <https://www.theguardian.com/uk/essex>.
