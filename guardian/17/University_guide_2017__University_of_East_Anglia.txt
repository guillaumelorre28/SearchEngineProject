Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates 2016-17. Between £14,000 - 
£28,550 for international undergraduates, depending on course.

Bursaries/Scholarships UEA offers a range of bursaries and scholarships, 
including music, and elite sport scholarships. Visit
uea.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/finance 
<https://www.uea.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/finance?utm_campaign=web_listing_guardian&amp;utm_source=profile&amp;utm_medium=weblink&amp;utm_content=fees_and_funding>
 for full details and updated figures for 2017-18.

Accommodation UEA has an accommodation guarantee for first year undergraduate 
students. Accommodation fees range from £73 per week for a self-catered, twin 
room with shared bathroom, to £240 per week for a one bedroom, premier, 
self-contained flat.
