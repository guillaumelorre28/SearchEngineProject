Fees Full-time home/EU first degree students from Scotland and the non-UK EU 
have their tuition fees paid by the Student Awards Agency for Scotland (SAAS).

Fees are £7,400 for full-time undergraduates who are normally resident in 
England, Wales and Northern Ireland.

Bursaries/Scholarships A range of bursaries to assist with living costs are 
currently on offer. For updates on what assistance will be available for 
2017-18, emailask@uws.ac.uk <mailto:ask@uws.ac.uk>

Accommodation Self-catering university accommodation costs range from £85 per 
week for a single room in a shared flat to £142 per week for a single occupancy 
one bedroom flat, according to 2015-16 prices.
