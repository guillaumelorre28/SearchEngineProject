Fees £9,000 for full-time home and EU undergraduates in 2016-17. For part-time 
students, £750 per 10 credits (max of 80 credits per year). £12,500 for 
international students.

Bursaries Roehampton is a member of the Santander Universities Network, which 
offers scholarships up to £5,000 for one year of a course. In addition, the 
university is part of a government programme to encourage men into primary 
teaching careers, and offers a £1,000 scholarship for each year of a three year 
course in primary education. Music and sports based scholarships are also 
available. The university also has 29 master’s scholarships, worth £10,000 
each, available.

Figures quoted are for the year 2016-17, visit roehampton.ac.uk/finance 
<http://www.roehampton.ac.uk/Finance/Undergraduate-Scholarships-UK-and-EU-2016-17/>
 for more information and updates.

Accommodation All first year students, who make Roehampton their firm choice, 
can live in campus accommodation. Prices for campus accommodation range from 
£114.45 – £164.85 per week. All rooms have a wash basin; standard rooms share 
bathroom facilities, while the top end are en-suite. The university also offers 
first year students accommodation at its Spring Mews site in Vauxhall.
