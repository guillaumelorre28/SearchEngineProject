Fees £9,000 for home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17, or £1,000 for placement 
years.


 For international students, fees range from £13,500 to £16,500, depending on 
subject area. Placement year fees are £2,500.

Bursaries A range of bursaries are on offer, including the Aston excellence 
scholarship, available to UK and EU students who achieve AAB or above in their 
A-levels or equivalent qualifications. Successful students will receive for up 
to a £2,000 reduction on their tuition fees.

The aspire scholarship, a university wide scholarship available for UK 
students, living on campus, that have a household income of £18,000 or less.


Figures quoted are for the academic year 2016-17, for updates and a full list 
of the bursaries on offer, visitaston.ac.uk/scholarships 
<http://www.aston.ac.uk/scholarships/>.


Accommodation Prices for Aston student village are £124.60 a week, for 51 
weeks in standard en-suite accommodation or up to £135.45 a week for 42 weeks 
in deluxe en-suite accommodation.

Weekly prices for Lakeside accommodation are £121.10 a week for a 42 week stay 
or £117.60 a week for a 51 week stay. All rooms are en-suite.
