Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. £6,750 per year 
for part-time students. For international students, fees range from £11,000 - 
£11,500.

Bursaries A variety of awards are available, including support for parents, 
disabled students allowance and adult dependent grants. Visit
anglia.ac.uk/help-with-finances 
<http://www.anglia.ac.uk/student-life/help-with-finances/uk-eu-undergraduate> 
for more information.

Accommodation Prices from halls are from approximately £92.61-£159.14. There 
is no official guarantee of accommodation for first-year students, but in 
reality the university can accommodate about 90% of applicants from outside the 
local eligibility distance radius, which is 35 miles for Cambridge and 15 miles 
for Chelmsford.
