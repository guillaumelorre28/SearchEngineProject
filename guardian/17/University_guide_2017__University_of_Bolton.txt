Fees £9,000 for UK and EU students studying full-time undergraduate courses in 
2016-17 or 2017-18. Fees for part-time students are 2016-17 or 2017-18.

For international students, are £11,250 for 2016-17 or £12,000 in 2017-18.

Bursaries/Scholarships Bolton offers a range of bursaries. The Bolton 
Brilliance Bursary is a cash award available to students who commence their 
course of study atUniversity of Bolton 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/universityofbolton> in September 2016, 
having previously achieved 128 Ucas points at A2 level or with BTEC NQF 
Qualifications at DDM. The Bursary is worth up to a maximum of £3,000 in total. 
This is paid as a £1,000 cash bursary in each academic year of your studies up 
to a maximum of three academic years. Sports bursaries and care leavers 
bursaries are also available.

Accommodation Accommodation is guaranteed for all new students to the 
university that have applied through Ucas and accepted the University of Bolton 
<https://www.theguardian.com/education/universityofbolton> as their firm choice 
subject to submitting a completed Halls application on or before 31st July, 
2016. Halls cost £80.00 per week in 2016-17.
