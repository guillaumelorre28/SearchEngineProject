Fees £9,000 for home and EU students or, £1,800 for placement year for 
2016-17. The cost of the full award for a part-time undergraduate course is 
calculated on a pro-rata basis of the full-time equivalent (based on 120 
credits) award cost shown above.


 Welsh and EU students are entitled to a tuition fee grant of up to £5,100 
which means that eligible Welsh and EU students will actually borrow £3,900 
(not £9,000) per year in 2016-17.

Fees for overseas students in 2016/17 this will be £10,200 per year with a 
£3,100 fee for the placement year.


Bursaries The university has more than 100 scholarships available, with the 
majority of awards worth between £1,000 and £4,500 in a single academic year. 
Some of the industry scholarships include a placement year job. Visit
harper-adams.ac.uk/undergraduate-scholarships 
<http://www.harper-adams.ac.uk/finance/undergraduate-scholarships.cfm> for more 
details.

Accommodation Prices for university halls range from £102 per week for a 
catered, shared room with shared bathroom in a single-sex university house to 
£167.75 for a catered, en-suite room on a mixed sex floor of halls.

The university aims to accommodate all first year students on campus. Prices 
quoted are for the year 2016-17.
