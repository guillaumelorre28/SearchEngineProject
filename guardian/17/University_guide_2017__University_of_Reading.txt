Fees £9,000 for full-time undergraduates in 2016-17. Part-time and 
international fees vary, view individual course pages for details.

Bursaries A range of bursaries are on offer including placement bursaries 
(worth up to £1,000 for students from low-income households), sports 
scholarships for talented athletes and care leavers bursaries. Awards for the 
year 2017 are not yet confirmed, visitreading.ac.uk/fees-and-funding 
<https://www.reading.ac.uk/ready-to-study/study/fees-and-funding/fees-and-funding-ug/home-eu-undergraduate-student-fees.aspx>
 for updates and more information.

Accommodation Rooms in halls cost from £101 per week. The rent includes all 
utility bills, internet access, contents insurance, maintenance and communal 
cleaning.
