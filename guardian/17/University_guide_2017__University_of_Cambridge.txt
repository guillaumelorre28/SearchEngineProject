Fees £9,000 for full-time undergraduates in 2016-17. Fees for international 
students range from £15,816 to £38,283.

Bursaries If you are a home or EU student and your household income is below 
£42,620 you are likely to be eligible for an annual Cambridge bursary of up to 
£3,500 per year if the start date of your course is 2012 or later.

Visit for admin.cam.ac.uk/cambridgebursary/ 
<http://www.admin.cam.ac.uk/univ/cambridgebursary/> more information.

Accommodation Almost all students are housed by their college for the full 
length of their course. Standard rents are between £100-£185 per week for a 
standard room with a shared bathroom or £120-£195 per week for a room with an 
en-suite.
