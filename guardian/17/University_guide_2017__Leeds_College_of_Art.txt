Fees £9,000 for home/EU students in 2016-17. Fees for international students 
are £12,000.


Bursaries A range of bursaries on offer, including means-tested awards. For 
full details visitleeds-art.ac.uk/apply/finance/undergraduate-financial-support 
<http://www.leeds-art.ac.uk/apply/finance/undergraduate-financial-support/>.


Accommodation There’s a variety of student accommodation to choose from – from 
city-centre apartments to shared houses. Halls of residence are available from 
£103.66-105.91 per week.

Rent for Carlton Hill (Leeds LS7) is approximately £113.22 per week, while 
rent for Liberty Park (Marlborough street) is £122 per week inclusive of 
utility bills as well as internet and en-suite with a deposit of £200 required.
