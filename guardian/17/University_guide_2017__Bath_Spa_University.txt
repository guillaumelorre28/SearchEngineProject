Fees £9,000 per year for home and EU undergraduates in 2016-17. BA/BSc degree 
courses delivered at Weston College are £7,500.

Part-time fees are charged by module and weight of module. For 20 credit 
modules,£1,500; for 40 credit modules, £3,000 and for 60 credit modules, 
£4,500. Art and design part-time courses are non-modular and the tuition fee is 
£4,500.


Prices start at £11,600 for international students studying full-time degree 
courses.


Bursaries Bath Spa University has a number of bursaries worth £3,500 each, 
split over 3 years, to eligible first year students who are new to higher 
education in 2016-17. The bursary is open to students who have household income 
assessed by student finance as below £25,000 and living in England or the EU.

A number of other scholarships and awards are available, including the 
Jennifer Skellett Award, Cullum Family Award, and the Gane Travel Award. For 
more information visitbathspa.ac.uk/bsu-bursaries-and-scholarships 
<http://www.bathspa.ac.uk/study-with-us/undergraduate/fees-and-finance/bsu-bursaries-and-scholarships>
.

Please note bursaries and scholarships shown are for students starting in 
2016-17.

Accommodation Prices will start from £80 for a shared twin room. In 2015-16 a 
single room with shared bathroom facilities started at £120 per week; en-suites 
started at £141 per week; and studio flats were from £165 per week.
