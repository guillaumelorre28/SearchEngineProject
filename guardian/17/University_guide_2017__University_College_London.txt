Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. International 
student fees range from £16,130 to £31,720.

Bursaries Students whose household income is £42,875 or less (for 2016 entry), 
will be eligible to receive a UCL undergraduate bursary worth up to £2,000.

For details of all bursary schemes please see the UCL Scholarships 
<http://www.ucl.ac.uk/prospective-students/scholarships/undergraduate/hebss_nsp/>
.

Accommodation Weekly prices for 2014-15 range from £143.43 to £258.93 for UCL 
halls of residence. Visit
ucl.ac.uk/prospective-students/accommodation/residences/halls 
<http://www.ucl.ac.uk/prospective-students/accommodation/residences/halls> for 
more information.
