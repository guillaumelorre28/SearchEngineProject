Fees £9,000 for full-time undergraduates in 2016-17, subject to government 
changes. International fees cost up to £31,500.

Bursaries The Leeds Financial Support scheme for 2017 will be finalised in due 
course. Under the 2016 Leeds Financial Support (LFS) scheme, students with a 
household income of £10,000 or below will receive support of up to £2,500 per 
year.

Where the assessed household income is between £10,001 and £42,600 students 
will receive support of up to £2,000. There will also be other alumni-funded 
undergraduate scholarships.

For more information visit leeds.ac.uk/leeds_financial_support_non-repayable 
<http://www.leeds.ac.uk/info/128004/fees_and_funding/28/leeds_financial_support_non-repayable>
.

Accommodation The cost of university accommodation in 2015-16 ranged from £81 
to £191 a week, including insurance, internet access, energy costs, and 
membership of the university’s sports facilities.
