
The Russian English-language news channel RT has briefly interrupted the 
online video feed of the US network C-SPAN, broadcasting for around 10 minutes.


The feed from RT – for Russia <https://www.theguardian.com/world/russia> 
Today, which US officials accuse of being a propaganda outlet – interrupted 
footage of a congressional session featuring Representative Maxine Waters 
speaking about the Securities and Exchange Commission at around 2:30 pm (1930 
GMT) before the site returned to regular programming.

C-SPAN is investigating the incident, the public affairs network said in a 
statement.

“This afternoon the online feed of C-SPAN was briefly interrupted by RT 
programming,” it said.
