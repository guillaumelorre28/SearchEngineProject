
From 1989 to 1996, Jonathan Riley-Smith 
<https://www.theguardian.com/books/2016/oct/06/jonathan-riley-smith-obituary> 
was chair of theVictoria County History 
<http://www.victoriacountyhistory.ac.uk/>, run from the Institute of Historical 
Research <https://www.history.ac.uk/> at London University. Though his 
principal field of research was the Crusades, he was extremely energetic in 
seeking to sustain the work of this leading publishing project in English local 
history.
