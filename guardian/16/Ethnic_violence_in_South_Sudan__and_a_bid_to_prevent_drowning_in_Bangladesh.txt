Karen McVeigh reported from Bangladesh on how superstition is a major obstacle

<https://www.theguardian.com/global-development/2016/dec/30/superstition-prevents-action-against-children-drowning-in-bangladesh>
, the leading cause of death in children under the age of 18. She also spoke to 
Mohammed Alamgir, alifeguard at the resort town of Cox’s Bazar 
<https://www.theguardian.com/global-development/2017/jan/05/bangladesh-surfer-ambitions-chairman-board-coxs-bazar-recycled-materials>
, who has so far saved 20 lives with a surfboard he developed from recycled 
materials – as shown inour video
