
One day after Donald Trump’s inauguration, on 21 January, hundreds of 
thousands of people will gather in Washingtonfor the Women’s March on 
Washington. 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2017/jan/14/womens-march-on-washington-protest-size-donald-trump>
 And while America’s capital will host the largest event, it is hardly alone: 
more than 300simultaneous protests <https://www.womensmarch.com/> are expected 
across the US, with dozens more solidarity marches planned around the world.

Are you attending one of these events? Share your story with us via 
GuardianWitness. A selection of your responses will be included in coverage 
around Trump’s inauguration.

We want to know where you’re protesting – and why. Are you attending with 
friends or family, or were you inspired to show up solo? What does activism 
look like where you are, and what does it mean to you? Snap a photo, share a 
video, or just write us a note sharing your experience.
