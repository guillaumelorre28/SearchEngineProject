<https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2016/nov/21/storm-angus-floods-hit-south-west-england-with-more-rain-to-come>
Heavy rain is forecast to continue in south-west England, parts of northern 
England andWales <https://www.theguardian.com/uk/wales> with around 20-30mm of 
rain expected to fall in a three- to six-hour period in places with as much as 
40-60mm possible through the whole of Monday.

Andy Page, chief meteorologist at the Met Office, said Devon was likely to be 
most affected 
<https://www.theguardian.com/environment/2016/nov/21/government-criticised-for-lack-of-natural-flood-management-schemes>
: “Although the more persistent rain should clear from Devon by early 
afternoon, heavy and possibly thundery showers are likely to follow”
