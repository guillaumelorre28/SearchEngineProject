At the awards in London on Monday night, Podcast of the Year went to The 
Guardian’s Football Weekly, while Liverpool’s Philippe Coutinho was named the 
Player of the Year and Sky Sports’ Martin Tyler and Jamie Carragher were named 
as Commentator and Pundit of the Year respectively. Among the other winners, 
Fanzine of the Year was Doncaster Rovers’ Popular Stand.
