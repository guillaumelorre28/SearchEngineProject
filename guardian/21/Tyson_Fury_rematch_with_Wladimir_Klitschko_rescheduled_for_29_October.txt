
Tyson Fury has announced his rematch with Wladimir Klitschko 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/wladimir-klitschko> has been rescheduled for 
29 October in Manchester.

The 27-year-old was due to fight Klitschko this weekend but was forced to 
withdraw 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2016/jun/24/tyson-fury-pulls-out-wladimir-klitschko-injury>
 after sustaining an ankle injury in training.

On Thursday, Fury wrote on Twitter: “16 weeks Saturday till I blast Klitschko 
spark out, #&still!!!! Can’t wait #manchesteroctober29 #letshaveit”.
