
Borussia Dortmund <https://www.theguardian.com/football/borussiadortmund> have 
signed André Schürrle from VfL Wolfsburg on a five-year contract, last season’s 
Bundesliga runners-up said in a statement on Friday. Financial terms of the 
deal were not disclosed.

The Germany forward, 25, joined Wolfsburg 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/wolfsburg> from Chelsea in February 2015 
and scored nine goals in the top flight last season as the club finished eighth 
in the standings.
