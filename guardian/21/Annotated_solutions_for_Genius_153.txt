interchanged. The definition for each is contained in the clue for the other 
word in the pair.

** Fourteen other clues contain a superfluous word; when correctly linked, 
these give two-word definitions of the seven common word pairs: Mills & Boon 
(publishing people), law and order (civil obedience), Cakes and Ale (Somerset 
Maugham book), hale and hearty (very well), slap and tickle(sex play), kiss and 
tell (intimate revelation), park and ride (transport system).

The remaining seven clues are normal.
