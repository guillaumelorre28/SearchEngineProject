
Liverpool <https://www.theguardian.com/football/liverpool> are close to 
completing the signing of Loris Karius from Jürgen Klopp’s former club Mainz in 
a deal worth around £4.7m.

Klopp, who vowed there would be changes to the Liverpool squad this summer in 
the aftermath ofWednesday’s Europa League final defeat to Sevilla 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/blog/2016/may/19/jurgen-klopp-dortmund-liverpool-europa-league-final>
, wants the 22-year-old goalkeeper to increase competition with Simon Mignolet 
for next season.

Talks between the two clubs have progressed in recent days and a deal for 
Karius, who spent time with Manchester City as a teenager, could be completed 
next week.
