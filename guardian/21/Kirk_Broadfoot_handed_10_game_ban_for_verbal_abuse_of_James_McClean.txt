
Rotherham United’s Kirk Broadfoot has been handed a 10-match suspension by the 
FA after targeting James McClean with verbal abuse during a match withWigan 
Athletic <https://www.theguardian.com/football/wiganathletic>.

Broadfoot, a Scotland international and former Rangers defender, was charged 
for misconduct following an altercation with the Republic of Ireland winger 
during a match on 14 March. Broadfoot has also been ordered to pay a fine of 
£7,500 and complete an education programme.

a member of the opposition. Broadfoot was also fined £7,500 and ordered to
