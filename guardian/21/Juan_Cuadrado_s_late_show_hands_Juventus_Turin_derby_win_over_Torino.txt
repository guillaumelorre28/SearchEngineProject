
Substitute Juan Cuadrado scored three minutes into added time as Juventus 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/juventus> avoided dropping further into 
crisis with a 2-1 win over rivals Torino in a fine Turin derby.

Cuadrado slid into the box and redirected a cross from Alex Sandro seconds 
before the final whistle of an entertaining match at the Juventus Stadium.

Cesare Bovo had equalised early in the second half after Paul Pogba put 
Juventus ahead early on.

In an exciting climax, Juventus defender Leonardo Bonucci hit the crossbar in 
the 90th minute, before Cuadrado, who came on for the injured Sami Khedira 
early in the first half, made the decisive breakthrough.

Juventus moved into 10th place, level with Torino 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/torino> and Sampdoria, but eight points 
behind leaders Roma, who took on Inter Milan at the San Siro.

It was only the fourth win in 11 matches for Juventus, who have won the last 
fourSerie A <https://www.theguardian.com/football/serieafootball> titles.
