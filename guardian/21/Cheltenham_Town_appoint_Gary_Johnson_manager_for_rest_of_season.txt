
Cheltenham <https://www.theguardian.com/football/cheltenham> Town have 
appointed Gary Johnson as manager until the end of the season.

Johnson, who was sacked by Yeovil in February, has the immediate task of 
keeping Cheltenham in the Football League. They lie bottom ofLeague Two 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/leaguetwofootball> and face fellow 
strugglers York City away on Friday in his first game in charge.

The 59-year-old told the club’s website: “I understand the position the club 
are in and I will be doing my best along with [assistant] Russell [Milton] to 
keep the club in the Football League.”
