
Iñaki Williams has signed a new contract with Athletic Bilbao 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/athleticbilbao> that includes a release 
clause of €50m to ward off interest from several English clubs for the Spain 
Under-21 forward.

Liverpool, Manchester City, Arsenal and Tottenham have scouted the 21-year-old 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2016/jan/15/liverpool-inaki-williams-athletic-bilbao>
, who is a product of Bilbao’s academy, in recent weeks and were aware of his 
contract situation. However, with Atlético Madrid and Real Madrid also rumoured 
to be interested in signing Williams, the Basque club has now secured one of 
its brightest prospects until 2021.

“Athletic Club and Iñaki Williams have signed the renewal agreement by which 
the player will remain in the club until 30 June 2021,”read a statement 
<http://www.athletic-club.eus/en/news/16247/inaki-williams-contract-renewed.html>
 on Athletic’s website. “The cancellation clause will be €50m.”
