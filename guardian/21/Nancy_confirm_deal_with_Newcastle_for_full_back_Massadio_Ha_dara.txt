
Nancy have confirmed they have agreed a deal with Newcastle United 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/newcastleunited> for the defender 
Massadio Haïdara.

The 20-year-old full back has made 17 appearances in Ligue 1 this season and 
will today head to the north east for a medical.

"After 46 games and two-and-a-half years in the Nancy 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/nancy> shirt, Massadio Massadio Haïdara 
is leaving his former club for Newcastle United, currently sixteenth in the 
Premier League," a statement on the club's website read. "The two clubs have 
reached an agreement and will finalise the transfer on Tuesday."

On Monday, Mapou Yanga-Mbiwa 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2013/jan/21/newcastle-united-montpellier-yanga-mbiwa>
 was scheduled to undergo a medical at the club before a £7m move from 
Montpellier.
