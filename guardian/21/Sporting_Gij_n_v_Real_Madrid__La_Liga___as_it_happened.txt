Gijon bring on Rachid for Carmona. Real bring on Dani Carvajal for Danilo. “If

been very impressed by Sporting Gijon tonight. They’ve managed to keep Real at 
arms length, reducing them to difficult passes and shots unlikely to go in. The 
teams below the La Liga top four get disparaged quite often, but on this 
evidence “the rest” is quite good indeed.”
