Theophane, who is promoted by Floyd Mayweather 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/floyd-mayweather>, has won his last six 
fights since losing to Pablo César Cano in 2013. He is not ranked in the top 15 
by any of boxing’s four sanctioning bodies but, speaking on Thursday, 
Mayweather revealed that the 35-year-old will get a shot at Broner, who won the 
title against Russia’s Khabib Allakhverdiev in October.

“My fighter Ashley is from the UK … him and Adrien Broner will fight in 
Atlanta,” Mayweather said.

“We doing a big show in Atlanta at the end of January. Ashley and Adrien will 
be fighting for the title.”
