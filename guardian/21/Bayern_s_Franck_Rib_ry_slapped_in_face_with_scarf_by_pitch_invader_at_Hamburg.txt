
Franck Ribéry <https://www.theguardian.com/football/franck-ribery> was slapped 
in the face by a pitch invader wielding a scarf during Bayern Munich’s 3-1 win 
at Hamburg in the German Cup.

The supporter made his way on to the pitch in injury-time and appeared to hit 
the French winger near the halfway line before making a gesture with his hands.

“Fortunately, nothing much happened,’’ Ribéry told German television.

Ribéry scored the third goal for Pep Guardiola’s team in the 55th minute, 
after Robert Lewandowski and David Alaba scored in the first half.
