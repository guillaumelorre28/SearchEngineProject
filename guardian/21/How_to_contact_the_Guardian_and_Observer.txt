Corrections and clarifications of editorial content – the readers’ editors*
It is the policy of the Guardian and the Observer to correct significant 
editorial errors and handle editorial complaints as soon as possible. The 
readers’ editors are independent internal ombudsmen and can be contacted as 
follows:

Paul Chadwick – Guardian global readers’ editor
guardian.readers@theguardian.com <mailto:guardian.readers@theguardian.com>

observer.readers@observer.co.uk <mailto:observer.readers@observer.co.uk>

Please note that queries about subscriptions, vouchers, reader offers, 
advertising, marketing and other non-editorial areas are not handled by the 
readers’ editors. These should be addressed to the relevant non-editorial 
departments. Story suggestions should also be sent to the relevant editorial 
departments.
