
If Gaby Hinsliff (Self-help works for us as individuals – but as a society 
we’re failing 
<https://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/2016/dec/22/self-help-individuals-society-self-improvement-tolerance>
, 23 December) wants to see at first hand the power of singing together (badly) 
for passion and the joy of coming together to celebrate, she could do much 
worse than go to any lower league (or non-league) football match. Even when 
we’re not playing well, the capacity of being together and singing together to 
raise the spirits is unequalled. I should mention that the joy is probably 
greater at AFC Wimbledon than most other clubs.
Rupert Jeffery
London
