
Antoine Griezmann scored twice for Atlético Madrid 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/atleticomadrid> to salvage a 2-2 draw 
away at Málaga

The draw kept them in third place in La Liga 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/laligafootball>, but it leaves them seven 
points behind Real Madrid in second spot.

Griezmann scored his first goal in the 25th minute when Málaga’s goalkeeper 
Carlos Kameni spilled a throw-in, leaving the Atlético forward with a simple 
tap in.

Fortune swung Málaga’s way after 37 minutes when Atlético’s Fernando Torres 
scored an own goal while trying to make a clearance from a corner-kick.

The second-half featured a series of scoring chances for both sides at La 
Rosaleda.

Samuel García chipped the goalkeeper Jan Oblak to put Málaga 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/malaga> ahead in the 71st minute, but 
Madrid would not lie down and Griezmann scored his second goal with just 12 
minutes remaining, earning the visitors a draw.
