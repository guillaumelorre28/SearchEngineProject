
Everton <https://www.theguardian.com/football/everton> have agreed a deal to 
sign the teenage defender Matty Foulds from Bury.

The Premier League club announced on Thursday that the 17-year-old was set to 
join Roberto Martínez’s side for an undisclosed fee in January.

Foulds, a product of Bury’s academy, has made two senior appearances for the 
League One club to date, the first coming in August when he came on as a 
substitute in theCapital One Cup defeat to Leicester City 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2015/aug/26/leicester-city-joe-dodoo-bury-hat-trick>
.

A week later he played the full 90 minutes of a Johnstone’s Paint Trophy 
victory over Accrington Stanley.
