
Lucas Moura struck twice after the interval for a second-string Paris 
Saint-Germain <https://www.theguardian.com/football/parisstgermain> team as the 
Ligue 1 champions triumphed 2-0 at lowly Guingamp on Saturday.

Lucas opened the scoring from the penalty spot after 18-year-old Christopher 
Nkunku was brought down by Jérémy Sorbon in the 56th minute as he burst into 
the box.

The Brazil forward added a second goal in the 71st minute when he finished 
calmly from inside the area following Layvin Kurzawa’s cut-back.
