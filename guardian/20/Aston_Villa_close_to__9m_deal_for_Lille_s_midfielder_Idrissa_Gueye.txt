
Aston Villa <https://www.theguardian.com/football/aston-villa> are close to 
sealing a £9m deal for the Lille midfielder Idrissa Gueye, who is expected to 
fly into Birmingham for transfer talks on Thursday.

Gueye has been linked with several other Premier League clubs, including 
Southampton, West Ham United and Crystal Palace, but Villa have agreed a fee 
withLille <https://www.theguardian.com/football/lille> and hope to finalise the 
signing of the 25-year-old within the next 24 hours.

Gueye has played more than 130 games for Lille since making his debut for the 
Ligue 1 club in 2010 and would become Tim Sherwood’s second signing of the 
summer, followingMicah Richards’ move from Manchester City 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2015/jun/17/aston-villa-micah-richards-transfer-centre-half>
 on a four-year contract last month.

The Senegalese international, who has also been attracting interest from 
Marseille, could be Villa’s biggest outlay in the transfer market for four 
years.
