
Chelsea have signed Michy Batshuayi from Marseille on a five-year contract. 
The 22-year-old Belgium striker, who has joinedfor a reported £33.2m 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2016/jul/02/belgium-chelsea-michy-batshuayi-marseille-fans-leaving-summer>
 after two years at the Stade Vélodrome, said: “I am delighted to sign for one 
of the biggest clubs in Europe.

“Eden Hazard and Thibaut Courtois have told me many good things about the club 
and, with Antonio Conte coming in, it’s an exciting time to become aChelsea 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/chelsea> player. This is a fantastic 
opportunity.”

Batshuayi began his career at Standard Liège and joined Marseille in the 
summer of 2014, scoring 15 goals in his first season. He took a step forward 
last season with 26, including 17 in Ligue 1 even asMarseille 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/marseille> struggled to 13th in the table.
