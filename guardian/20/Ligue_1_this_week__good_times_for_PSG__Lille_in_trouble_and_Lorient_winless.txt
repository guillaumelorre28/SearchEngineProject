Zlatan returned from injury in Le Classique to make his 100th appearance for 
PSG in all competitions, scoring 83 goals since joining the club in July 2012. 
He also celebrated another win on Monday, picking up his eighth ‘Guldbollen’, 
the Swedish footballer of the year award, and his ninth overall. In his 
acceptance speech he paid tribute to two Swedish footballers who lost their 
lives to cancer this year, as well as his own brother who he lost a couple of 
months ago.
