
Joanna Lumley, Twelfth Night

‘I left no ring with her’

Joanna Lumley <https://www.theguardian.com/culture/joanna-lumley> plays Viola 
in Twelfth Night. Viola, disguised as a page, wonders why Olivia has sent her a 
ring – and whether Olivia has fallen in love with her.

for him to murder Duncan, he has a vision of a dagger.

virginity.
