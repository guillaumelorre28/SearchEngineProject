
Lionel Messi <https://www.theguardian.com/football/lionel-messi> suffered a 
knee injury in the opening stages of Barcelona’s La Liga match against Las 
Palmas, forcing his substitution after 10 minutes and leaving him on the 
sidelines for up to two months.

The Argentina forward went down after just three minutes, hurting his knee 
after shooting under pressure from theLas Palmas 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/laspalmas> defender Pedro Bigas Rigo. 
Messi received treatment and returned to the pitch, but was unable to continue 
and was substituted by Luis Enrique, with Munir El Haddadi coming on in his 
place.

He’ll be out for approximately seven to eight weeks.”

Messi’s injury compounded a bad week for the defending Spanish champions. They
