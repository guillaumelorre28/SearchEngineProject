
Martín Demichelis has signed for Espanyol 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/espanyol> on a one-year contract.

The 35-year-old centre-back was out of contract with Manchester City 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/manchestercity> where he spent three 
seasons and was part of the team who won the Premier League in 2014.

After starting his career at River Plate, the Argentinian won four Bundesliga 
titles with Bayern Munich before his first stint in Spain with Málaga.

Espanyol have also hired the former Watford manager Quique Sánchez Flores 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2016/jun/09/quique-sanchez-flores-named-new-watford-head-coach>
.
