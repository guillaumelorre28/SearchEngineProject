
Tottenham Hotspur <https://www.theguardian.com/football/tottenham-hotspur> 
have reached agreement with Montpellier to sign the defensive midfielder 
Benjamin Stambouli.

The 24-year-old former France Under-21 international is heading to England to 
have a medical.

“MHSC and Tottenham Hotspur FC have reached an agreement regarding the 
transfer of Benjamin Stambouli,” a statement on the Ligue 1 club’s website said.

“He will go to London in the next few hours to undergo a medical and finalise 
his contract.”
