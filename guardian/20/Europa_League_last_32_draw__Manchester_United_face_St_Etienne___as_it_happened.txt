The Europa League sometimes gets a bad rep, but it’s an important competition 
for Tottenham andManchester United 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/manchester-united>. Neither of them look 
like winning the league at the moment and they’re not in the top four either, 
so this could be their best chance of qualifying for the Champions League. 
Mauricio Pochettino has played weakened sides in the Europa League in the past 
two seasons and Tottenham have had their backsides handed to them by Borussia 
Dortmund and Fiorentina. He needs to be more ambitious this time, whoever 
Tottenham get.

The Europa League sometimes gets a bad rep, but it’s an important competition 
for Tottenham andManchester United 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/manchester-united>. Neither of them look 
like winning the league at the moment and they’re not in the top four either, 
so this could be their best chance of qualifying for the Champions League. 
Mauricio Pochettino has played weakened sides in the Europa League in the past 
two seasons and Tottenham have had their backsides handed to them by Borussia 
Dortmund and Fiorentina. He needs to be more ambitious this time, whoever 
Tottenham get.
