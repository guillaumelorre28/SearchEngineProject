
Tottenham have confirmed the signing of the Austria defender Kevin Wimmer from 
FCKöln <https://www.theguardian.com/football/cologne>.

As revealed by the Guardian 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2015/mar/20/tottenham-kevin-wimmer-fc-koln>
, a fee of €6m (£4.3m) for the 22-year-old was agreed in March and his move on 
a five-year contract has now been announced by Spurs.

“SIGNED: We are delighted to announce that we have completed the signing of 
@kevin28wimmer from 1. FC Köln,” said a post on Tottenham’s official Twitter 
account on Friday.

Wimmer has enjoyed a breakthrough season in Germany, starting in 24 of their 
25 Bundesliga matches. He becomes Tottenham’s first signing of the summer,with 
the club also interested in Burnley’s Kieran Trippier 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2015/may/26/bournemouth-join-tottenham-sign-kieran-trippier-burnley>
.
