Vigo’s pressure until the hosts tired andBarcelona

Xavi, who had come on as a second-half substitute, delivered the free-kick 
from which Mathieu struck at the far post, fending off Andreu Fontàs’ marking 
to head the ball in off the crossbar.Mathieu scored a similar header for the 
opening goal in last month’s 2-1 win over Real Madrid.

“It was a tough, very difficult game,” said the Frenchman. “Celta was better 
than us in the first half but sometimes games are decided in the details.”

Celta’s Fabián Orellana earned a straight red card with three minutes to play 
at the Balaídos after he threw a piece of turf and hit Sergio Busquets.

The result maintain’s Barcelona’s four-point advantage over Real at the top of
