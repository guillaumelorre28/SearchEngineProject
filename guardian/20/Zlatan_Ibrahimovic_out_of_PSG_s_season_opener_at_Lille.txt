
Zlatan Ibrahimovic <https://www.theguardian.com/football/zlatan-ibrahimovic> 
will miss Paris Saint-Germain’s season opener at Lille on Friday because of a 
knee injury.

The Sweden striker picked up the injury during the 2-0 win against Lyon 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2015/aug/01/paris-saint-germain-lyon-champions-trophy-psg-match-report>
 in the Champions Trophy last Saturday.

“Further checks showed a sprained ligament in his right knee,” the club said.
