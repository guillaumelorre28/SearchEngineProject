
Liverpool have made contact with the Poland international Piotr Zielinski 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2016/jan/28/liverpool-empoli-piotr-zielinski>
, according to the midfielder.

It was reported last month that Liverpool 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/liverpool> were set to hold talks over 
the signing of Zielinski, the 21-year-old who is on loan at Empoli from Udinese.

And on Monday he said: “There is a lot of truth in [talk of] the interest of 
[Liverpool manager]Jürgen Klopp 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/jurgen-klopp> in me. There was contact, I 
do not deny that.”

Zielinski scored in Empoli’s 2-0 Serie A victory over Fiorentina on Sunday.

He has 13 caps for Poland and is set to be part of their squad for Euro 2016.
