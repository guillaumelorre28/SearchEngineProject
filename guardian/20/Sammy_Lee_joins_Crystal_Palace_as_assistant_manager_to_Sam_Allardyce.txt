Crystal Palace <https://www.theguardian.com/football/crystalpalace> have 
appointed Sammy Lee as their new assistant manager, the Premier League club 
have announced.

Lee has joined the coaching staff at Selhurst Park, reuniting with new Palace 
boss Sam Allardyce, who he worked under during their time at Bolton from 2005 
until 2007.

The 57-year-old has also enjoyed coaching spells with the England national 
team as well as roles at Liverpool and Southampton at the end of last season.

Allardyce welcomed Lee to his management team at Selhurst Park 
<http://www.cpfc.co.uk/news/article/2016-17/sammy-lee-named-assistant-manager-of-the-club.-3511214.aspx>
, stating: “I am looking forward to once again working with Sammy. It was great 
working with him at Bolton and is someone who has a wealth of experience both 
at club and international level over the years as well.”
