Bournemouth <https://www.theguardian.com/football/bournemouth> lodged an 
appeal in the hope of getting the suspension overturned.

The FA confirmed in a statement on Thursday that the appeal had been 
unsuccessful, meaning Francis will miss the FA Cup third-round tie at Millwall 
on Saturday followed by Premier League games against Hull City and Watford. He 
has been an ever-present in the league this season.

“Simon Francis will serve a three-match suspension with immediate effect after 
his claim of wrongful dismissal was rejected following an Independent 
Regulatory Commission hearing,” a statement from the FA read.

“The Bournemouth defender was sent off for serious foul play during the game 
against Arsenal on Tuesday 3 January.”
