Hello. The big game in Spain this weekend is Barcelona’s trip to Sevilla this 
evening but Luis Enrique’s team could be five points behindReal Madrid 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/realmadrid> if they – and it would be 
some shock if the do not – beat struggling Leganés in this early kick-off.

The state of play midway through Round 11: Real are top, unbeaten and on 24 
points; Barça are second having suffered two defeats, with 22 points; Atlético 
are a further point behind having lost 2-0 at Sociedad last night, while 
Sevilla are behind them on goal difference.

For now, though, our attention is solely on events in Madrid. Kick-off is 12pm 
local time (11am GMT). Confirmed team news will be along in a few moments.

today. Less tweeting, more focus on the pitch!
 
90 min: There will be two minutes added on.
 
87 min: Rodriguez tries his luck. He gets it over the wall but it curls wide 
to Serantes’s right.
 
86 min: Ramos cannot continue, so Leganes are down to 10 men. Back on the 
pitch, Diaz hacks down Vazquez and is yellow carded. Rodriguez, Bale and 
Ronaldo are all eyeing up the free-kick.
 
83 min: Marcelo goes in late and hard on the achilles of Ramos, who then gets 
his ankle caught in the ground. That is nasty from the Real left back, who 
should really be booked.
 
82 min: And Leganes’s final swap is Diaz replacing the captain Mantovani.
 
81 min: Morata’s day is done. Vasquez comes in for the final nine minutes.
 
80 min: Bale is still hungry for his third. He shoots from just outside the 
box but it’s blocked by Rico after a lovely pass from Rodriguez.
 
79 min: Leganes, it should be said, have acquitted themselves well here. They 
are a limited team, but have worked hard and could have had a goal if it were 
not for Navas.
 
76 min: That will be it. Morata, who has had lumps kicked out of him, is set 
away by Modric. He takes a touch before curling the ball around Serantes. 
That’s his eighth of the season.
 
75 min: Leganes are going for it. Bustinza rampages forward and produces a 
wonderful turn before trying to pass from an inside right position. Real get 
rid.
 
72 min: Leganes have a huge chance to pull one back. Szymanowski is played 
through by Ramos with only Carvajal back for Real. He fends off the defender 
but is denied by an excellent save from Navas. The corner is cleared.
 
68 min: Real’s second change sees Rodriguez replace Isco. And Leganes’ second 
swap is Neves coming on for Perez.
 
66 min: It comes off the wall.
 
65 min: Another foul on Morata. Bustinza is the culprit this time. It should 
be a yellow card, but only the foul is given. Ronaldo will eye this one up from 
about 25 yards, left of the goal.
 
62 min: Modric makes his return at the Bernabeu, coming on for Kovacic.
 
61 min: Mantovani is booked for dragging down Morata – again.
 
61 min: Leganes have their first attempt on target. And it’s not very good. 
Lopes rolls the ball at Navas from 20 yards.
 
59 min: Bustinza gets a firm talking to after he drags down Morata during a 
Real counterattack.
 
57 min: Leganes are giving away a lot of cheap fouls now and are unable to get 
out of their own half. Conceding just before the break has really deflated them.
 
55 min: Leganes make their first change. Ramos comes in for Machis.
 
54 min: It took Real 38 minutes to have a shot on target but in the first 
seven minutes of the second period they have already had three. A third only 
seems like a matter of time.
 
52 min: Bale eyes up a free-kick from 35 yards. It is heading for the top 
right corner but takes a slight deflection and goes wide. The corner is given 
short to Marcelo and Leganes clear. Real have been excellent since the break.
 
48 min: Bale is denied his hat-trick after breaking clear, Serantes denying 
him with a good save. And then a few seconds later, Ronaldo finds Bale from the 
left. This time his header is goalbound but the Leganes keeper reacts quickly 
to save.
 
48 min: Kroos threads a lovely pass to Bale who then tries to find Ronaldo 
from the right. Insua gets there first to head away.
 
46 min: Real get us going again.
 
And that’s the break. Real have been poor but Bale’s brace late in the half 
means they are comfortable without moving up the gears. Leganes were doing so 
well but the hosts have converted their only shots on target.
 
45 min +1: Bale taps in from close range after a Kroos free-kick is knocked 
down by Varane. Then Morata plays it across goal and Bale finishes it off. 
Simple.
 
44 min: A chance for Leganes as Szymanowski has a shot deflected over for a 
corner. Real are unhappy because the visitors continued to play while Bale went 
down injured following an aerial challenge. The corner, meanwhile, is cleared.
 
42 min: Insua cynically rugby tackles Morata from behind as the striker broke 
from midfield. And he’s booked too.
 
40 min: Kroos and Nacho are both booked after the former clips Machis out wide 
and the latter complains that it wasn’t a foul. It was maybe a free kick but 
the card looks harsh.
 
38 min: He has been quiet up until now but the Wales star puts Real in front 
with the game’s first shot on target. Isco plays a sublime diagonal ball from 
the left that catches Leganes on the hop for the first time and Bale nips in, 
rounds Serantes, who did get a hand to the ball, and tucks into the open goal 
from a tightish angle.
 
37 min: Ronaldo misses the target from just outside the area, scuffing a 
right-footed drive to the right of Serantes’ goal.
 
33 min: Morata wins a free-kick just inside his own half and when play 
restarts he then commits a foul of his own.
 
30 min: Will there be a shot on target before half-time? Real have just won 
their first corner, so maybe. Kroos jogs over to take but it’s cleared at the 
near post.
 
28 min: This has been pony.
 
26 min: Gabirel almost finds Szymanowski with a neat flick in the box but the 
ball ends up going out of play. That’s a worry for Real, who do not look good 
in defence.
 
25 min: Kroos feeds Isco. He has time and space but shoots well over.
 
22 min: Ronaldo’s first chance is blown over, which is fair on Leganes because 
he was a yard offside. Varane found him with a long ball that evaded Insua and 
bounced intothe social media star’s path 
<https://twitter.com/Cristiano/status/794567817962070016>. He chose power over 
accuracy and missed the target by some way. We are still waiting for a first 
shot on target.
 
22 min: Bustinza fouls Ronaldo just inside Leganes’s half. It’s taken quickly 
and the ball is worked out to the right. There, Bale picks up possession and 
sends a good cross towards Morata near the back stick. He tries to score from a 
tight angle when he may have been better off trying to head across goal. It 
goes onto the roof of the net.
 
20 min: Another awful pass from Perez, who overhits it for a goal-kick.
 
18 min: Marcelo shanks an attempted pass into the stands. More boos from the 
home fans, but for a different reason. This has been pretty poor from Real.
 
17 min: Boo! Hiss! Jeer! Ronaldo is booked for dissent after having a good old 
moan at the referee following a tackle from Perez.
 
16 min: Szymanowski goes down in the box after no contact. Play, correctly, 
continues.
 
14 min: Maybe it was too early for Real to have their porridge this morning … 
Ronaldo plays what should be a simple lateral pass behind Isco and throws his 
arms up in frustration.
 
13 min: Varane opts for a long ball down the right to find an advancing 
Carvajal but there is too much on the pass and it trickles out for a goal-kick.
 
11 min: Leganes will be happy with this. They are sitting deep and keeping it 
tight, meaning Real’s front three have barely had a touch.
 
9 min: Kroos’s delivery is met by Mantovani’s head. Rico follows up the hoof 
clear.
 
8 min: Machis is booked for tripping Marcelo from behind as the Real left back 
tries to attack.
 
7 min: You can tell these players are not used to playing at such an early 
time of day. It has been a ragged start with no clear opportunity in front of 
goal and far too many poor passes.
 
4 min: Carvajal, who was born in Leganes, has not started very well against 
his hometown club – he has given away possession cheaply twice from right back.
 
3 min: Real enjoy a spell of possession at the back but Leganes break the play 
up and Insua hopelessly shoots wide from silly range.
 
2 min: Not a bad start from the visitors, who attack down the left through 
Szymanowski before he runs out of room and gives Real a goal-kick. He will be 
key if Leganes are to get an unlikely result here.
