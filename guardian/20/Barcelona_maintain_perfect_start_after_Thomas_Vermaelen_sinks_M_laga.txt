
Thomas Vermaelen scored his first goal for Barcelona 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/barcelona> in a laboured 1-0 win at home 
to Málaga on Saturday that maintained a perfect start to their La Liga title 
defence.

With a fit-again Neymar restored to the attack alongside Lionel Messi and Luis 
Suárez for the first time this season, Barcelona were looking to the South 
American trio to break down a stubbornMálaga 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/malaga> at the Camp Nou.

However, it was Vermaelen who finally broke through following relentless 
pressure when he fired a loose ball into the net from a 73rd-minute corner.
