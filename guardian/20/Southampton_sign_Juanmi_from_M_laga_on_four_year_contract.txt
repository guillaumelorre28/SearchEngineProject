
Southampton <https://www.theguardian.com/football/southampton> have made 
Juanmi their first signing of the summer after securing the Spanish forward on 
a four-year contract.

The 22-year-old arrives at St Mary’s from Málaga 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/malaga>, for whom he scored eight goals 
last season, including the winner away to Barcelona.

A series of tweets published through Saints’ official account confirmed the 
transfer of the player who won the Under-19 European Championship with Spain in 
2011 and 2012.
