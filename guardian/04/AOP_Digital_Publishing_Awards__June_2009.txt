
GNM picked up three awards at the AOP Digital Publishing Awards 2009 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards-2009> on 2 June 2009, 
after receiving 10 nominations across 17 categories - the most for a newspaper 
website. The prizes were awarded in the following categories:

Editorial Team - Consumer
Winner: Comment is Free - Guardian News & Media
Judges said: "This editorial team brings traditional editorial rigour and 
values to the future of online citizen journalism - it rocks!"

Research & Insights Project
Winner: The Brand Aid Panel - Guardian News & Media
Judges said: "A timely research project for these recessionary times which 
delivers effective R.O.I. information to advertisers."

The Digital Advertising Sales Team were also highly commended.
