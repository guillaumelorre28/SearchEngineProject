
The Guardian has won two prizes at the prestigious Webby Awards 2011 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards-2011>.

A multimedia report on Haiti six months after the earthquake won best News & 
Politics: Series, and a film made by the photographer Briony Campbell with her 
dying father, 'Saying goodbye with my camera', won the People's Voice category 
for Documentary: Individual Episode.

Established in 1996, the Webbys <http://www.webbyawards.com/> are the leading 
international awards honouring excellence on the internet.
