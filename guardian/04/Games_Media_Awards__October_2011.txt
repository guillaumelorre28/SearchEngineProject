
The Guardian <https://www.theguardian.com/international> has topped the Games 
Coverage by a National Newspaper category at theGames Media Awards 
<http://www.mcvuk.com/events/read/games-media-awards-2011> (GMAs) for the fifth 
consecutive year.

The awards, which honour video game coverage across the specialist and 
mainstream sectors, is voted for by a cross-section of games industry 
professionals.

To read more on the awards, and the Guardian's achievement, please visit our 
Games Blog 
<https://www.theguardian.com/technology/gamesblog/2011/oct/27/guardian-games-media-awards>
