
The Guardian has won a prestigious prize from the Society for News Design 
(SND), in the inaugural year of their annual Best of Digital News Design 
competition.

Announced on Saturday 1 October, the Guardian's iPhone app 
<https://www.theguardian.com/mobile/iphone/guardian> was named the World's Best 
Designed mobile app.

Judges said: "Making a news app feel different from other news apps is an 
almost impossible feat, but the Guardian'siPhone app 
<https://www.theguardian.com/mobile/iphone/guardian> rose to the challenge and 
has created an app that utilizes its space effectively yet beautifully..."

More information on the SND website 
<http://www.snd.org/2011/10/worlds-best-designed/>.
