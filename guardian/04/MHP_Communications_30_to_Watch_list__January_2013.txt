
Three Guardian journalists have been named in the 'MHP 30 to Watch' list, 
which was revealed during the evening of Thursday 17 January 2013.

James Ball <https://www.theguardian.com/profile/jamesball>, Adam Gabbatt 
<https://www.theguardian.com/profile/adam-gabbatt> and Josh Halliday 
<https://www.theguardian.com/profile/josh-halliday> were selected by a number 
of senior journalists and were chosen for their work being above and beyond the 
industry standard.

The awards, which launched last year, recognise the contribution of young 
journalists to the UK media industry and celebrate work that makes a real 
impact and uses new, innovative methods to cover the news agenda.
