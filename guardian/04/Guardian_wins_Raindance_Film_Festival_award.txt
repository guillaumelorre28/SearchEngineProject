
The Guardian’s first virtual reality experience, ‘6x9: A virtual experience of 
solitary confinement 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/ng-interactive/2016/apr/27/6x9-a-virtual-experience-of-solitary-confinement>
’ won the audience award for best virtual reality film at this year’s Raindance 
Film Festival awards.

The awards showcase features, shorts, music videos, virtual reality films and 
web series by filmmakers from the UK and around the world.

The full list of winners can be found here 
<http://raindancefestival.org/winners-2016/>.
