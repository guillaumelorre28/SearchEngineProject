
The British Council for Offices (BCO) has named GNM's floors of Kings Place 
London's 'best corporate workspace' at their annual awards ceremony.

Judges commented on a "thriving busy workplace that has allowed the improved 
interaction necessary to create a newspaper, website and broadcaster 24 hours a 
day."
