
The Guardian has won the Best Website award and Best Use of Online video award 
- for its global guide to the first world war 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/ng-interactive/2014/jul/23/a-global-guide-to-the-first-world-war-interactive-documentary>
 - at the European Digital Media Awards.

Of the website, the judges commented:

“The Guardian, has long been a leader in digital innovation having evolved 
from a British to a global news website in the face of notable competition from 
international rivals.”

The Guardian also received a special mention in the Best Mobile Service 
category for our app.
