
The Guardian has been named Newspaper of the Year at the Football Supporters’ 
Federation 
<http://www.fsf.org.uk/latest-news/view/the-2015-fsf-awards-with-william-hill-results>
 (FSF) awards for the third year in a row.

The Football Supporters’ Federation awards celebrate the best aspects of the 
sport - from bloggers and fanzines to commentators and pundits.

James Dart, the Guardian sport digital editor, said: “It’s an absolute honour 
to collect the award on behalf of everyone in the sports department at the 
Guardian.”
