
A Guardian investigation into the mortality rates in NHS vascular surgery 
across the UK has been given a special commendation in the Royal Statistical 
Society's statistical excellence in journalism awards.

The investigation, by the health editor, Sarah Boseley, Rob Evans and Gozde 
Zorlu, was published in June 2010.
