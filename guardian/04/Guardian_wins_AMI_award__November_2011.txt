
The Guardian has won Ability Media International's (AMI 
<http://www.abilitymedia.org/>) People's Choice award for journalistic 
excellence.

Created by Leonard Cheshire Disability and presented on Sunday 20 November, 
the award recognises the Guardian's outstanding coverage ofdisability issues 
<https://www.theguardian.com/society/disability>. Voted for by members of the 
public, this is the first year that the People's Choice award for journalistic 
excellence has been given.

For more information visit the AMI awards website 
<http://www.amiawards.org/awards-2011>.
