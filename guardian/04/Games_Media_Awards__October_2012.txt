
Guardian journalist Keith Stuart has been honoured at the Games Media Awards 
2012 <http://www.mcvuk.com/news/read/gma12-the-winners-in-full/0104870>, 
receiving the top prize in the 'writer for a national newspaper category'.

The awards recognise video game coverage across the specialist and mainstream 
sectors and are voted for by a selection of games industry professionals.

Click here <https://www.theguardian.com/technology/gamesblog> to visit the 
Guardian's Games Blog.
