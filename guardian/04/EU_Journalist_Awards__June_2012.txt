
Observer writer Mark Townsend has won an EU Journalist Award 
<http://journalistaward.stop-discrimination.info/?lang=en> for his article The 
Face of Slavery in Modern Europe 
<https://www.theguardian.com/law/2011/oct/16/britains-child-sex-trade?INTCMP=SRCH>
. The European Commission 'together against discrimination' award recognises 
work considered to have helped tackle prejudice across Europe.

Mark Townsend's Observer Magazine investigation into the trafficking of 
foreign children to south London was described by one of the judges as "a real 
page turner" that reveals the systematic problems that face vulnerable 
youngsters.
