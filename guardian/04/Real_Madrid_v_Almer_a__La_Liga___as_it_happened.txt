So Madrid couldn’t get close to Barcelona’s efforts of last night, though 
James’ goal was an absolute jazzer. Basically, they’re just not as good, but 
still good enough, so are still in the hunt. Walking off the pitch, Ronaldo 
does not look at all happy, but I imagine he’ll cope.

his goal.

86 min No Odegaard - Nacho comes on for Varane. However will we cope; won’t 
someone please think of the algorithms?
 
Much sniggering, as Isco waves his foot over the ball just outside the box, 
left of centre, then snaps a ball for Hernandez outside him. He crosses low and

net. Except Arbeloa arrives in front of him and taps home, so Ronaldo looks all 
disappointed.

51 min Odegard is warming up!
