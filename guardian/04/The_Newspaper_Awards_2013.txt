
Guardian News & Media have won four prizes at The Newspaper Awards 2013 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards-2013>, which were 
announced at a ceremony in London on Wednesday 24 April.

- Fujifilm Grand Prix: The Guardian Print Centre for the bookmark
- Printed Innovation of the Year: The Guardian Print Centre for the bookmark 
- The Environment Award: Guardian News & Media, Volunteering Week
- Digital News Service of the Year: theguardian.com

The awards celebrate the very best in the newspaper industry and are held 
annually.

Click here <http://www.newspaperawards.co.uk/index.php?pid=6> for a full list 
of winners.
