
GNM's Customer Insight team picked up the award for Best In-House Research 
Team at theMarket Research Society Research Awards 2010 
<http://www.mrs.org.uk/awards/index.htm>, beating off competition from BSkyB, 
Capital One, Channel 4, Digital UK, O2 and Pfizer.

The judges said: "With its innovative and effective ways of working set 
against the backdrop of a decrease in year on year budgets, GNM has completely 
aced it on influencing the business. Research has been built into the 
development of every product and a huge amount has been achieved by a dedicated 
and driven team."
