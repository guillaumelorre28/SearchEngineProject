
The Guardian and Observer have had great success at the Record of the Day 
Awards <https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards> for Music 
Journalism and PR.

There were category wins for Alexis Petridis (Record Reviews: Writer of the 
Year); The Guardian (Best Music Coverage in a National Newspaper); Observer 
Music Monthly (Magazine of the Year). Also making the shortlist were: Paul 
Lester (Breaking Music: Writer of the Year); The Guide (Best Music Coverage in 
a National Newspaper); Alexis Petridis (Live Reviews: Writer of the Year).
