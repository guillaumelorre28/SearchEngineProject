
Guardian US has been named best of show in the online category at the National 
Headliner Awards <http://www.headlinerawards.org/> - awarded by the Press Club 
of Atlantic City - forthe Counted 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/ng-interactive/2015/jun/01/the-counted-police-killings-us-database>
.

Senior reporters Jon Swaine and Oliver Laughland, plus the wider Guardian US 
staff, were recognized for their work documenting the the number of deaths 
resulting from the use of force by law enforcement officers across the US.

The National Headliner Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards> program is one of the 
oldest and largest annual contests recognizing journalistic merit in the US.

More information, including a full list of winners, can be found here 
<http://www.headlinerawards.org/>.
