
The Guardian US has won the 2015 Amnesty International Media Award in Digital 
Innovation forThe Counted 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/ng-interactive/2015/jun/01/the-counted-police-killings-us-database>
. Journalists Jon Swaine, Oliver Laughland and Jamiles Larteywere honored at 
this year’s awards ceremony <http://amnesty.keystone-group.co.uk/>, which took 
place at Milton Court in London on Thursday, November 26.

Amnesty’s coveted awards recognize excellence in human rights reporting and 
journalism’s contribution to public awareness of human rights issues.

For more information on the Amnesty International Media Awards visit here 
<http://amnesty.keystone-group.co.uk/>.
