
GuardianWitness <https://witness.theguardian.com/> has won three Lovie awards: 
'Social News and Information', 'Mobile and Applications' and the People's Award.

The Lovie Awards <http://www.lovieawards.eu/> recognise the unique nature of 
the European internet community, from Europe's top web and creative networks 
and content publishers to cultural and political organisations and bedroom 
bloggers.

Now in its third year, The Lovie Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards> celebrate the most 
pertinent stories in Europe and reflect the growth of the internet as a tool 
for business and everyday lives.
