
Following the Ashes whitewash the England and Wales Cricket 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/cricket> Board yesterday announced the 
launch of a wide-ranging inquiry aimed at recovering the urn at the earliest 
available opportunity in 2009, as well as improving England's dismal one-day 
form which has seen them slip to eighth in the world rankings.

David Collier, the ECB <https://www.theguardian.com/sport/ecb> chief 
executive, said: "The performance in the Ashes series has been a great 
disappointment and a number of lessons must be learned."
