
Guardian News & Media has won three awards at the 2010 Production Journal 
Newspaper Awards <http://www.newspaperawards.co.uk/index.php?pid=6>.

The Guardian Print Centre beat the Times and the Telegraph to win the Best Use 
of Colour category and The Guardian won Best Use of Citizen / Community 
Journalism award for coverage of the G20 protests which judges desribed as 'an 
agenda-changing scoop'.

The Guardian iPhone app also won the Fujifilm Grand Prix category and was 
highly commended in the best use of new media category. Judges said: "Simple, 
clear and effective - a genuine revenue model for the future and the perfect 
use of new technology to increase reach of editorial content in a user-friendly 
app."
