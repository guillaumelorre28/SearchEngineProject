The super-agency and Victoria police were forced to cancel Operation Fortitude 
<https://www.theguardian.com/australia-news/2015/aug/28/we-shut-them-up-melbourne-celebrates-border-force-backdown>
 that was scheduled to take place at the weekend after a backlash from the 
community.

Many questions remain about just what was planned for the weekend and what 
other types of immigration operations have been under way.
