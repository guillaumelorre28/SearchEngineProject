
Guardian Jobs <http://jobs.theguardian.com/> has won a National Online 
Recruitment Award <http://www.norauk.com/> (NORA) for a record fifth year in 
succession.

The awards, whose aim is to identify and acknowledge the recruitment websites 
that work best for candidates, are highly regarded in the industry as they are 
judged by the general public and a panel of online recruitment experts.

The 11th annual awards event took place on Wednesday 2 November.

For more information on the awards visit the NORA website here 
<http://www.norauk.com/awards/>.
