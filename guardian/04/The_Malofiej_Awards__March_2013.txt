
Guardian News & Media's US interactive team have won three prestigious prizes 
for infographics atThe Malofiej Awards <http://www.malofiej21.com/>.

The Malofiej Awards <https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards> are 
considered one of the top honours for infographics and bring together some of 
the most prominent infographic artists, cartographers, programmers and visual 
journalists both online and in print.

- America: Elect! 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/interactive/2012/nov/06/america-elect-graphic-novel>
: our innovative graphic novel telling the story of Mitt Romney's presidential 
run, received a silver medal.

- Gay rights state by state 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/interactive/2012/may/08/gay-rights-united-states>
: our interactive, which was copied around the world, received a bronze medal.

- IsMittRomneyThePresident <http://www.ismittromneythepresident.com/> (along 
with sister site IsBarackObamaThePresident): the graphic launched several 
months before the election showing the current polling for each candidate and 
received a bronze medal.
