
Peter Preston has been named Media Commentator of the Year in the inaugural 
Editorial Intelligence CommentAwards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>, for his column in The 
Observer.

Other nominations included: The Observer, Best Comment Pages and Jeff Jarvis, 
also in the Media Commentator category.

The judging panel was chaired by the former Press Complaints Commission 
chairman Sir Christopher Meyer and the award ceremony was hosted by Peter York.
