
The Guardian has won an award at this years UK Agile Awards 
<http://www.agileawards.co.uk/> ceremony. The awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>, presented on Wednesday 5 
October, recognise people, projects and products that have used the Agile 
methodology of software and product development.

The Guardian won the Best Agile Team award, after being shortlisted alongside 
Napp Pharmeceuticals/Cardiff University and Simply Business.

For more information visit the UK Agile Awards website 
<http://www.agileawards.co.uk/Awards2011.html>.
