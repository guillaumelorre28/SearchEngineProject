
The Guardian’s website theguardian.com 
<https://www.theguardian.com/international> has been awarded Best News Website 
at the World Association of Newspapers and News Publishers’ (WAN-IFRA 
<http://www.wan-ifra.org/>) World Digital Media Awards 2015, which showcase 
innovation in the industry.

Judge Mario García of García Media described the Guardian’s website as 
“Perhaps one of the best digital displays for any newspaper in the world. I 
like the way the brand is carried out digitally, but also how storytelling 
plays such a key role.”
