
The Guardian has won a prestigious Rai (Radiotelevisione Italiana) Prix Italia 
award. The Guardian won the Best Multimedia Section prize for aninteractive page
 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/interactive/2011/mar/22/middle-east-protest-interactive-timeline>
 charting pro-democracy rebellions across the Middle East. This is the first 
year that an award has been given in this category and was presented to the 
Guardian on Friday 23 September.

Now in it's 63rd year, Prix Italia recognises international achievements in 
radio, television and the web. For more information visit the Prix Italiawebsite
 <http://www.prixitalia.rai.it/2011/EN/Default.aspx>.
