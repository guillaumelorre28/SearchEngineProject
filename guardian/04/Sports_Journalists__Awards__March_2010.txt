
The Guardian and Observer sports coverage has won three prizes 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2010/mar/09/guardian-sports-journalists-association-awards>
 at this year's Sports Journalists' Association awards. Nominated 22 times 
across 13 categories, GNM also received seven commendations.

David Conn won News Reporter of the Year; David Hills, Diarist of the Year and 
Donald McRae, Interviewer of the Year. Commendations went to Rob Smyth (Live 
Blog); Donald McRae (Feature Writer); David Conn (Sports Story); Tom Jenkins 
(Portfolio); Ashes coverage (Team of the Year); Paul Hayward and David Conn, 
placed second and third respectively, in the coveted Sports Writer of the Year 
category.
