
German midfielder Marko Marin has joined Belgian champions Anderlecht 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/anderlecht> - his third loan spell since 
signing for Chelsea in 2012.

Marin, who has a contract at Stamford Bridge until 2017, spent the first half 
of the season on loan atFiorentina 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/fiorentina> having been at Sevilla last 
season. However the 25-year-old made no Serie A appearances and will now move 
to the Belgian Pro League for the remainder of the season.

The Germany international has made just six Premier League appearances for 
Chelsea <https://www.theguardian.com/football/chelsea> since joining from 
Werder Bremen for £7m.

“RSC Anderlecht goes for quality and not quantity,” said Anderlecht’s general 
manager Herman Van Holsbeeck.

“The arrival of Marko Marin is a win-win situation for both parties. Here he 
can play at the highest level and help the purple and white achieve its 
ambitions.”
