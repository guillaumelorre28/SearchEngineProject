
The Guardian's Middle East Editor, Ian Black, has been awarded the Peace 
Through Media Award by theInternational Council for Press and Broadcasting 
<http://www.ncfpeace.org/drupal/node/448>.

Patron of the International Media Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>, Sheikh Mohamed Bin Issa 
said, "This award reflects the high estimation Ian Black is held in by his 
colleagues. This was vote was, uniquely perhaps in awards of this kind, a vote 
by his own peers."
