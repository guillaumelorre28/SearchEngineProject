
The Guardian, Observer and environmenttheguardian.com 
<http://www.environmenttheguardian.com> were awarded the top prize at the 
Environmental Journalism Awards yesterday, as the news organisation, website, 
publication or broadcaster judged to have excelled in the field of 
environmental journalism in 2009. David Adam was also shortlisted in the 
environmental journalist of the year category.

The awards, run by Press Gazette and Peugot, are the top green journalism 
awards in the UK.
