
Guardian journalists Paul Lewis and Tanya Gold have been named Reporter of the 
Year and Feature Writer of the Year respectively at the 2010 British PressAwards
 <https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>.

Paul received his award for his coverage of the death of Ian Tomlinson 
<https://www.theguardian.com/uk/2009/apr/07/ian-tomlinson-g20-death-video> at 
the G20 protests. In the Feature Writer category, judges said Tanya "laughed at 
herself but never at the reader".
