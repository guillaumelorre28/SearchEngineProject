
Andrew Sparrow, senior political correspondent for the Guardian website, has 
been honoured with the Mainstream Media Blogger prize at theEditorial 
Intelligence Comment Awards 2012 <http://www.commentawards.com/index.htm>. 

The awards, now in their fourth year, celebrate the achievements of the UK's 
finest print and online commentators and their editors. Winners were announced 
on Wednesday 17 October and were selected by a panel of industry leaders across 
the media, politics, business and culture.

Andrew writes a daily politics blog, Politics Live 
<https://www.theguardian.com/politics/series/politics-live-with-andrew-sparrow>
, for Guardian.co.uk.

information about the awards.
