
Tim Hayward has won the New Media award from the Guild of Food Writers for his 
work on the Guardian and Observer'sWord of Mouth 
<https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/wordofmouth> blog. Observer writer 
Jay Rayner was also shortlisted in the Restaurant Reviewer of the Year category.

Established in 1984, the Guild of Food Writers is an independent body that 
aims to contribute to the growth of public interest in, and knowledge of, food 
and to campaign for improvements in the quality of food produced and consumed 
in the UK.

The awards were presented by the legendary food critic Egon Ronay and other 
winners on the night included Jamie Oliver, Heston Blumenthal and the Riverford 
Farm Cook Book.
