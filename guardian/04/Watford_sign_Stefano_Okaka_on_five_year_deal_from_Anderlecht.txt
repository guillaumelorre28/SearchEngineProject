
The Italy striker Stefano Okaka has joined Watford on a five-year deal from 
Anderlecht <https://www.theguardian.com/football/anderlecht>. The Premier 
League side announced the signing on their website but gave no financial 
details of the transfer.

The 27-year-old helped Anderlecht to finish third in the league last season, 
scoring 17 goals, but has clashed with the new coach, René Weiler, according to 
the Belgian media.

The Italian has played in the Premier League before, spending part of the 2010 
season at Fulham on loan from Roma.

Watford have lost two and drawn one
