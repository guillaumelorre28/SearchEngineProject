
The Guardian has come top of Campaign Magazine's list of the ten best press 
ads of 2009 for its promotion of theclassic comics 
<https://www.theguardian.com/books/2009/sep/05/funny-business-free-comics-jon-ronson>
 series.

Campaign said, "The Guardian's comics giveaway was the best newspaper 
promotion of the year and these ads do it justice. The cut-outs of comic 
characters create great impact - especially the Dennis the Menace execution".
