
The Guardian's 'Three Little Pigs' TV campaign

London - won gold in the Film category for media and entertainment at this

The Guardian's accompanying print and outdoor ad campaign, 'The Whole Picture' 
- again created by BBH - also won gold, this time in the Integrated Campaigns 
category for multimedia.
