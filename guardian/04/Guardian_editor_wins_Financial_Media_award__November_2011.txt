
Last week Patrick Collinson, editor for Guardian Money 
<https://www.theguardian.com/uk/money>, won an award for Outstanding 
Achievement in Financial Journalism at this year'sFinancial Media awards 
<http://www.abi.org.uk/Events/2011/11/ABI_Financial_Media_Awards_2011/ABI_Financial_Media_Awards_2011.aspx>
.

Hosted by the Association of British Insurers <http://www.abi.org.uk/> (ABI) 
the awards recognise high standards in financial services journalism. The 
ceremony took place on Thursday 3 November at the Grange St Paul's hotel.

To read more on the awards, click here
