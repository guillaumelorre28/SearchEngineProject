
Guardian News and Media’s audience team have scooped the 2014 MRS award for 
best in-house research team.

Commenting on their decision, judges said: “We were amazed by the number of 
research projects the Guardian team undertakes each year. The team is strategic 
and has successfully embedded itself into the business… and the team is able to 
demonstrate impressive commercial returns on investment, the Holy Grail in 
research”.

Read the full list of winners here <https://www.mrs.org.uk/awards>.
