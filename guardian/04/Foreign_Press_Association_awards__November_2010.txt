
Guardian writer Saeed Kamali Dehghan has been named Journalist of the Year at 
theForeign Press Association awards <http://www.foreign-press.org.uk/> for his 
coverage of the 2009 Iranian presidential election.

Saeed also won the TV Feature/Documentary category for a film about the life 
and death of Iranian protestor Neda Agha-Soltan.
