
The Guardian and Observer's Chris Cook 
<https://www.theguardian.com/profile/chriscook> has won the John Oaksey Trophy 
for Racing Reporter of the Year at the Horserace Writers and Photographers 
Association Derby Awards.

The award was voted for by fellow industry professionals and was announced on 
Monday 3 December. Cook was previously shortlisted in 2010, the year he was 
first eligible for the prize, and this year saw off tough competition from the 
Guardian's Greg Wood – the 2009 winner – the Racing Post's Jon Lees and Marcus 
Armytage of the Telegraph.

Click here <http://www.hwpa.org/> for more information about the awards.
