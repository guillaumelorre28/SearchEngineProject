
The Guardian website has been named ‘Best News Website’ at the World Digital 
MediaAwards <https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>, hosted by 
WAN-IFRA, for its coverage of the NSA story.

This first edition of the awards, which were presented on 17 October during 
the 7th Tablet and App Summit taking place at World Publishing Expo in 
Amsterdam, was open to winners of WAN-IFRA’s European Digital Media Awards and 
Asian Digital Media Awards.
