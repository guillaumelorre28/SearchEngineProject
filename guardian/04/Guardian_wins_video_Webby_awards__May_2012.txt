
The Guardian has won three awards in two categories at this year's Webby Awards
 <https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>.

In the Online Film & Video category, 'I will never be cut 
<https://www.theguardian.com/global-development/video/2011/apr/18/female-genital-mutilation-video>
' won both the People's Voice and Webby award in the Documentary: Individual 
Episode section, whilst 'Egypt, football and revolution 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/video/2011/jul/07/egypt-football-revolution-video>
' won the Webby in the Sports section.

A full list of winners can be found here
