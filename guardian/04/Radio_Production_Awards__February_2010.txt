
Francesca Panetta, senior audio producer with the GNM multimedia team, has won 
two awards at The Radio Academy'sRadio Production Awards 2010 
<http://www.radioacademy.org/events/radio-production-awards/rpas-winners/>.

The only nominee to win two awards, Francesca picked up Best Online Producer 
and also the Creative Award, for which she was up against some of the most 
experienced producers in broadcasting.

Producer of Politics Weekly and the Hay Festival Podcast, Francesca has been 
involved in many special projects including our Webby-nominatedBerlin Wall 
guides 
<https://www.theguardian.com/travel/interactive/2009/oct/23/berlin-wall-history-guide>
. In her spare time, she produces theHackney Podcast 
<http://hackneypodcast.co.uk/>, which formed part of her entry tape for the 
creative award.

For more information about the awards, click here 
<http://www.radioacademy.org/events/radio-production-awards/>.
