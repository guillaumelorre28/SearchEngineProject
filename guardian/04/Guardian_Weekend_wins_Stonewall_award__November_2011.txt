
Guardian Weekend has won Publication of the Year at Stonewall's sixth annual 
awards <http://www.stonewall.org.uk/what_you_can_do/events/2595.asp>.

The event celebrates the contribution of individuals and organisations to the 
lives of lesbian, gay and bisexual people in Britain today. Hosted by Stephen K 
Amos, the ceremony took place on Thursday 3 November.
