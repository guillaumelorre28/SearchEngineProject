
The Guardian’s books blog <https://www.theguardian.com/books/booksblog> has 
been awarded best traditional print media blog at this year’s Vuelio Blog 
Awards.

The Vuelio Blog Awards <https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards> 
recognise the success and reward the creativity of the UK’s most influential 
bloggers and vloggers.
