
Andrew Brown <https://www.theguardian.com/profile/andrewbrown>, editor of the 
Guardian's Comment is Free belief blog, has taken the book prize in this year's 
Orwell Journalism awards. He won the award for his book Fishing in Utopia, a 
personal memoir that examines the personal and social system in Sweden. Andrew 
beat 197 other authors to take the prize in what was a record year for 
submissions. Read more onMedia Guardian 
<https://www.theguardian.com/media/2009/apr/23/patrick-cockburn-orwell-prize>. 

The Orwell Prize <http://www.theorwellprize.co.uk/home.aspx> is one of the 
most prestigious awards for political writing.
