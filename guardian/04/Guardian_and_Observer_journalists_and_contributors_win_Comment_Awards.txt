
Four Guardian and Observer journalists and contributors have been honoured at 
The Editorial Intelligence CommentAwards 2015 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards-2015>. Now in its seventh 
year, the Awards celebrate the achievements of the UK’s finest and most 
influential print and online commentators and editors.

Foreign Commentator of the Year - Gary Younge, the Guardian

Technology and Digital Commentator of the Year - Cory Doctorow, the Guardian

Young Commentariat of the Year - Bintu Sannoh, The Observer

Chair’s Choice - Andrew Rawnsley, The Observer

A full list of winners can be viewed here 
<http://www.commentawards.com/winners.php>.
