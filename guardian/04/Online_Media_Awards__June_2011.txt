
Guardian.co.uk has won two awards for its online and data journalism at the 
inaugural Online MediaAwards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>.

The site won the Best news-led journalism award and Guardian.co.uk/data won 
the Technical innovation award for the Guardian Datastore and Datablog, which 
publishes and analyses the data behind the news.

The Guardian's online WikiLeaks coverage also received a commendation in the 
Investigation category, and its photography was commended in the Best use of 
photography category.

For more information visit www.onlinemediaawards.net 
<http://www.onlinemediaawards.net>
