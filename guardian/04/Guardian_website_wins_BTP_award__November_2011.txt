
Guardian.co.uk/travel <https://www.theguardian.com/uk/travel> has won Travel 
Website of the Year at theBritish Travel Press Awards 
<https://www.kingley.co.uk/the-2011-british-travel-press-awards-winners>.

The ceremony, which took place on Monday 28 November at the Royal Institute of 
British Architects, recognised excellence and achievement in travel writing, 
photography and broadcasting.

The judging panel described the Guardian site as "a hugely well thought out, 
helpful resource that allows for the reader to genuinely research their travel 
inspirations."

For more information on the awards visit the BTPA website 
<https://www.kingley.co.uk/the-2011-british-travel-press-awards>.
