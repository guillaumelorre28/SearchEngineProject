
Guardian cartoonist Kipper Williams 
<https://www.theguardian.com/business/series/kipperwilliams> has won The 
Cartoon Trust Award for Joke Cartooning.

Announced at a ceremony in central London on 17 October, Kipper was honoured 
for his work over the past year on the Guardian's financial pages.

Run by the Cartoon Art Trust, the awards are recognised as a prestigious 
honour in the industry. Read morehere 
<http://procartoonists.org/tag/cartoon-art-trust/>.
