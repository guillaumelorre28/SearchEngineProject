
Guardian News & Media won 'Commercial Interiors Project of the Year' for the 
Kings Place building at the MixologyAwards 2009 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards-2009>. The competition was 
very high with 9 interiors projects shortlisted including Taylor Wessing, 
Standard Chartered Bank and Unilever. The Mixology Awards are judged by an 
independent panel of architects, specifiers and consultants and focus on the 
office interiors market.
