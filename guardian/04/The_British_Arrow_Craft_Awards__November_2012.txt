
The Guardian's 'Three Little Pigs' 
<https://www.theguardian.com/media/video/2012/feb/29/open-journalism-three-little-pigs-advert>
 TV campaign, created by Bartle Bogle Hegarty (BBH) London, has won several 
prizes at The British Arrow Craft Awards.

The TV advert won the overall winner award for the 'Best Crafted Commercial' 
and also silver awards in the editing, production design, sound design and 
direction categories.

The awards recognise the best of British advertising. For more information and
