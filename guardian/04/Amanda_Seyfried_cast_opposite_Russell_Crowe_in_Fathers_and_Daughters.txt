
Amanda Seyfried will star opposite Russell Crowe in a new relationship drama 
from The Pursuit of Happyness director Gabriele Muccino,according to the 
Hollywood Reporter 
<http://m.hollywoodreporter.com/news/amanda-seyfried-star-opposite-russell-649234>
.

Fathers and Daughters sees the Mamma Mia! actor as a young woman pondering the 
travails of her current relationship as she also recalls the time she spent 
with her father, a famous author, 25 years earlier. Crowe secured a deal to 
play the novelist earlier this week.

Seyfried, 27, recently starred in Lovelace 
<https://www.theguardian.com/film/movie/156631/lovelace>, a biopic of the 
troubled porn star Linda Lovelace, and as Cosette in the Oscar-winning musical
Les Misérables <https://www.theguardian.com/film/movie/150107/miserables>. She 
is due to take a lead role inSeth MacFarlane 
<https://www.theguardian.com/culture/seth-macfarlane>'s spoof western A Million 
Ways to Die in the West, and will feature in the post-apocalyptic literary 
adaptation Z for Zachariah in 2015.
