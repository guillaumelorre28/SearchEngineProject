
Morrissey, meet Morrissey. The audiobook of Autobiography is to be read by the 
actorDavid Morrissey <https://www.theguardian.com/stage/david-morrissey>, the 
fansite True to You – usually regarded as speaking with the imprimatur of Moz – 
revealed on Sunday evening.

In a to-the-point statement, True to You announced: "Penguin Classics will 
release a complete and unabridged digital download of Morrissey's Autobiography 
on 5 December 2013. The audiobook is read by acclaimed actor DavidMorrissey 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/morrissey>."

Morrissey (the singer) and Morrissey (the actor) are not believed to be 
related. Morrissey (the actor) is best known for his work on a number of 
high-profile TV series, including State of Play, Red Riding and The Walking 
Dead.

 The print edition of Autobiography 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/2013/oct/17/morrissey-autobiography-triumph-mired-moaning-review>
 went straight to No 1 on its publication, becoming one of the fast-sellest 
memoirs ever.It shifted 34,918 copies in its first week 
<https://www.theguardian.com/books/2013/oct/23/morrissey-autobiography-first-week-sales-record>
 – more than 6,000 more than the record for a musician's memoirs set byKeith 
Richards's Life 
<https://www.theguardian.com/books/2010/oct/31/keith-richards-life-rolling-stones>
 in 2010.
