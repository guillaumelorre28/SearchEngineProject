
The Guardian has been honoured with twenty four awards at this year’s Society 
for News Design Awards <http://www.snd.org/competitions/>(SND).

The SND is an international organisation for news media professionals and 
visual communications who create print, web, mobile publications and products.

The full list of wins are as follows:

 * London skyline interactive 
<https://www.theguardian.com/artanddesign/2015/dec/11/city-of-london-skyline-of-tomorrow-interactive>
 won an Award of Excellence in the Features: Single-subject project category

<https://www.theguardian.com/politics/ng-interactive/2015/may/07/live-uk-election-results-in-full>

category

<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/ng-interactive/2015/oct/19/homan-square-chicago-police-detainees>
