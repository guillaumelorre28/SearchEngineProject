
Guardian Weekend's Editor Merope Mills has won the Editor of the Year award in 
the Newspaper & Free Circulation Consumer Brand category at theBritish Society 
of Magazine Editors (BSME) Awards 2013. <http://www.bsmeawards.com/index.php>

Merope said: "I'm delighted to have won this award, though this is definitely 
a team effort and this award is for everyone at Weekend Magazine."

The BSME is the only society in the UK exclusively for magazine and website 
editors. Their aim is to represent the needs and views of all editors, acting 
as a voice for the industry.
