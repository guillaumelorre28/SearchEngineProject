
The Guardian has won a second prize award at the World Press Photo Awards 2014 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards-2014>.

The award was given in the Interactive Documentary category for NSA Files: 
Decoded, 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/interactive/2013/nov/01/snowden-nsa-files-surveillance-revelations-decoded>
 an innovative multimedia feature which was developed by the Guardian US team 
and published in November 2013.

Find out more here 
<http://www.worldpressphoto.org/2014-multimedia-contest/jury-interviews>.
