
CHRISTMAS

In swanky Val d’Isere, France, Christmas means live music, parades, carol 
concerts and festive menus, plus a visit from Father Christmas. Prices at 
Chalet La Vieille Maison, a three-star catered chalet, have been reduced by 
£400pp. So a stay from 19-26 December is now £645pp with chalet board 
(breakfast, afternoon tea and dinner) based on 12 sharing, including flights 
from Gatwick, withigluski.com 
<http://www.igluski.com/val-d-isere/chalet-la-vieille-maison_p26329?holidayid=1437941614&oid=1421139110>
. There’s also free ski guiding or coaching for all guests, and lift pass and 
ski hire for one. The chalet is a converted farmhouse, with beams and exposed 
stone.


There’s 15% off a week’s self-catering from 19-26 December, at Résidence Cami 
Real inSaint-Lary Soulan (ernalow.co.uk 
<http://www.ernalow.co.uk/france/french-pyrenees/saint-lary-soulan/residence-cami-real>
) in the French Pyrenees. It now costs £118pp based on six sharing the 
apartment. Take the train via Paris to Lannemezan (from £167pp, an hour from 
St-Lary by coach) or fly to Toulouse with easyJet (from £245pp). The apartments 
are in the centre of the village, with use of an indoor pool. A new gondola 
takes skiers to the slopes in eight minutes.

Spot skiing santas and take in Christmas mass at the church in La Plagne, 
France, part of the vast Paradiski area. There is £150 off a week in Chalet La 
Forêt, a three-star catered chalet in La Plagne 1800, £674pp chalet board based 
on two sharing from 19-26 December, including return flights from Gatwick with
igluski.com 
<http://www.igluski.com/la-plagne-1800/chalet-la-foret_p32468?holidayid=1439695856&oid=1420661994>
. Lift passes have been reduced to £1, saving a further £178.

With good après, a skiable glacier and links to gnarly La Grave and pretty 
Venosc, Les Deux Alpes, in France, isn’t a bad bet. Chalet Les Rouses, a 
four-star catered chalet, has £204pp off, so a week costs £735pp chalet board, 
based on two sharing from 19-26 December, including flights from Gatwick, with
skisolutions.com 
<http://www.skisolutions.com/countries/france/resorts/les-deux-alpes/accommodations/les-rouses>
.

Underrated Swiss powder heaven Engelberg has a new gondola, the Titlis 
Express, that speeds access to the 2,428-metre Stand area. Stay at Hotel 
Terrasse from 19-26 December for £957pp half-board, based on two sharing, 
including lift passes and flights from Luton withcrystalski.co.uk 
<http://www.crystalski.co.uk/ski-resorts/switzerland-ski-holidays/engelberg/>.

Action Outdoors 
<https://www.action-outdoors.co.uk/holiday/cart?ActivityID=12092> has a bargain 
week inChamonix with UCPA, a non-profit French organisation that runs outdoor 
sports holidays for under-40s. It costs £405pp full board for 19-26 December, 
including ski pass, ski hire and evening entertainment, but another £62pp for a 
twin room rather than a four- to six-person dorm, and another £165 for return 
coach travel from London.

Beautiful scenery and great food combine in Monterosa, Italy. Chalet Hotel de 
Champoluc has £300pp off a week’s stay, so it’s £649pp chalet board for 19-26 
December, including flights from Gatwick, withinghams.co.uk 
<https://www.inghams.co.uk/ski-holidays/ski-resorts/italy/champoluc/chalet-hotel-de-champoluc>
. Inghams also has a two-for-one lift pass offer over Christmas.

With great nightlife and snow parks, Mayrhofen in Austria is a fun resort. 
Chalet Tirol has £150pp off a week’s stay, taking it to £658pp chalet board for 
19-26 December, including flights from Gatwick,inghams.co.uk 
<https://www.inghams.co.uk/ski-holidays/ski-resorts/austria/mayrhofen/chalet-tirol>
.

Named after the lotion you might rub on your bruises, Chalet Arnica in 
family-favouriteLa Rosière, France, has a special family offer (two adults and 
two children aged two to 11) of £2,479 for chalet board for 20-27 December, 
including flights from Gatwick and transfers,espritski.com 
<http://www.espritski.com/ski-chalets/arnica/>.

You can’t beat Austria’s St Anton for off-piste plus lairy après. Chalet Elfie 
has £230pp off a week, so it’s £658pp chalet board based on two sharing for 
20-27 December, including flights from Gatwick,skitotal.com 
<http://www.skitotal.com/chalets/chalet-elfie/>.

Street party, clubbing or full-throttle après for your Austrian New Year? 
Three-star Hotel Elizabeth Rose inMayrhofen, Austria, has £200 off a week’s 
stay. The £849pp deal with igluski.com 
<http://www.igluski.com/mayrhofen/hotel-elizabeth-rose_p36045?holidayid=1440881858&oid=1421247151>
 for 26 December to 2 January includes dinner and flights from Gatwick.

For a more chilled celebration, Les Hauts de Peyragudes in Peyragudes in the 
French Pyrenees, offers great value, from £98pp self-catering from 26 
December-2 January, based on six sharing withernalow.co.uk 
<http://www.ernalow.co.uk/france/french-pyrenees/peyragudes/les-hauts-de-peyragudes>
. Eating out here is much cheaper than in the Alps, and the resort has a Vallée 
Blanche off-piste itinerary and is rarely crowded, even at New Year. Fly to 
Toulouse or take the train, as before.

<https://www.action-outdoors.co.uk/holiday/cart?ActivityID=11995> has a

London is another £150. Bars and clubs in Chamonix will go wild on New Year’s 
Eve.
