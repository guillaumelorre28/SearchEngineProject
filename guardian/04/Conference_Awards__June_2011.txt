
The Guardian Activate Summit held in London in 2010 has been named Best 
European Conference at theConference Awards 2011 
<http://www.conferenceawards.co.uk/winners.html>. The awards recognise and 
reward excellence within the conference and events industry.

Activate is the Guardian's platform for leaders working across all sectors who 
are proving that, through the use of technology and the internet, we can make 
the world a better place.

The 2011 Activate Summit takes place tomorrow, Wednesday 22 June, at Kings 
Place. For more information about Activate visitwww.theguardian.com/activate 
<https://www.theguardian.com/activate>.
