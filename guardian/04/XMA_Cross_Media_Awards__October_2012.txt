
The Guardian Facebook app has won at the 2012 XMA Cross Media Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>, receiving first prize in 
the 'best use of Facebook by a newspaper' category.

The judging panel were looking for news publishers who have excelled in 
delivering content, entertainment and advertising over social media networks 
and who have integrated their readers in an engaging way. The judges described 
the app as "a brilliant innovation."

The XMA Cross Media Awards are organised by WAN-IFRA <http://www.wan-ifra.org/>
 - the World Association of Newspapers and News Publishers - and aims to honour 
news publishers that have delivered innovative multimedia products and services 
to their audiences.
