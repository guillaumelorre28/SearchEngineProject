
The Guardian has won two prizes at the Amnesty International UK Media Awards 
<http://www.amnesty.org.uk/content.asp?CategoryID=10058> 2011. 

Jack Shenker won the Gaby Rado memorial award for his reports from Egypt 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/2011/jan/27/egypt-riot-security-force-action>
 during the uprisings there.

Guardian Weekend Magazine was named joint winner of the Magazines - Newspaper 
Supplements category for Alice Vasey's piece 'Handle With Care'.

The Amnesty International UK Media Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards> recognise excellence in 
journalism that makes a significant contribution to the UK public's 
understanding of human rights.
