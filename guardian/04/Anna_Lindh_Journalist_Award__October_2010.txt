
Sarfraz Manzoor has triumphed in the press category at the prestigious Anna 
Lindh Journalist Awards <http://www.euromedalex.org/news/winners-announced> at 
a ceremony in Monaco.

The prize was in recognition of his piece 'My Month of Being Jewish' 
<https://www.theguardian.com/film/2010/apr/08/month-jewish-david-baddiel-infidel>
 which appeared in the Guardian in April.

The Anna Lindh Journalist Award is the Mediterranean region's leading prize 
for reporting across cultures and on issues of cultural diversity.
