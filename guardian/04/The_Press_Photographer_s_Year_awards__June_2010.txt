
The Guardian has won three prizes at the Press Photographer's Year 
<http://www.theppy.com/content/results2010> awards, more than any other 
newspaper.

David Levene won first place in the Live News category, Sean Smith came top in 
Sports Features and Felix Clay was awarded best Digital Film forBarton's 
Britain: The Bridgewater Canal <http://vimeo.com/11015100>. 

The Press Photographer's Year awards is the only competition that recognises 
outstanding press photography taken for the UK news media.
