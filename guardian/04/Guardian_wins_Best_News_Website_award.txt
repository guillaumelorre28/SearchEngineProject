
The Guardian website has been named ‘Best News Website’ at the European 
Digital Media Awards 
<http://www.wan-ifra.org/articles/2014/04/04/winners-of-the-european-digital-media-awards-2014>
, hosted by WAN-IFRA, for its groundbreaking coverage of the revelations on 
government by whistleblower Edward Snowden.

The awards were presented during the Digital Media Europe Conference, which 
celebrates ground-breaking digital products that engage readers while 
increasing their online business, on Tuesday 14 October.
