
Party Tricks actor Asher Keddie 
<https://www.theguardian.com/culture/asher-keddie> has confirmed she is 
pregnant, following an appearance at the GQ Men of the Year awards in Sydney.

Keddie appeared on the red carpet with husband Vincent Fantauzzo and wore a 
form-fitting black dress that showed her visible “baby bump”. She told
news.com.au 
<http://www.news.com.au/entertainment/awards/asher-keddie-reveals-pregnancy-at-gq-men-of-the-year-awards-2014/story-fnil5php-1227128800761>
: “We’re thrilled. We’re really excited and can’t wait ... I just feel good 
now.”

The Gold Logie winner is best known for her role on the popular Australian 
drama Offspring, and in 2013 spent a significant period of filming wearing a 
prosthetic belly. “I can’t take this one off at the end of the day, but I don’t 
want to.”

Fantauzzo and Keddie married in Fiji earlier this year in a beach ceremony.

This will be the 40-year-old actor’s first child, and the second for 
Fantauzzo, who has a four-year-old son called Luca. The artist’s portrait of 
his son recently won the 2014 Archibald people’s choice award. His portrait of 
Keddie also won the people’s choice the previous year.

Fantauzzo picked up artist of the year award at the ceremony. Other winners 
included Hollywood actor Dave Franco for international man of the year and 
swimmer Ian Thorpe for man of influence.
