
Guardian News & Media has won the prestigious 'Special Award' at the 
Anti-Slavery Day Media Awards, for its seriesModern Day Slavery in Focus.
 
<https://www.theguardian.com/global-development/series/modern-day-slavery-in-focus>

The series was chosen as 'the most innovative media initiative' by a select 
panel of judges.

The Observer's Gethin Chamberlain 
<https://www.theguardian.com/profile/gethin-chamberlain> was also among the 
winners.

The Anti-Slavery Day Media Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>, in association with The 
Human Trafficking Foundation, were set up to reveal the injustice of modern day 
slavery in the UK. They recognise the media's contribution to raising public 
awareness.

Click here <http://www.antislaveryday.com/awards/> for more information.
