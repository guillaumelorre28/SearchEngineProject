
The Guardian has been awarded four awards at this year’s British Media Awards 
<http://britishmediaawards.com/>. The British Media Awards highlight the best 
in innovation and excellence in the media industry.

The Guardian’s Professional Networks picked up gold for content team of the 
year. Other wins included a bronze awards for product team of the year and app 
of the year.


Former editor-in-chief Alan Rusbridger was recognised with an outstanding 
contribution to British Media award.


Further information can be found on the British Media Awards site 
<http://britishmediaawards.com/2016-winners-2/>.
