
The Guardian's investigations executive editor David Leigh has won a Daniel 
Pearl Award for his story exposing oil firm Trafigura's dumping of toxic waste 
in the Ivory Coast.

The prize was shared jointly with BBC Newsnight, the Norwegian Broadcasting 
Corporation and De Volkskrant newspaper.

The Daniel Pearl Awards for Outstanding International Investigative Reporting 
are presented by the International Consortium of Investigative Journalists, a 
project of theCenter for Public Integrity <http://www.publicintegrity.org/> in 
Washington, D.C.
