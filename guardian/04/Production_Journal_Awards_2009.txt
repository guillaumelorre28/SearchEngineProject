
GNM picked up two awards and five commendations at the Production Journal 
awards 2009. The Observer was a joint winner of the newspaper design of the 
year category alongside The Sunday Times while the Guardian and Observer 
'subscriber' initiative took the Grand Prix category for the most significant 
contribution to newspaper success.There were also commendations for: 
Guardian.co.uk - Electronic news site; The Guardian and Observer - Most 
outstanding use of colour; GPC Manchester - Environmental newspaper of the 
year; and GPC - Newspaper printer of the year.
