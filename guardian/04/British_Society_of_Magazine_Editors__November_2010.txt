
Guardian Weekend magazine editor Merope Mills has won the best Newspaper & 
Current Affairs Magazines category at theBritish Society of Magazine Editors 
(BSME) <http://www.bsme.com/awards/5/bsme-awards/49/2010-bsme-awards-winners/> 
awards for the second year in succession.

The awards are for newspaper and magazine editors and judged by a panel of 
industry experts.
