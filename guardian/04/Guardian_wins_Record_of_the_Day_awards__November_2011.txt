
The Guardian has won two major awards at this year's Record of the Day Awards 
for Music Journalism 
<http://www.recordoftheday.com/news-and-press/article.php?contentID=1729>.

At the event, held in London on 23 November, Alexis Petridis was named record 
reviews writer of the year for the eighth successive year and Film & Music won 
most effective music coverage in a national newspaper for the sixth year 
running.

Record of the Day <http://www.recordoftheday.com/> is a daily news digest for 
the music industry.
