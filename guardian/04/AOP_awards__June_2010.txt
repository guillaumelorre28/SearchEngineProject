
Guardian.co.uk has swept the board at the prestigious Association of Online 
Publishers <http://www.ukaop.org.uk/> awards 2010, including the top Gold award 
for digital consumer publisher.

Matthew Weaver <https://www.theguardian.com/profile/matthewweaver> won digital 
editorial individual while the Guardian's iPhone app, which launched in 
December 2009, andmobile website <http://m.theguardian.com/> triumphed in the 
innovation category.

The Guardian Open Platform <https://www.theguardian.com/open-platform> - a 
suite of online services that enables users to build applications with the 
Guardian - was recognised for launch of the year.

Guardian.co.uk won the use of video category, and GNM Word of Mouth Research, 
a study and communications planning tool that provides a practical framework 
for identifying influential people, won the Research & Insights Project award.
