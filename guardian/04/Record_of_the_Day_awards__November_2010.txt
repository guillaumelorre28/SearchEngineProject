
The Guardian has won two major awards at the prestigious Record of the Day 
awards for music journalism.

Alexis Petridis was named record reviews writer of the year for the seventh 
successive year; judges said he was "head and shoulders" above the competition. 
Film & Music won best music coverage in a national newspaper for the fifth year 
running and was praised for its "endless inventiveness".

Record of the Day is a news digest for the music industry.
