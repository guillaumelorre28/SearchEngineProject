
The Guardian has won three awards in the Webby Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>, the online world's most 
prestigious celebration of digital excellence.

In the Online Film & Video: News & Politics: Series, the Guardian won the 
Webby forQatar: the migrant workers forced to work for no pay in World Cup host 
country 
<https://www.theguardian.com/global-development/video/2013/sep/25/qatar-migrant-workers-world-cup-host-video>
.

The Web: Best Practices Webby went to NSA Files - Decoded 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/interactive/2013/nov/01/snowden-nsa-files-surveillance-revelations-decoded>
, an interactive created by the Guardian's US team to analyse and expand on the 
Guardian's groundbreaking coverage of the Snowden revelations.

Columbite Tantalite 
<https://www.theguardian.com/stage/video/2013/dec/11/columbite-tantalite-short-film-chiwetel-ejiofor-video>
, a joint project between the Guardian, the Young Vic theatre and directed by 
actor Chiwetel Ejiofor, also picked up the Webby for Online Film & Video: 
Drama: Individual Short or Episode.
