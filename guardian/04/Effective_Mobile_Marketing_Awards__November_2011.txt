
The Guardian's mobile site, m.guardian <http://m.theguardian.com/>, has won the
Most Effective Mobile Site award at this year's EMMAs.

The Effective Mobile Marketing Awards 
<http://mobilemarketingmagazine.com/awards/> (EMMAs) are organised by Mobile 
Marketing magazine 
<http://www.mobilemarketingmagazine.com/content/effective-mobile-marketing-award-winners-revealed>
 and recognise worldwide talent in the mobile marketing industry. The ceremony 
was attended by the Guardian's Steve Wing and Lee Fels who collected the award, 
the first form.guardian <http://m.theguardian.com/>.

For more information please visit the EMMA site 
<http://mobilemarketingmagazine.com/awards/awards/2011-winners/>.
