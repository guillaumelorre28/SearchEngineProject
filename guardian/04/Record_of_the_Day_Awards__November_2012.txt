
The Guardian has won four awards at this year's Record of the Day Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards> for Music Journalism.

The Guide won an award for 'Most Effective Music Coverage in a Newspaper', 
while Alexis Petridis was named both 'Best Music Writer' (as voted for by 
students) and 'Record Reviews: Writer of the Year'.

Observer journalist Miranda Sawyer also won the 'Outstanding Contribution to 
Music Journalism' prize.

Now in their eighth year, the awards celebrate future music media talent as 
well as established journalists and editors.

Record of the Day <http://www.recordoftheday.com/> is the music industry's 
daily digest providing a raft of original and aggregated news from all UK and 
international media outlets, encompassing press and online.
