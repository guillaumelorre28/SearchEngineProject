
The Guardian has been awarded best use of online video for ‘Building the Atom 
Bomb’ 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/ng-interactive/2015/sep/21/building-the-atom-bomb-the-full-story-of-the-nevada-test-site>
 (the story of the US’s development of nuclear weapons created in collaboration 
with ITV) at this year’s World Digital Media Awards.

The World Digital Media Awards 
<http://www.wan-ifra.org/microsites/world-digital-media-awards> recognise 
publishers that have created ground-breaking digital products that engage 
readers while increasing online business. The awards are part of WAN-IFRA’s 
wide variety ofawards programs <http://www.wan-ifra.org/microsites/awards>, 
designed to publicise and share the best publishing cases from across the 
global industry.


The full list of winners can be found here
