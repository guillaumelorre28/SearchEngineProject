
The Guardian has won four Webby awards, the leading international awards 
honouring excellence on the internet, including two People’s Voice awards voted 
for by the public.


The Guardian’s Android and iOS apps were awarded best mobile site and apps  
<http://webbyawards.com/winners/2016/mobile-sites-apps/general-sites-apps/news/>
 in the news category andtheguardian.com 
<https://www.theguardian.com/international> won the People’s Voice award for 
websites 
<http://webbyawards.com/winners/2016/websites/website-features-and-design/best-user-experience/>
 in the Best User Experience category.

Mekong: a river rising 
<https://www.theguardian.com/environment/ng-interactive/2015/nov/26/the-mekong-river-stories-from-the-heart-of-the-climate-crisis-interactive>
 – an interactive focusing on how the 70 million people who live along 
South-East Asia’s Mekong river are struggling to deal with the impacts of 
climate change – was awarded thePeople’s Voice award for websites 
<http://webbyawards.com/winners/2016/websites/website-features-and-design/best-use-of-video-or-moving-image/>
 in the best use of video or moving image category.

We walk together: a Syrian family’s journey to the heart of Europe 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/video/2015/sep/10/we-walk-together-a-syrian-familys-journey-to-the-heart-of-europe-video>
 was the winner in theonline film and video: news and politics individual 
episode 
<http://webbyawards.com/winners/2016/online-film-video/general-film/news-politics-individual-episode/>
 category.

The Guardian was also recognised as an honouree in the following categories:


Advertising and Media: Best Branded Editorial Experience - The Guardian Media 
& Tech Professional Network -The internet of nearly everything

Online Film and Video: Drama: Long Form or Series - Off the Page: Microplays 
from the Royal Court and the Guardian 
<https://www.theguardian.com/stage/series/off-the-page-microplays-royal-court-guardian>

Mobile Sites and Apps: Best Podcast - The Biggest Story in the World 
<https://www.theguardian.com/environment/ng-interactive/2015/mar/16/the-biggest-story-in-the-world>

Websites: Best Visual Design Aesthetics - The Guardian 
<https://www.theguardian.com/uk>

Websites: Best Individual Editorial Experience - The County: a five part 
series on America’s deadliest police 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2015/dec/01/the-county-kern-county-deadliest-police-killings>
