
The Guardian’s first virtual reality project, 6x9: a virtual experience of 
solitary confinement 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/ng-interactive/2016/apr/27/6x9-a-virtual-experience-of-solitary-confinement>
, has been honoured with the top prize in the Virtual Reality, Documentary 
Short category at CINE’s 59th Golden Eagle Awards.

CINE is a non-profit organisation that champions creators of media content. 
CINE’s Golden EagleAwards <https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards> 
celebrate the productions of hundreds of filmmakers at every level of their 
careers.


Further information about the awards can be found here <https://cine.org/#>.
