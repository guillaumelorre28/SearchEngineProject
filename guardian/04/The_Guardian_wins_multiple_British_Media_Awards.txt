
The Guardian has picked up multiple awards at this year’s British Media Awards 
<http://britishmediaawards.com/> - a ceremony dedicated to highlighting 
innovation and excellence across the media industry.


The wins included App of the Year, Product Team of the Year, Website of the 
Year and Editorial Campaign of the Year for its campaign to end female genital 
mutilation.

The Guardian’s sales team also picked a silver award for Sales Team of the 
Year.

Judges commented that the Guardian’s website impressed them with “an 
exceptional content and product proposition that clearly understands its 
audience”.

The full list of winners can be found here 
<http://britishmediaawards.com/2015-winners/>.
