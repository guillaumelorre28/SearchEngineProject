
GNM's Customer Insight team has picked up a prize at the inaugural Media 
Research Group <http://www.mrg.org.uk/> (MRG) awards. The best research 
initiative award was given for the team's Word of Mouth project.

New for 2010, the MRG Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards> aim to recognise some of 
the best work in media research over the past 24 months.
