
Guardian journalist Amelia Gentleman has won the Orwell Journalism Award at a 
prestigious ceremony in London last night.

The Orwell Prize, which was established to recognise George Orwell's ambition 
to 'to make political writing into an art,' is Britain's most prestigious prize 
for political writing and takes place annually.

For more information on the awards and to see a full list of winners, please 
clickhere <http://theorwellprize.co.uk/the-orwell-prize/>.
