
Guardian News & Media has been recognised at the 2010 Association of 
Circulation Excellence (ACE) awards 
<http://www.acecirculation.com/content/ace-press-award-winners-2010> picking up 
three awards including: Gold - Sustainability Award; Silver for Promotion of 
the Year (Subscriber); Bronze - Circulation Excellence.

When awarding the Sustainability award, judges said "When It is clear that 
sustainability issues play a key part in GNM's thinking and planning. The entry 
demonstrates not only company buy-in but also local community involvement and 
plenty of relevant applications to the publishing industry in general."

The awards recognise the effort and success achieved by circulation and 
distribution staff in all aspects of the newspaper and magazine industry.
