
The Guardian's 'Three Little Pigs' 
<https://www.theguardian.com/media/video/2012/feb/29/open-journalism-three-little-pigs-advert>
 TV campaign, created by Bartle Bogle Hegarty (BBH) London, has been named 
number one in the Business Insider's 10 Best Ads of 2012 list.

The list celebrates stand out campaigns which are original and have great 
entertainment value. The Guardian's advert was described as being "the most 
original idea of the year."
