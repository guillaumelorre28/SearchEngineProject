
Yasir Mirza, head of diversity and inclusion at the Guardian, has won at the 
Brit Writers'Awards 2012 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards-2012>, receiving the top 
prize in the 'International Innovation in Publishing' category for his work 
surrounding the editorial diversity writers' workshops.

The awards were announced on Saturday 1 December in London and celebrates the 
publishing industry in opening the doors to aspiring writers.

For more information about the awards, click here <http://www.britwriters.com/>
