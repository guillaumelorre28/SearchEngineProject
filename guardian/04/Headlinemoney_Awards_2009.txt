
Guardian Money was judged the best personal finance section in any national 
newspaper as it picked up a series of accolades at the coveted Headlinemoney
Awards <https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards> in May 2009. The 
Guardian and Observer's head of consumer, Jill Insley, picked up the consumer 
journalist of the year award. Money's consumer champion Tony Levene also won 
praise.
