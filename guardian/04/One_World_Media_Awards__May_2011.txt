
The Guardian has won two awards at the One World Media Awards 
<http://oneworldmedia.org.uk/awards/> 2011.

The Guardian's global development website 
<https://www.theguardian.com/global-development> came first in the Millennium 
Development Goals category and Jonathan Steele won the Press Award for hisG2 
article 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/2010/nov/19/afghanistan-aid-programme-taliban>
 on the effectiveness of the UK's aid and development teams in Helmand province.

The One World Media Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>, now in their 23rd year, 
reward the most outstanding media coverage of the developing world.
