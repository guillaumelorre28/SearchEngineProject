
The Guardian and The Observer claimed five prizes at the British Press Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>. Guardian.co.uk was named 
website of the year for the second year in succession. Other awards went to: 
Philip French, Critic of the Year, Charlie Brooker, Columnist of the Year; 
Decca Aitkenhead, Interviewer of the Year; Dave Hill, Digital Journalist of the 
Year, for his London blog on theguardian.com.
