
Jasná, Slovakia


Slovakia’s largest ski area is a good place to learn, with low prices, and 
gentle slopes by the hotels. The mountains, despite being called the Low 
Tatras, rise to over 2,000 metres and have a decent snow record.
Pistes 45.7km Lifts 26
Bonus: Beginner Package £175pp including six days’ equipment hire, five days’ 
ski school, five-day lift pass.
Book From £390pp for seven nights half-board at Hotel Fis Jasna including lift 
pass. Flights not included:sunshineworldslovakia.com 
<http://www.sunshineworldslovakia.com/low_tatra_packages.php>.
