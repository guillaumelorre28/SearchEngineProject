
Guardian News & Media Research and Customer Insight team have beaten Google 
and News International, amongst others, to win Gold in the Best Research 
Initiative category at the prestigious Media WeekAwards 2009 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards-2009>.

The award was for Engagement 
<http://www.adinfo-theguardian.com/display/research/engagement/engagement-intro.shtml>
, a research study and media planning tool that enables an evaluation of 
national newspapers according to levels of consumer media engagement. This is 
the third time in four years the team has won Gold in this category.

Guardian Commercial also had reason to celebrate, picking up Silver in the 
Sales Pitch Medium Category for Constellation Wines Robert Mondavi.
