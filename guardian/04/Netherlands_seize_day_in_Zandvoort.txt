
The Netherlands captured pole position for the season four opening race in 
Zandvoort, Holland, with a time of 1.24.213. New Zealand took second, with 
Ireland and Malaysia making up the second row. With the cars of several teams 
including those of Team GB, not yet ready,A1GP 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/a1gp> confirmed a change to their 
regulations to avoid excluding them for the entire season.

Teams competing at the Dutch track will score championship points for the 
2008-09 A1GP World Cup. However, all teams will now count their best nine event 
scores out of 10 in determining the championship. The final points standings 
will be calculated by dropping their worst result from the sprint and feature 
races of one event.
