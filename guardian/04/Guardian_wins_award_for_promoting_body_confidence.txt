
The Guardian has won the award for responsible media and advertising at the 
2014Body Confidence Awards 
<http://www.berealcampaign.co.uk/news-and-events/2014/10/17/congratulations-to-the-body-confidence-awards-winners/>
.

The awards were held by the APPG on Body Image at the end of the annual ‘Be 
Real: Body Confidence for Everyone’ - a new national movement for body 
confidence.

The Guardian was selected for its Body Image 
<https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/series/up-front+body-image> column, 
which brings the body image debate to the wider public, with itsfashion for all 
ages <https://www.theguardian.com/fashion/series/fashion-for-all-ages>series 
also recognised for showing a commitment to representing different people.
Judges commented that the Guardian has “been sparking debate in this area long
