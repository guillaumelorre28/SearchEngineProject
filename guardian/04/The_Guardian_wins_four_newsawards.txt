
The Guardian has been honoured with four newsawards 
<http://www.newsawards.co.uk/>, which celebrate the best in news media print, 
technology and business innovation.


The Guardian picked up awards for both News Website of the Year and News App 
of the Year (UK and International) as well as Best Use of User Generated 
Content forThe Counted 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/ng-interactive/2015/jun/01/the-counted-police-killings-us-database>
 and Most Innovative Use of Mobile Technology forThe Minute 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/series/the-campaign-minute-2016>. 

It also came highly commended in the Digital Innovation of the Year category 
for Guardian visuals and in the Best Use of Advertising Technology category for 
dynamic ad insertion. The Observer was also highly commended as Sunday 
Newspaper of the Year.

A full list of winners can be found here <http://www.newsawards.co.uk/winners/>
