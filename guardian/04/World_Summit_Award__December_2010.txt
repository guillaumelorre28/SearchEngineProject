
The Guardian's mobile site and iPhone app have been recognised at the World 
Summit Award <http://www.wsa-mobile.org/> for best mobile content. 

The Guardian was one of five winners within the m-Media and News category 
which included You Are The Reporter Russia and ABC Australia.

The World Summit Award (WSA) aims to select and promote the world's best 
e-content and most innovative ICT applications.
