
From landscape photography to atmospheric shots of a street market to wildlife 
and nature – we’d love you to send us your best travel shot. We’ll choose and 
publish a selection of our favourites, and the winning image will receive a 
£200 voucher which can be used against anExodus Travels 
<https://www.exodus.co.uk/> holiday.

The winner will be entered for the overall annual prize, a 16-night holiday 
for two to Costa Rica with Exodus Travels 
<https://www.exodus.co.uk/costa-rica-holidays/wildlife/discover-costa-rica/wuc-86197?flights=1>
.

Please read this before you post your image:

• You must be a UK resident to enter
• You must upload the highest possible resolution of your shot via 
GuardianWitness. 
• We’ll only consider one photograph from each person, so don’t submit more 
than one.
• We can’t consider photos that have been published elsewhere.
• You’ll also need to provide a caption of up to 50 words on where it was 
taken, what’s happening in the shot, what inspired you to take it, any 
technical details if pertinent.You must also supply your full name with the 
caption.
