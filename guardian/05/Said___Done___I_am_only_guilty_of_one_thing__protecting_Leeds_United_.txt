socks and belts 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/blog/2016/dec/05/lazio-roma-derby-serie-a>
 in Stuttgart”. Lulic: “White people sell socks too.”

• Assessing Lulic for a potential extended ban: Italy FA head Carlo Tavecchio, 
who launched his “Racists? A bad race” zero-tolerance campaign in 2015 - a year

<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2014/nov/05/fifa-italian-fa-president-carlo-tavecchio-banana-eaters>

think. Calmness and consistency – that’s the answer.”6 Dec – Sacks him
