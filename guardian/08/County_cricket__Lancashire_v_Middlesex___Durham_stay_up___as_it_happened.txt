Thank you and good night

One more week, then. Four more days!

Yorkshire head to Lord’s, nine behind Middlesex 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/middlesex>, but one ahead of Somerset, who 
host relegated Nottinghamshire.

Hampshire, eight points adrift, host newly-safe Durham. Warwickshire, second 
from bottom, welcome Lancs, third from bottom.

Just wonderful.

Anyway, that’s all from me, for this evening (train to London calls), and this 
season. Next week is Lord Selve’s sign-off at Lord’s. Wave the great man off 
fondly. Hopefully Vic and/or Paul Weaver will be getting in on the act too.

Cheerio! Thanks for all your comments, it’s what keeps this all going.
