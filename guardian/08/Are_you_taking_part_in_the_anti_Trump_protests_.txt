
Protests and rallies against the election of Donald Trump have broken out in 
several US cities 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/live/2016/nov/09/donald-trump-us-election-2016-live-reactio?CMP=share_btn_tw>
.

Protesters in New York City massed between Union Square and Trump Tower, while 
US citizens also tookto the streets in Chicago 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/live/2016/nov/09/donald-trump-us-election-2016-live-reactio?page=with:block-5823cb39e4b0199b9389550c#block-5823cb39e4b0199b9389550c>
, Philadelphia,and San Francisco 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/live/2016/nov/09/donald-trump-us-election-2016-live-reactio?page=with:block-5823ce73e4b0eda0192fe09f#block-5823ce73e4b0eda0192fe09f>
.

Chants heard at the NYC protest included ‘black lives matter’, ‘deport Donald 
Trump’, ‘fuck Donald Trump’, ‘no fascist USA’, ‘Trump, you’re fired’, ‘trans 
rights are human rights’, ‘women’s rights are human rights’, and ‘my body my 
choice’.
