
Kevin Pietersen <https://www.theguardian.com/sport/kevin-pietersen> was 
undergoing a scan on Saturday after injuring his right calf during Rising Pune 
Supergiants’ Indian Premier League defeat by Royal Challengers Bangalore.

The former England batsman, having come in at No3 and faced only one ball, 
retired after hurting his leg taking off for a single during Friday’s match. He 
called for assistance straight away and was carried from the field.

The 35-year-old posted a picture on Facebook 
<https://www.facebook.com/kevinpietersen/> soon after the incident of himself 
in the dressing room in a wheelchair, with a strapping on his calf, alongside 
the word “Urgh!”.

On Saturday morning he added: “Thanks for all the get well soon msgs, guys! On 
our way for a scan. Will update you all later!”
