
As Donald Trump is now talking of a fence in some places rather than a wall (
Report 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2016/nov/13/trump-deportation-immigration-criminals>
, 14 November), perhaps he might also consider a hedge. In 1869 a hedge 
stretched across the whole of India – a distance of 2,300 miles.The Great Hedge 
<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inland_Customs_Line> was a customs barrier put 
up by the British to levy a duty on salt – cutting off an affordable supply of 
an absolute necessity of life.
