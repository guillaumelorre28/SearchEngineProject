The image, featuring bats in New Zealand and England strip colours, is a nod 
to today’s men’s semi-final in Delhi 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/live/2016/mar/30/england-v-new-zealand-world-twenty20-semi-final-cricket-score-live>

1992.

The match starts at 2.30pm BST – and comes after England’s women have faced 
Australia in their semi-final. Australia have won the last three women’s 
tournaments, beating England in the past two finals.
