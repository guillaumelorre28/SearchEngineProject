
Wes Anderson <https://www.theguardian.com/film/wes-anderson>, director of 
Rushmore and The Royal Tenenbaums, has announced details of his long-awaited 
next project.

Entitled The Life Aquatic, it will reunite Anderson with Bill Murray 
<https://www.theguardian.com/film/bill-murray>, Owen Wilson and Angelica 
Huston, and will also star Jeff Goldblum, Bud Cort and Peter Stormare. Anderson 
also co-wrote the script with Noah Baumbach.

Described as a comedy/adventure, the film follows oceanographer Steve Zissou 
and his crew in their underwater explorations, including hunting a mythical 
shark.

Stop-motion animator Henry Selick, director of The Nightmare Before Christmas, 
will collaborate with Anderson to create the underwater scenes. Filming begins 
in Italy in the autumn.
