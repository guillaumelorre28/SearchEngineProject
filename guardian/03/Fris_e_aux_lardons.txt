
4 heads of frisée, washed, trimmed
 455g large cut country bacon (about 1/2 inch cubes)
 dressing
 2-3 chicken livers
 115 ml Balsamic vinegar
 340g blended oil
 2 shallots, minced
 salt and pepper
 croutons
 4 slices of baguette, toasted
 Roquefort or chèvre

For the dressing: Sauté chicken livers and purée liver with oil, vinegar, 
shallots, salt and pepper.

For the salad: Sauté lardons in pan. Transfer with bacon fat into mixing bowl. 
Toss with frisée with dressing. Spread cheese on croutons. Serve salad in 
individual plates with a crouton.
