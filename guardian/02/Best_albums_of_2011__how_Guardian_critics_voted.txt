
Here's the full list of votes from our critics. We asked them for their top 10 
albums and tracks of 2011. Then we tallied up their votes, awarding 10 points 
for first place, nine points for second place and so on, ending with a solitary 
point for any albums in 10th place. The total number of points were counted and 
any ties were decided by who got the most first-place votes.
