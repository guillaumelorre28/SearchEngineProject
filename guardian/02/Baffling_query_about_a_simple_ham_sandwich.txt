
A city wired up to warn asthmatics when the air pollution is bad is not a 
smart city (It’s time to put IT at the centre of cities 
<https://www.theguardian.com/public-leaders-network/2015/aug/03/lead-push-smarter-life-cities>
, 3 August). A smart city would use IT to manage traffic so air stays fit to 
breathe.
Judith Hanna
London
