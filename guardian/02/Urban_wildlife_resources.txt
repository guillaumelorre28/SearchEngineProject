Websites

British Trust for Ornithology <http://www.bto.org> Runs scientific surveys of 
Britain's bird populations.

Buglife <http://www.buglife.org.uk> Britain's main insect and invertebrate 
conservation organisation.

Butterfly Conservation  <http://www.butterfly-conservation.org> Dedicated to 
saving Britain's butterflies and moths and their habitats.

The Mammal Society  <http://www.mammal.org.uk> Devoted to research and 
conservation of all Britain's mammal species.

National Trust <http://www.nationaltrust.org.uk> Britain's largest 
conservation charity, protecting green spaces as well as stately homes.

Plantlife <http://www.plantlife.org.uk> Dedicated to saving Britain's wild 
plants and their habitats.

RSPB <http://www.rspb.org.uk> Europe's largest bird conservation organisation.

Wildfowl and Wetlands Trust <http://www.wwt.org.uk> This wetlands charity has 
nine centres in the UK, including the London Wetland Centre.

The Wildlife Trusts  <http://www.wildlifetrusts.org> The national federation 
of 47 local trusts, each of which covers a particular county, region or in the 
case of Scotland, country.
