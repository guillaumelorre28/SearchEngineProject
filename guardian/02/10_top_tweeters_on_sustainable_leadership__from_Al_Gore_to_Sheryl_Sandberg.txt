
To celebrate the launch of Guardian Sustainable Business’ leadership hub, 
we’ve compiled a list of ten top tweeters who are sharing examples of 
sustainable leadership in action and are helping drive forward the 
sustainability agenda.

Of course, there will be plenty more to add to the list so please do share 
your recommendations in the comments below or tweet us@GuardianSustBiz 
<https://twitter.com/GuardianSustBiz>.

The outdoor clothes company that asks its customers to think twice before 
buying something new, even if it negatively affects its own bottom line is a 
true example of sustainable corporate leadership.
