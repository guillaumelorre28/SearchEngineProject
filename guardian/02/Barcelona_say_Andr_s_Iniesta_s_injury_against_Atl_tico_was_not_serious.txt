
Barcelona have said that the knee injury Andrés Iniesta picked up during 
Saturday's goalless draw at Atlético Madrid 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2014/jan/11/atletico-madrid-barcelona-la-liga-match-report>
 is not serious.

Iniesta injured his left knee in a tackle in the 22nd minute of the La Liga 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/laligafootball> match and though the 
Spain midfielder struggled on he had to be replaced at half-time, by Lionel 
Messi.

The Barça manager, Gerardo Martino, said he was happy with the result. "It's a 
good point if we keep in mind that we played away to a team that have not 
conceded any points at home up until today. We wanted to win the match but we 
can't be reproached for getting this result. I have the feeling that the match 
will make us stronger."

Barcelona <https://www.theguardian.com/football/barcelona> visit Getafe on 
Thursday in the Copa del Rey after winning the first leg of their last-16 tie 
4-0. They then visit Levante on Sunday in the league.Barcelona and Atlético 
Madrid are level with 50 points at the midpoint of the season, with the Catalan 
club top on goal difference.
