
Barcelona have said that Andrés Iniesta 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/andres-iniesta> will be sidelined for 
three weeks with a muscle tear in his left thigh.

The midfielder was injured in the first half of Barcelona's 2-2 draw with Real 
Madrid 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2012/jan/25/barcelona-real-madrid-copa-del-rey>
 in the Copa del Rey quarter-finals on Wednesday. Barcelona advanced 4-3 on 
aggregate.

Barcelona <https://www.theguardian.com/football/barcelona> also lost the 
forward Alexis Sánchez during the game with a shoulder injury that will keep 
him out for one or two weeks.

Both players will miss Saturday's La Liga 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/laligafootball> match at Villarreal and 
the first leg of the Copa del Rey semi-finals next week, against either 
Valencia or Levante.

Barcelona are already without the striker David Villa, the midfielder Ibrahim 
Afellay and the defender Andreu Fontàs because of long-term injuries.
