
Andrew Breitbart <https://www.theguardian.com/media/andrew-breitbart>, the 
Conservative internet publisher and activist, has died at the age of 43.

A statement published on his Breitbart.com <http://www.breitbart.com/> site 
said he "passed away unexpectedly of natural causes" shortly after midnight in 
Los Angeles.

He is survived by his wife, Susannah Bean Breitbart, 41, and four children. 
His death prompted a wave of reaction on Twitter and elsewhere in the media.
