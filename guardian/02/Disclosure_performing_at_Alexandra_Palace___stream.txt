
Last month, London's 10,000-capacity Alexandra Palace hosted the Lawrence 
brothers, the ubiquitous dance revivalists and chart-toppers known as 
Disclosure.

Featuring many guest vocalists – AlunaGeorge's Aluna Francis on the high-energy
White Noise <http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bkk2H3Ztrfk>, Sam Smith on the 
love-lornLatch <http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=93ASUImTedo>, Eliza Doolittle on 
the garage-inflectedYou & Me <http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W_vM8ePGuRM> and 
Sasha Keable on the deep house grooves ofVoices 
<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NojLRA_mBpk> – who appeared on their No 1 
album,Settle 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/musicblog/2013/dec/13/best-albums-2013-disclosure-number-6>
, the performance erupted from under the duo's looming, illustrated album 
artwork.
