
They say the bookie always wins, but not on Tuesday night. 

Three weeks ago, bookmaker Paddy Power paid out £800,000 in winnings to 
punters who had backed Hillary Clinton to be the next US president, only now to 
be left with a bill for £4m to pay clients who had backedDonald Trump 
<https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2016/nov/09/donald-trump-wins-us-election-news>
.

“We’re in the business of making predictions and decided to put our neck on 
the line by paying out early on Hillary Clinton, but boy did we get it wrong,” a
Paddy Power <https://www.theguardian.com/business/paddy-power> spokesman said.


“We’ve been well and truly thumped by Trump, with his victory leaving us with 
the biggest political payout in the company’s history and some very, very 
expensive egg on our faces.”
