
1 BBC Symphony Orchestra

Edward Gardner 
<http://www.bbc.co.uk/music/artists/5501ec7f-731c-4b8d-8679-1680224930d6>’s 
latest concert with the BBC Symphony Orchestra concentrates onmusic from 
eastern Europe <http://www.bbc.co.uk/events/edmwhn>. There’s Janáček’s overture 
Jealousy, two of Smetana’s symphonic poems, and the UK premiere of Eötvös’s 
orchestral piece The Gliding Of The Eagle In The Skies.
Barbican Hall, EC2, Saturday 7 Jan 
<http://www.barbican.org.uk/music/event-detail.asp?ID=19132>

2 BBC National Orchestra of Wales

BBC NOW’s Great Brits <http://www.bbc.co.uk/events/ebrmbp> is a series devoted 
to recent British scores. The programme includesDavid Sawer 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/2016/aug/15/facing-the-music-composer-david-sawer>
, Gavin Bryars, Charlie Barber, and Howard Skempton’s lingering Lento as the 
concert’s centrepiece.
Wales Millennium Centre, Cardiff, Friday 13 Jan  
<https://www.wmc.org.uk/Productions/2017-2018/HoddinottHall/greatbrits1/?view=Standard>

3 Cuarteto Casals

A weekend of some of the greatest string quartets ever written: the six works 
thatMozart <https://www.theguardian.com/music/mozart> published in 1785, with a 
dedication toHaydn <https://www.theguardian.com/music/joseph-haydn>. Cuarteto 
Casals divide the performance into two recitals.
Wigmore Hall, W1, Saturday 7 Jan and Sunday 8 Jan 
<https://www.wigmore-hall.org.uk/whats-on/cuarteto-casals-201701071930>

4 Andris Nelsons

Around 18 months after he stepped down as music director of the City of 
Birmingham Symphony Orchestra <https://cbso.co.uk/>, Nelsons 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/andris-nelsons> returns to Symphony Hall as 
a guest. The main work in his programme is Bruckner’s Symphony No 4, but before 
that he harks back to the start of his career as a trumpeter, conducting Håkan 
Hardenberger inPeter Maxwell Davies 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/peter-maxwell-davies>’s Trumpet Concerto.
Symphony Hall, Birmingham, Thursday 12 Jan 
<https://www.thsh.co.uk/event/andris-nelsons-conducts-bruckner>

5 Written On Skin

George Benjamin <https://www.theguardian.com/music/george-benjamin>’s opera is 
revived, with Christopher Purves and Barbara Hannigan returning as The 
Protector and his wife Agnès in the 12th-century tale.Iestyn Davies 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/2015/jun/28/iestyn-davies-countertenor-farinelli-interview>
 is the boy with whom Agnès falls fatally in love.
Royal Opera House, WC2, Friday 13 Jan to Monday 30 Jan 
<http://www.roh.org.uk/productions/written-on-skin-by-katie-mitchell>
