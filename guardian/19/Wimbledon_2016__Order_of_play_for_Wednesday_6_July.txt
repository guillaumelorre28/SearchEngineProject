
All eyes will be on Centre Court on Wednesday as Roger Federer 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/roger-federer> and Andy Murray continue 
their Wimbledon campaigns. The pair, after Novak Djokovic’s exit, are both 
eyeing the title and will be favourites to dispose of Marin Cilic and 
Jo-Wilfried Tsonga in the men’s quarter-finals.

There is more British interest on Court No2 with Johanna Konta in action with 
Maria Sanchez against Timea Babos (Hun) & Yaroslava Shvedova.
