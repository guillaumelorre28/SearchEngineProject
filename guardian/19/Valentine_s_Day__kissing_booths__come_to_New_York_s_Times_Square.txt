As Valentine’s Day approaches, snogging in Times Square is about to be 
honoured – encouraged even – once more, with the installation of kissing booths 
in the tourist-packed area in Midtown Manhattan.

Created by local designers at Collective–LOK <http://collective-lok.com/>, the 
“Heart of Hearts” installation will be a ring of nine 10ft-high hearts . And 
inside each heart, diamond-shaped spaces form “kissing booths” for enamoured 
couples.

The Times Square Alliance <http://www.timessquarenyc.org/index.aspx> has run a 
competition to find romantic public art installations for the square for the 
past eight years and this design was chosen as the winner.

The golden, mirrored hearts will simultaneously reflect the couples within it 
and the square around it, creating a “kaleidoscopic interior that dissolves the 
boundaries between viewing and performing”.

According to the designers: “Times Square is the heart of New York City – a
