 Jacub Górnicki. Photograph: Jacub Górnicki 
Jakub joined EP Foundation two years ago. After playing an active role in 
promoting and developing Sejmometr, parliamentary monitoring portal in Poland, 
he focused on securing funding from Open Society Foundations and Omidyar 
Network. In 2012 he joined the EPF board and became international ambassador. 
His primary focus now is to encourage organisation from other countries to use 
technology to embrace change.

He is also implementations manager for Sourcefabric, an organisation devoted 
to building open source software for professional journalists and publishers. 
Jakub works with journalists from Georgia, Ghana, Turkey, Yemen and more 
countries.
