 Activate London 2012: Daniel Hirschmann 
Daniel is an artist, maker and educator who uses relationships with technology 
as the core of his practice. He has been making interactive installations, 
spaces and objects for over ten years and has worked with organisations ranging 
from Nike, and AOL to the Science Museum and the Whitney Museum of Art. He has 
shown work internationally and has taught physical computing, Arduino, 
programming and interaction design locally and abroad for over eight years. He 
geeks out about meaningful new businesses, making and awesome tech.
