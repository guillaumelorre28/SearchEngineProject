 we’d like to see pictures of your journeys through the years.

Whether you’ve gone the whole distance from Moscow to Vladivostock

 in the east or tookthe trans-Mongolian route

have pictures of the engines themselves, the people you met while travelling, 
or perhaps of sights on the way such asLake Baikal

Did you travel in luxury or share a coach with strangers? Maybe you made great 
friends on your trip? Perhaps you are or were a local to one of the communities 
it served and commuted cross-country on the service? Whichever it is, send us 
your pictures and we’ll compile some of your most interesting memories for a 
gallery on the Guardian.
