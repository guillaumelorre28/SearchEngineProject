Jonathon is a passionate believer in putting the user at the heart of product 
development. He has launched numerous award-winning digital properties for 
multiple brands - both big and small. He's held senior product roles at 
Guardian News and Media (where he launched a range of award-winning mobile and 
tablet apps), the BBC (where he managed their flagship News and Sport services) 
and has previously been director, product at a top-tier VC-backed start-up 
among many other roles. He is currently senior product manager, iOS, at Skype.
