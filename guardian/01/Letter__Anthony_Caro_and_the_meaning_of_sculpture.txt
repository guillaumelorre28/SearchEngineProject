
A charming example of Sir Anthony Caro 
<https://www.theguardian.com/artanddesign/2013/oct/24/sir-anthony-caro> 
preferring to let his work speak for itself was afforded toward the close of a 
somewhat long-wordy questionnaire by Peter Fuller in Art Monthly. When Fuller 
asked what his sculptures meant, Caro replied: "What does breakfast mean?"
