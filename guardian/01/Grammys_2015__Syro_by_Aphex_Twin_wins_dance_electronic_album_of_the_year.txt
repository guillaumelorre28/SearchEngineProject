
Aphex Twin <https://www.theguardian.com/music/aphex-twin> has won the best 
dance/electronic album at the 2015 Grammys for his album Syro beating off 
competition from Deadmau5, Little Dragon, Royksopp & Robyn and Mat Zo.

The artist who originally put out the album via the dark web, took home his 
first Grammy after releasing his first record in 12 years.

Aphex Twin (real name Richard D James) has recently released an EP on Warp 
records and has also uploaded more than 150 demo tracks on his Soundcloud 
account.
