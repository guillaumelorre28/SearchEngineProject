 Activate London 2012: William Hoyle 
William joined techfortrade as founding chief executive in February 2011.  
Prior to his appointment, he spent seven years as chief executive of Charity 
Technology Trust (CTT), a UK based social enterprise. Under his leadership CTT 
grew to serve over 10,000 UK charities, connecting them with the technology 
solutions needed to achieve their missions. His previous career includes 25 
years in the financial services and technology sectors, including payments and 
mobile and fixed line services businesses. William is also chair of trustees of 
Business Launchpad, a London youth enterprise charity and a board member of 
Warsaw based Fundacja TechSoup.
