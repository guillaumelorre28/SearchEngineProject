
When Georges Méliès's 1902 silent film A Trip to the Moon was recently 
restored, Air were the obvious choice to provide a new soundtrack. Now they 
have expanded on the project to make a full-length album inspired by the movie. 
Tim Jonzereviewed it for the Guardian today 
<http://www.guardian.co.uk/music/2012/feb/02/air-voyage-dans-lune-review?newsfeed=true>
, praising its "warm, homemade feel". Have a listen using the widget above and
