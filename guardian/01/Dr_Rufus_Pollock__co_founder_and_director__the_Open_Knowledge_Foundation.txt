Rufus is co-founder and director of the Open Knowledge Foundation and a 
Shuttleworth Foundation Fellow. The Open Knowledge Foundation is a 
community-based, not-for-profit founded in 2004. It has been a pioneer in the 
field of open data and open knowledge since its inception in 2004. The 
foundation builds tools and communities to create, use and share open knowledge 
- content and data that everyone can use, share and build on - and currently 
has activities in more than a dozen countries around the world.
