
Kevin is editor and digital strategist for the Media Development Loan Fund's 
Knowledge Bridge project, which helps independent news organisations to make 
the transition to digital. Kevin has over fifteen years of digital journalism 
experience. In 1998, he became the first online journalist for the BBC outside 
of the US, based in the BBC's flagship Washington bureau. In 2006, he joined 
the Guardian as their first blogs editor and later became digital research 
editor. From 2010 to 2012, as an independent strategist he worked with news 
organisations including Al Jazeera, India's Network18 and Reed Business 
Information.
