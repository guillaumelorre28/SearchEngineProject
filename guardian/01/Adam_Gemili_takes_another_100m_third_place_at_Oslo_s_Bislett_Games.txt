
Adam Gemili <https://www.theguardian.com/sport/adam-gemili> of England 
finished third in the 100 metres at the Diamond League meeting at the Bislett 
stadium in Oslo. The 20-year-old, who was also third-best in Rome last week, 
recorded a time of 10.11sec to finish behind Richard Thompson (10.02) of 
Trinidad & Tobago and France’s Jimmy Vicaut (10.04). England’s Richard Kilty 
and Daniel Talbot finished seventh and eighth.

Asha Philip and Jodie Williams were fifth and sixth in the women’s 100m, which 
was won by Myriam Soumaré of France, while Eilidh Child of Scotland finished 
third in the 400m hurdles with a time of 55.33sec.
