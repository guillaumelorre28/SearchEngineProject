 Activate London 2012: Ivan Sigal 
Ivan is the executive director of Global Voices 
<http://www.globalvoicesonline.org>, a non-profit online global citizens' media 
community. He also designs and creates media projects around the world, with a 
focus on networked communities. He writes and speaks about media, conflict, 
networked societies, development, humanitarian disasters. Ivan has travelled to 
and worked in over seventy countries, including long periods in Eastern Europe, 
the former Soviet Union and Asia.
