
Swansea City <https://www.theguardian.com/football/swansea> have completed the 
signing of the 23-year-old Dutch defender Mike van der Hoorn from Ajax.

The centre-back, who has represented Holland at under-21 level, joined Ajax 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/ajax> from Utrecht in July 2013, and made 
43 appearances in three years.

He strengthens the manager Francesco Guidolin 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2016/may/11/swansea-city-francesco-guidolin-signs-new-contract>
’s central defensive options following Kyle Bartley’s loan move to Leeds United.

The deal comes a day after Swansea signed Van der Hoorn’s compatriot Leroy Fer 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2016/jul/05/leroy-fer-permanent-swansea-move-qpr>
 from Queens Park Rangers for an undisclosed fee. Fer impressed Guidolin while 
on loan last season.
