
The Beano will feature cartoon versions of stars including Alesha Dixon and 
double Brit winners JLS in a special edition forSport Relief 
<https://www.theguardian.com/tv-and-radio/sport-relief>.

They will appear in strips in the Beano's monthly edition, BeanoMAX, along 
with England cricketer Stuart Broad and Manchester United striker Michael Owen.

The comic's special guests take on some of the Beano's best-known characters 
in a series of Sport Relief challenges, includingJLS 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/jls> in a battle of the bands against Dennis 
the Menace and his Dinmakers.

Dixon becomes best pals with Minnie the Minx, Broad plays cricket with Roger 
the Dodger and Michael Owen gets a football masterclass from the Bash Street 
Kids.

BeanoMAX, which also includes a 32-page insert dedicated to Sport Relief, goes 
on sale today.

Sport Relief, which launched in 2002, returns for a weekend of fundraising 
activity on 19 March, including a night of television programmes on BBC1.
