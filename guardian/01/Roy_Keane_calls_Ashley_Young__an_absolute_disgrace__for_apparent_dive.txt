
Roy Keane has called Ashley Young “an absolute disgrace” for an apparent dive 
in theChampions League <https://www.theguardian.com/football/championsleague> 
match against CSKA Moscow.

Young left his leg in against the defender Georgi Shchennikov in the 
second-half ofUnited’s 1-0 victory 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2015/nov/03/manchester-united-cska-moscow-champions-league-match-report>
 in the hope of winning a penalty as United struggled to get on top of their 
Russian opponents amidgrowing disquiet among their fans 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/2015/nov/03/manchester-united-louis-van-gaal-fans-disapproval>
.
 Roy Keane makes his views felt. 
“I think the guy’s a disgrace and if he’s a Man United player I’m a Chinaman,” 
the former captain said on ITV.

Young was fined by Sir Alex Ferguson and dropped for diving against Aston 
Villa in 2012
