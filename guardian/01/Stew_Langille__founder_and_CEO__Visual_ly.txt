 Activate London 2012: Stew Langille 
Stew is the CEO and co-founder of Visually. Visually is the #1 destination for 
data visualization on the web. Prior to Visually, Stew was VP marketing for 
mint.com. With a strong focus on low cost acquisition methods he successfully 
developed Mint into one of the most successful Web 2.0 brands. He also serves 
as an adviser for startups in developing their brand and social media 
strategies, primary as a mentor to 500 Startups.
