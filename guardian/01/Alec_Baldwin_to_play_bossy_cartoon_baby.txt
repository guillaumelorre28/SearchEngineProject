
Alec Baldwin has been cast as a garrulous businessman baby in the latest 
animated movie from the studio behind Kung Fu Panda and How to Train Your 
Dragon,reports Variety 
<http://variety.com/2014/film/news/alec-baldwin-to-voice-dreamworks-animations-boss-baby-1201317332/>
.
The outspoken star has signed on to voice the lead in Boss Baby, based on 
Marla Frazee’sillustrated children’s book 
<http://www.amazon.co.uk/Boss-Baby-Classic-Board-Books/dp/1442487798> about a 
briefcase-carrying, suit-and-tie-sporting sprog. Oscar-winner Kevin Spacey will 
voice the movie’s villain, Francis E Francis. The plot sees Boss Baby teaming 
up with his seven-year-old brother to foil the nefarious corporate bad guy’s 
evil plans.
Tom McGrath, who co-directed all three of Dreamworks’ Madagascar movies, is 
taking charge of the cameras. He will work from a screenplay by Michael 
McCullers of the Austin Powers films with the aim of a March 2016 release date.
