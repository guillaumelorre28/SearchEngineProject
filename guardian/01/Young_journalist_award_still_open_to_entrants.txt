
There's still time to enter the Anthony Howard 
<https://www.theguardian.com/media/anthony-howard> Award for Young Journalists. 
The award, established by Haymarket Media Group in memory of the celebrated 
political journalist and former deputy editor of theObserver, offers a bursary 
of £25,000 and three successive internships at theTimes, the Observer and the 
New Statesman.

Details and rules at anthonyhowardaward.org.uk 
<http://www.anthonyhowardaward.org.uk/>. Applications will close at 23.59 this 
Thursday, 1 May.
