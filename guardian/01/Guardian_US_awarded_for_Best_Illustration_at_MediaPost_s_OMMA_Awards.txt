
Guardian Labs US picked up an award at MediaPost’s OMMA Awards 
<https://www.theguardian.com/gnm-press-office/awards>.

The team was recognized within the Illustration category for collaborating 
with Amazon Studios and promoting the second season of the detective show 
Bosch. Guardian Labs US created a true crime reporting series,‘How to Solve a 
Murder’ <https://www.theguardian.com/how-to-solve-a-murder> that drives 
awareness through illustrations in a clever way.


The OMMA (Online Marketing Media and Advertising) Awards honor agencies and 
advertisers that push the potential of digital advertising.


A full list of winners can be found here 
<http://www.mediapost.com/ommaawards/winners/>.
