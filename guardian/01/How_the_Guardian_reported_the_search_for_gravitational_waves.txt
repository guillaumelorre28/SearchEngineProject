
Einstein's theory of relativity stands up to scrutiny: archive, 4 February 1924
 Read more  
<https://www.theguardian.com/theguardian/2014/feb/04/albert-einstein-relativity-theory>
Gravitational waves were predicted as part of Einstein’s theory of general 
relativity. Scientists have been searching for evidence of their existence ever 
since.

In 1971, the Guardian’s Night Sky column 
<http://static.guim.co.uk/sys-images/Guardian/Pix/pictures/2016/2/11/1455208933838/3-July-1971---gravitation-001.jpg>
 reported on attempts by Dr J Weber, at the University of Maryland, to detect 
gravitational radiation from very large masses. NASA meanwhile was planning to 
place a gravimeter on the moon.
