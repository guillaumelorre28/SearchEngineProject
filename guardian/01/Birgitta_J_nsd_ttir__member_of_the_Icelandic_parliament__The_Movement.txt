Birgitta is a member of parliament of Althing, the Icelandic parliament, 
formerly representing the Citizens' Movement, but now representing The 
Movement. She was elected to the Icelandic parliament in April 2009 on behalf 
of a movement aiming for democratic reform beyond party politics of left and 
right. Birgitta has been an activist and a spokesperson for various groups, 
such as Wikileaks, Saving Iceland and Friends of Tibet in Iceland. She acts as 
a spokeswoman for the Icelandic Modern Media Initiative. She is also a poet, 
writer, artist, editor, publisher and internet pioneer.
