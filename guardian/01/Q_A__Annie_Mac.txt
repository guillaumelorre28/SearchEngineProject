
Annie Mac, 31, was born in Dublin. Having studied at Queen's University 
<http://www.qub.ac.uk/home/TheUniversity/GeneralInformation/KeyContacts/> in 
Belfast, she moved to London where she worked as a radio plugger, a 
broadcasting assistant and presenter for the student radio networkSBN 
<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Student_Broadcast_Network>. In July 2004 she was 
given her own show on Radio 1 and since September 2009 she has hosted the 
prestigious Friday 7-9pm slot. On 5 June, her Annie Mac Presents club night 
celebrates its first birthday atKoko <http://www.koko.uk.com/> in Camden, north 
London.

What is your earliest memory?
Getting hit by my brother.

Which living person do you most admire, and why?
My parents, for still being in love after 40 years.

What is the trait you most deplore in yourself?
Lack of willpower.

What is the trait you most deplore in others?
Overfamiliarity

Aside from a property, what's the most expensive thing you've bought?
A holiday in Ibiza.

What is your most treasured possession?
It was a massive box of photographs. I lost them in a move.

What would your super power be?
The ability to sleep anywhere at anytime.

What do you most dislike about your appearance?
I have big hockey player's calves. And my boobs have shrunk recently.

Who would play you in the film of your life?
Colm Meaney <http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000538/>, the Irish guy with 
the curly hair who's in Star Trek: The Next Generation.

To whom would you most like to say sorry, and why?
To my now deceased bunny rabbit, Bonnie: I should have cleaned out your cage 
more.

What is your favourite word?
Dublin.

What is your favourite book?
Borstal Boy <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Borstal_Boy> by Brendan Behan.

What would be your fancy dress costume of choice? 
Bianca Jagger when she went to Studio 54 
<http://www.camparijoy.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/bianca-studio-54.jpg>
 in New York.

What is the worst thing anyone's said to you?
In secondary school a boy told me I was mediocre. I was devastated.

What is your guiltiest pleasure?
Bread.

What or who is the greatest love of your life?
Music.

What does love feel like?
A punch in the face.

What was the best kiss of your life?
My first, when I was 13. I was so relieved it was over, as I was stressing for 
days over how to do it…

What is the worst job you've done?
When I was 16 I worked as a chambermaid at a hotel in Hamburg. I had a boss, 
Frau Plas. She made me clean dog poo from the street as a punishment for 
singing while cleaning.

What has been your biggest disappointment?
Not getting into drama college in Dublin. I cut all my hair off as a sort of 
self-punishment for being crap.

If you could edit your past, what would you change?
The width of the jeans I wore when I was 18.

If you could go back in time, where would you go?
NYC late 70s… block parties with Kool Herc 
<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DJ_Kool_Herc> and Grandmaster Flash 
<http://www.grandmasterflash.com/>.

What is the closest you've come to death?
Falling out of a raft while whitewater rafting down the crocodile-infested 
Zambezi river <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zambezi> in Zimbabwe.

What do you consider your greatest achievement?
That I like myself.
