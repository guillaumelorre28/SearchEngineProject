
Dharmesh Thakker joined ATV in 2007 as a member of the firm's cleantech and IT 
Infrastructure practice areas. He observes on the boards of AltaRock Energy, 
Calisolar, Nuventix, Qumu, and Sub-one Technology. He is a founding member and 
co-president of the Wharton Cleantech and Energy Network and also serves on the 
cleantech advisory board at TiE. Dharmesh brings 10 years of business 
development, product strategy and sales experience at start-ups and public 
companies to assist ATV's portfolio companies. Dharmesh received his M.B.A. 
from The Wharton School and a B.S. in Electrical Engineering from The 
University of Texas at Austin
