
Mike Noonan is a managing director of NYSE Euronext and head of the New 
Business Group for the Americas. He is responsible for directing the overall 
activity for new corporate listings. Mr. Noonan has a combined 14 years of 
investment banking experience. Most recently, he came from JP Morgan where he 
was an executive director in the Equity Capital Markets Group covering 
technology, media and telecom clients. Prior to JP Morgan, Mr. Noonan was a 
managing director and spent 10 years at Bear Stearns, covering TMT clients in 
both coverage and equity capital market functions. Prior work experience 
includes various corporate banking roles within Wachovia Corporation in 
Charlotte, North Carolina. Mr. Noonan has a BA from Davidson College and an MBA 
from Duke University's Fuqua School of Business.
