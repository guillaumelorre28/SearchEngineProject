
Born in Newcastle, Andrea Riseborough 
<https://www.theguardian.com/film/andrea-riseborough>, 34, studied at Rada. She 
was Bafta-nominated for her 2008 portrayal of Margaret Thatcher in the BBC’sThe 
Long Walk To Finchley 
<https://www.theguardian.com/culture/tvandradioblog/2008/jun/13/margaretthatcherthelongwalktofinchley>
. Her movies includeMade In Dagenham 
<https://www.theguardian.com/film/2010/sep/30/made-in-dagenham-film-review>, 
Shadow Dancer 
<https://www.theguardian.com/film/2012/aug/23/shadow-dancer-review>, Oblivion 
<https://www.theguardian.com/film/2013/apr/10/oblivion-review>, with Tom 
Cruise, andBirdman 
<https://www.theguardian.com/film/2015/jan/04/birdman-alejandro-gonzalez-inarritu-review-michael-keaton>
; she also appears inBloodline 
<https://www.theguardian.com/tv-and-radio/2015/mar/21/bloodline-review-netflix-sam-shephard-sissy-spacek>
 on Netflix. Her most recent TV drama,National Treasure 
<https://www.theguardian.com/tv-and-radio/2016/sep/20/national-treasure-review-robbie-coltrane>
, is out on DVD; at Christmas, she will appear opposite Toby Jones in a 
two-part BBC adaptation of Agatha Christie’sThe Witness For The Prosecution 
<https://www.theguardian.com/media/2016/aug/15/kim-cattrall-toby-jones-bbc-agatha-christie-witness-for-the-prosecution>
. She lives in Los Angeles.
