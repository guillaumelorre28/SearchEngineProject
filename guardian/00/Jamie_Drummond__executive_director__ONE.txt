
Jamie co-founded DATA in 2002 and co-founded ONE in 2004. They merged in 2008. 
ONE's mission is to help the world's poorest countries beat AIDS and achieve 
the Millennium Development Goals. DATA, ONE and partners persuaded the Bush 
Administration and bipartisan leadership in the US congress, with others, to 
launch a series of initiatives for Africa. Through the G8, DATA helped 
negotiate and advocate for a new high watermark of promises at the 2005 Summit 
for Africa.
 
Jamie was formerly global strategist for Jubilee 2000 Drop the Debt, and prior 
to that worked at Christian Aid. In 2007 the World Economic Forum elected Jamie 
a Young Global Leader.
