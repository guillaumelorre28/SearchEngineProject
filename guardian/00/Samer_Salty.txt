
Samer Salty founded zouk ventures in 1999. He has considerable management 
experience and has spent the last ten years building technology companies. He 
currently sits on the boards of Orb Energy and Solarcentury, two of zouk's 
cleantech investments. As a founding member, Samer is an active member of the 
advisory board of the Cleantech Venture Network in Europe. Prior to zouk, Samer 
worked in M&A and PE within the TMT Group at JPMorgan and was also head of 
global markets for the middle east. Before investment banking, Samer spent 
seven years designing and implementing communications and computer systems for 
the FAA and a number of other international civil aviation authorities.
