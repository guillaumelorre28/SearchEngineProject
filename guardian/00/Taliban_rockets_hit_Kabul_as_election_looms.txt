
At least eight rockets hit Kabul on Tuesday in the first big attack on the 
relatively calm capital in the run-up to this month's presidential election, 
police said. In southernAfghanistan 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/afghanistan>, a suicide bomber killed five 
people and wounded 18 , and a provincial governor escaped injury in an attack 
on his convoy. Officials said one rocket in the capital damaged a senior 
interior ministry official's house near the US embassy. A Taliban spokesman 
said militants fired rockets at the international airport and at a military 
headquarters, which were near embassies and government buildings.
