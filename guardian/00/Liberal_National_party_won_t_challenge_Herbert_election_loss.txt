
The Liberal-National party will not challenge the election result in the north 
Queensland <https://www.theguardian.com/australia-news/queensland> seat of 
Herbert after exhausting its inquiries.

The LNP had been considering taking the result to the court of disputed returns

“The LNP has exhausted its enquiries in relation to the vote in the federal 
seat of Herbert and advises it will not be taking any legal action,” it said in 
a statement on Tuesday

Sitting MP Ewen Jones, who lost the Townsville-based seat by 37 votes to 
Labor’s Cathy O’Toole, said he was disappointed for his parliamentary 
colleagues and himself.
