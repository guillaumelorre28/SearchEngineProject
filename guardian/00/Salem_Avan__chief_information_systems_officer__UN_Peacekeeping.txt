
Salem is responsible for information technology for peace operations, the 
largest department of the United Nations.

He joined peacekeeping in 1994 during the conflict in the former Yugoslavia 
and served with the peacekeeping forces in a number of countries. He also 
worked in Croatia with the Organisation for Security and Cooperation in Europe 
and in the Special Court for Sierra Leone.

In 2003 Salem moved to the United Nations Headquarters where he is responsible 
for strategy and direction of information technology in field missions, 
covering information and communications technology security, knowledge 
management, operational resilience, infrastructure and process automation.
