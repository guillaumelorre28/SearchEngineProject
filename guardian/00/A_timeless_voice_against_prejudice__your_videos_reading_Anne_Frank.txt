
To mark the 70th anniversary of Anne Frank’s death, readers have been 
celebrating her life and her words against prejudice – which are as necessary 
as they’ve ever been –in a rather unusual manner 
<https://www.theguardian.com/books/booksblog/2015/apr/14/notsilent-join-social-media-campaign-remember-anne-frank>
: instead of holding a minute of silence, they’ve been recording themselves 
reading aloud her diary for 60 seconds, and posting on social media with the 
hashtag#NotSilent 
<https://twitter.com/search?f=realtime&q=%23notsilent&src=typd>. Here are some 
of our favourite videos that readers have posted online – including some from 
authors, actors and other celebrities.
