
The super-producer is a now familiar figure in hip-hop (Timbaland, Pharell 
etc) but Marley Marl was one of the first. He took advantage of new sampling 
possibilities to broaden hip-hop's sonic palette and make the names of a new 
roster of rappers such as Kool G Rap and Big Daddy Kane. Each are in full 
effect on In Control, Volume One, which showcased Marl at the height of his 
powers.
