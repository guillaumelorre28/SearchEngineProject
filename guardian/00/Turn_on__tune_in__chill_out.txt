
A couple of years after the Second Summer of Love, the chill-out room was as 
much a draw as the main dancefloor. The Orb, initially made up of Jimmy Cauty 
of the KLF and Alex Paterson, were the chief pioneers here, already with cult 
singles to their name when Paul Oakenfold recruited Paterson to DJ at his 
London club night Land of Oz. Soon, they were on ToTP playing chess and dressed 
as astronauts, allusions to the psychedelic whimsy of the 60s all too obvious.
