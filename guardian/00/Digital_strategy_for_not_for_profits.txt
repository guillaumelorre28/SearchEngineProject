
Having a robust and targeted digital marketing strategy can make a world of 
difference to any business, especially for organisations that operate with 
limited budgets. This one-day Masterclass offers not-for-profits the chance to 
reassess their approach to digital marketing and closely investigate their 
goals, tactical approach and expected outcomes.


Digital marketing expert Stuart Buchanan <http://stuartbuchanan.com/>, who was 
a founding member of independent radio station FBi, and has helped develop 
digital strategies for Sydney Festival, Sydney Opera House and MONA, will help 
you to identify the macro trends that should be underpinning your strategy, the 
ways in which you can examine and critique your approach and the practical 
techniques for implementing your approach over the short and long term.

This course is for you if...

 * You work in marketing at a not-for-profit or arts organisation

 * You are looking to clarify your approach and direction in digital marketing

 * You are a senior manager seeking to reappraise your organisation’s use of 
digital marketing 
Course description

Offering both ‘big picture’ analysis and a hands-on practical approach, the 
course is aimed at a common sense and cost-effective approach to digital 
marketing, with an emphasis on working with limited resources.

Topics on the day will include:

• Understanding what a digital strategy includes and the framework under which 
it can be developed
• An overview of your customers and audiences who are currently using digital 
channels, and how that informs that your strategy
• Analysing your organisation’s vision and how that can be practically realised
• Conducting the right research and asking the right questions
• Defining your organisation’s personality online
• Finding the right way to tell your story through content
• Planning and scheduling
• Analysing opportunities, channel-by-channel – including web, mobile, apps, 
social media, email, search & SEO and content seeding

You will leave this Masterclass with the the beginnings of an effective 
digital marketing strategy that will help your organisation build the 
foundations of a robust communications and engagement strategy.
