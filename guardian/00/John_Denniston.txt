
John Denniston is a partner with Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers. At KPCB, 
John has worked with a wide variety of portfolio companies, with a primary 
emphasis on the Greentech industry. John was a member of the KPCB partner team 
that many years ago conceptualized and launched KPCB's Greentech investment 
initiative. He is actively involved in Greentech public policy issues, having 
testified before several congressional committees. John serves on the board of 
advisors of the National Renewable Energy Labs and is a frequent speaker at 
Greentech industry conferences.
