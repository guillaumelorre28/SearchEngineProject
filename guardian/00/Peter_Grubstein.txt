
Peter S.H. Grubstein is a founder and a managing director of NGEN Partners. 
Prior to establishing NGEN in 2000, Mr. Grubstein made diverse investments with 
a number of firms, ranging from venture capital to LBOs and leveraged 
recapitalizations. Before becoming an investor, Mr. Grubstein was president of 
the American Tanning Company. He is currently a member of the board of 
directors of Hycrete, Renaissance Lighting and Textronics. Mr. Grubstein is an 
observer on the board of Konarka Technologies, and serves on the advisory board 
of the Cleantech Venture Network. Mr. Grubstein received a BA degree from Yale 
College.
