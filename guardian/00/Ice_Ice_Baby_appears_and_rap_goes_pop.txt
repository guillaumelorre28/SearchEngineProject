
He was hailed as a hip-hop Elvis, the bequiffed white guy taking black music 
mainstream – but Vanilla Ice was destined for a footnote in hip-hop history. 
More important was that his Ice Ice Baby, and MC Hammer's U Can't Touch This, 
proved that rap could work as pop. Detractors had a field day – 3rd Bass 
brilliantly lampooned them both on Pop Goes the Weasel – but pop rap was here 
to stay.
