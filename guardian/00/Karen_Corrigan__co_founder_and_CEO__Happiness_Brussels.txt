
Karen is chief executive and strategic partner of Happiness Brussels, an 
agency she co-founded in 2006 with the ethos "everything and everyone is media".
 
Happiness Brussels is now internationally recognised as one of the leading 
agencies in Europe, ranking number three independent agency worldwide at Cannes 
Lions in 2009 and Interactive Agency of the Year at Eurobest.
 
Happiness's award-winning success is founded on diversity and a fearless 
approach to pushing the boundaries of communications creativity. The agency 
buzzes with the talent of 14 different nationalities and cultures, from which 
pure ideas are born, launched and proven to make a global difference.
