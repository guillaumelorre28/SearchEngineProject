
Sylvia Burks is the leader of Pillsbury's Clean Technology Group. Her practice 
focuses on the representation of established and emerging technology companies 
in the areas of corporate securities, financing, mergers, acquisitions and 
strategic alliances, with a particular focus on cross-border transactions for 
public and private companies based in Europe and Asia. Ms. Burks frequently 
speaks on topics including cleantech, Recovery Act opportunities, cross-border 
licensing and acquisitions, venture capital investments and investments in the 
technology and life sciences sectors.

Ms. Burks holds a J.D. from the University of Texas School of Law and an 
M.B.A. from the University of California, Los Angeles.
