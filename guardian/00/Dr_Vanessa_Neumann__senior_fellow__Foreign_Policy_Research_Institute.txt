
Vanessa is a senior fellow at the Foreign Policy Research Institute. She holds 
a Ph.D. in political philosophy from Columbia University. From 2005-2007, 
Vanessa was adjunct assistant professor of political philosophy at Hunter 
College of The City University of New York.

A native Venezuelan, Vanessa has worked as a journalist in Caracas, London and 
the US. She is editor-at-large of Diplomat, a London-based magazine and a 
regular contributor to the Washington, DC-based magazine The Weekly Standard on 
Latin American politics. She has also written for The Sunday Times, the 
Guardian, The Daily Telegraph and Standpoint.
