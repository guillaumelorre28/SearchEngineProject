extending her family, as she concluded her sellout world tour this week.

As she came to the end of her concert in Phoenix, Arizona, on Monday night – 
the last of over 100 shows – she was filmed saying: “I’m going to have another 
baby.”


The 28-year-old British singer is a mother to four-year-old son Angelo with 
her partner Simon Konecki.
