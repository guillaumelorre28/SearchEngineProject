
Katie Hill is the energy portfolio manager with Acumen Fund India, a social 
venture fund that invests in enterprises serving low-income markets with 
critical goods and services. In addition to scaling up Acumen's Energy 
Portfolio in India, Katie manages deals in the water application / agriculture 
sector. A graduate of Stanford University, Katie's background is in economic 
development and microfinance, having previously worked in Uganda, Nepal and 
Ecuador.
