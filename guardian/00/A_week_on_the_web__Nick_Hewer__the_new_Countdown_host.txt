
Nick Hewer, the PR man now better known Lord Sugar's sidekick on The 
Apprentice, has been named as thenew host of Countdown 
<http://www.guardian.co.uk/media/2011/nov/16/apprentice-nick-hewer-host-countdown>
.

He will join Channel 4's longest-running show on 9 January 2012, and replaces 
Jeff Stelling, who will presumably be moving on to spend more time with his 
football.

It's a tough gig. Indeed many would argue original host Richard Whitely was 
irreplaceable. His successors in the chair, Des Lynam, Des O'Connor and 
Stelling, certainly haven't been universally popular.

So how did the web react to Hewer's big-money transfer? Let us solve that 
conundrum for you...
