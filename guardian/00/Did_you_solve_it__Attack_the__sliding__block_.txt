
Earlier today I set you the following sliding-block puzzle: Can you get T to 
the bottom right-hand corner in five moves. A move takes any single piece to 
another position by sliding it between the others.

Solution:

 * Move 1 to the lower left hand corner

 * Move T to the right

 * Move 2 to the upper left corner

 * Move 3 to below 2

 * Move T to the lower right corner
