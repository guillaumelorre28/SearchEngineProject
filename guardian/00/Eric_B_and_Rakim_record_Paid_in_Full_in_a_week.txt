
One of the most revered acts, with MC Rakim often acclaimed as the Greatest of 
All Time (aka GOAT). Yet their masterpiece, which matched Rakim's laid-back 
swagger to some of the best James Brown breaks, came together in just seven 
days. Recorded in Marley Marl's studio, the duo claim to have worked 48-hour 
shifts and recorded in single takes in order to get the work completed within 
budget. With the album since selling over 1m copies, that debt has been paid in 
full.
