
Amal Alamuddin appears to have changed her last name after marrying actor 
George Clooney <https://www.theguardian.com/film/georgeclooney>.

Her professional profile on the Doughty Street Chambers website 
<http://www.doughtystreet.co.uk/barristers> has been changed from Amal 
Alamuddin to Amal Clooney. The page on the website that carries her profile has 
crashed since the story was published.

The London-based barrister, 36, married Clooney 
<https://www.theguardian.com/film/2014/sep/29/george-clooney-amal-alamuddin-wedding-14-minutes-venice>
 in a four-day celebration in Italy last month.

She had originally changed her name to Amal Alamuddin Clooney but is listed 
simply asAmal Clooney <https://www.theguardian.com/world/amal-alamuddin> on the 
website.

She has started work on her latest case 
<https://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/oct/09/amal-alamuddin-clooney-wife-greece-elgin-marbles-bid>
, in which she is advising Greece on a bid to have the Parthenon marbles, 
currently in the British Museum, returned to the country.
