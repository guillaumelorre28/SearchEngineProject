
Ory is the Google policy and government relations manager for Africa. She is 
co-founder of Ushahidi <http://www.ushahidi.com/> and served as the 
organisation's executive director from inception until December 2010. She is 
also the co-founder of Mzalendo <http://www.mzalendo.com>, a website that 
tracks the performance of Kenyan MP's.

Ory has been recognised as one of the Most Influential Women in Technology by 
Fast Company (2011), one of the top 100 Global Thinkers by Foreign Policy 
Magazine and a Huffington Post 2010 Game Changer. She is also the recipient of 
the Monaco Media Forum Award, and the World Economic Forum Tech Pioneers award.
