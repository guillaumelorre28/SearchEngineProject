
Michael's baby sister was grown up, no longer answering to her recently 
divorced husband, singer James DeBarge, or her father, and now ex-manager, Joe. 
An emancipated modern pop star still needed the occasional bit of help, though. 
Cue the producers Jimmy Jam and Terry Lewis, former Prince proteges and key 
players in R&B's annexation of the charts. This would be the core of Janet's 
subsequent six-year hot streak, and of much pop that followed.
