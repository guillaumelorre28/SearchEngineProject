
A man has been charged with murder after a car crashed through a house and 
killed a woman at a funeral viewing in northQueensland 
<https://www.theguardian.com/australia-news/queensland>.

The 48-year-old died and at least 12 other people were seriously injured when 
a council-owned vehicle was driven into the front of a home at Kowanyama on 
Friday morning.

It’s believed about 50 mourners were viewing a body at the time.

Two doctors and two nurses from the Royal Flying Doctor Service happened to be 
attending a clinic nearby and were among the first at the scene.

Two planes from the service were also sent.
