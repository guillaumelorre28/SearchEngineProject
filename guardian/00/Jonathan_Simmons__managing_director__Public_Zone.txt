
Jonathan is the managing director of Public Zone, a pro-social digital agency 
executing digital strategy for non-profit organisations including the National 
Trust, Centrepoint, Prostate Cancer Charity and Channel 4's coverage of the 
London 2012 Paralympic Games.

A publishing specialist, Jonathan cut his teeth at the Guardian before moving 
to Roma football club, and on to Zone, where he led projects for 
Lastminute.com, Channel 4 and Vodafone. He founded Public Zone in 2005, 
following his passion for education and communities and has overseen its growth 
to a leading agency in the sector. He tweets @PublicZone
