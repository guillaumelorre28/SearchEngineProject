
Hans Dellenbach is the CFO of Emerald Technology Ventures in Zurich and is 
responsible for investor relations, legal & compliance and the overall 
administration of the firm. Besides this, Hans also assesses deals and monitors 
portfolio companies from the financial perspective, from due diligence to 
transaction structuring and finally exit. Hans joined Emerald shortly after its 
creation in 2001 as a fund administrator and financial analyst, being 
responsible for the financial assessment and monitoring of investments as well 
as investor relations and reporting. Hans holds a bachelor's degree with a 
major in finance from the School of Economics and Business Administration in 
Zurich/Winterthur and is a CFA charterholder.
