
Kate is an award-winning business journalist both on camera and in print. She 
contributes to national UK newspapers and also writes regularly for specialist 
publications, including DTVE (formerly Cable & Satellite Europe), Hollywood 
Reporter and Broadcast Magazine in the UK.

In 1990, she was international editor for pay TV title Cable World magazine; 
then as media editor for European Business News - where she created and 
co-presented the award-winning, media show called Media Report. From 1998 - 
2001, Kate was on-screen media and telecommunications editor at CNBC Europe.
