
Whitney Rockley is a partner at Nomura New Energy & Clean Technology Ventures 
where she is responsible for investing and growing expansion stage companies 
focused on developing energy and water solutions. Prior to Nomura, Whitney 
started up and managed corporate venture capital funds for a large Canadian 
power and water company (EPCOR) and a large Canadian oil and gas company 
(EnCana). While at EnCana, she was seconded to Nth Power, a leading energy 
venture capital fund in San Francisco. In addition to Whitney's venture capital 
experience, she has extensive operating experience in the areas of analytics, 
finance and environment. Whitney sits on the boards of Novinium and DeepFlex 
and is an observer on the SpectraSensors board. Whitney is a member of the EU 
advisory board for the Cleantech Network.
