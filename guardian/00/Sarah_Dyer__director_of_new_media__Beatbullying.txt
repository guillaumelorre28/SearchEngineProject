
Sarah is director of New Media at Beatbullying. She is a multi award-winning 
interactive designer with nearly 20 years experience, with a particular 
interest in the way young people use the internet. Before joining Beatbullying, 
she worked as creative director at a number of agencies, such as Save The 
Children UK, Equality and Human Rights Commission and Womankind Worldwide.

Sarah was instrumental in the design of CyberMentors website which is 
revolutionising the provision of mentoring and counselling online and is 
currently working with her team on a major new platform development being built 
in Python and Erlang, which is underpinning the next generation of CyberMentors 
websites.
