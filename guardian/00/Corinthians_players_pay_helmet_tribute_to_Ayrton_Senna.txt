
Corinthians players paid an unusual tribute to the late Ayrton Senna 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/ayrton-senna> on Wednesday evening, with the 
entire side donning replicas of Senna's distinctive yellow and green racing 
helmet as they lined up before the game.

Senna, who died after a crash at Imola exactly 20 years ago on Thursday, was a 
fan ofCorinthians <https://www.theguardian.com/football/corinthians>, his 
home-town club in São Paulo.
