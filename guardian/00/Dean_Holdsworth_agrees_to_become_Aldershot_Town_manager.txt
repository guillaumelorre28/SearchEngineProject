
Dean Holdsworth has reached an agreement in principle to become the new 
manager ofAldershot Town 
<https://www.theguardian.com/football/aldershottownfootball>.

The 42-year-old former Wimbledon and Bolton striker will leave his post as 
Newport County manager to replace Kevin Dillon at the EBB Stadium.

He is set to finalise the terms of his contract with the League Two club 
tomorrow ahead of a press conference at 1.30pm.

Holdsworth leaves Newport in the Blue Square Bet Premier play-off places after 
guiding them to promotion from the Conference South last season with a record 
haul of 103 points, beating the previous mark set by Aldershot in 2007-08.
