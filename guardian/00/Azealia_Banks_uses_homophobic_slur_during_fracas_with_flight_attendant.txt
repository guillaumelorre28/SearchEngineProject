
Azealia Banks called a flight attendant a homophobic slur while trying to exit 
a plane on Monday night, a videoobtained by TMZ 
<http://www.tmz.com/2015/09/22/azealia-banks-flight-homophobic-slur-delta-video/>
 shows.


The rapper was on a flight from New York to LA that landed just before 1am on 
Tuesday,TMZ reported 
<http://www.tmz.com/2015/09/22/azealia-banks-flight-homophobic-slur-delta-video/>
.

The video <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NpsdmV8BKzQ> obtained by TMZ shows 
Banks playing tug-of-war with a flight attendant over her luggage, saying: 
“Give me my bag.” She then calls the flight attendant a “fucking faggot”.


A spokesperson for Delta Air Lines said in a statement: “We are aware of the 
situation and are looking into it.”


On Twitter, where she has frequently used homophobic slurs 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/2013/jan/07/azealia-banks-perez-hilton>, 
Banks defended her actions,according to the New York Daily News 
<http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/gossip/azealia-banks-drops-homophobic-slur-airplane-meltdown-article-1.2369767>
: “Ok so I get hit in the face by a man and no one cares. The [sic] only hear 
what I called the man who was blocking my exit,” she wrote.

“I don’t care. I’ve said it before and I’ll say it again. I don’t tolerate 
bitchassness and I don’t tolerate men putting their hands on me.”


Banks has since deleted the tweets.
