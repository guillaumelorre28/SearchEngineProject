
Yong Zhang is a cleantech practice partner of Qiming Ventures. Prior to 
joining Qiming, Yong was the China technology growth leader of General Electric 
company, responsible for managing the $30 million In China For China fund and 
driving the overall company external technology BD and collaboration. Prior to 
this, he held a variety of management positions in GE and Siemens, both in 
China and the U.S., in R&D and engineering management. He received his 
bachelor's and master's degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and Ph.D. 
degree in Electrical Engineering from the National University of Singapore.
