
Rick Fratus is a senior vice president with Macquarie Funds Group. He is a 
member of the private equity team's Senior Partners Committee and Investment 
Committee. He heads the team's investments in energy, clean technology, venture 
capital, and growth capital. Rick is also the senior portfolio manager for the 
Macquarie Clean Technology Fund, a $205 million clean technology private equity 
fund of funds. Rick received his MBA from the University of Texas, Austin, and 
his Bachelor of Science degree from the U.S. Naval Academy with merit.
