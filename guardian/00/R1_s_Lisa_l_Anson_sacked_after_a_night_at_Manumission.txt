
Radio 1 likes its presenters to keep up with the kids. So in the late 90s, not 
content with their summer roadshows in Blackpool, they sent their DJs off to 
Ibiza. Zoe Ball famously pulled an all-nighter and only just made it to the 
studio in time, only to be sick two minutes before going on air. Lisa I'Anson 
wasn't quite so lucky. She was still busy with the night before at the 
notorious Manumission club when her breakfast show started.Radio
