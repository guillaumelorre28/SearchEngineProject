
Robin Soderling, the No1 seed, beat the defending champion Andy Roddick 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/andy-roddick> in straight sets to win the 
Brisbane International title at the Pat Rafter Arena in Brisbane.

Soderling, of Sweden, finished the tournament without dropping a set as he 
enjoyed a 6-3, 7-5 win over the American and which relied heavily on his strong 
serving and returning game.

The Czech Republic's Petra Kvitova 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/petra-kvitova> beat Germany's Andrea 
Petkovic 6-1, 6-3 in the women's final.

Kim Clijsters <https://www.theguardian.com/sport/kim-clijsters>, the US Open 
champion, made short work of Romania's Alexandra Dulgheru in the first round of 
the Sydney International tournament at the Olympic Park. The Belgian, seeded 
No3, took just over an hour to win 6-1, 6-2 to set up a clash with either 
Russia's Nadia Petrova or a qualifier for a place in the quarter-finals.

Russia's Svetlana Kuznetsova also reached round two with a 6-2, 6-2 victory 
over Australia's Jelena Dokic.
