
Eden Hazard will not play for Belgium in their Euro 2016 qualifier against 
Andorra on Friday because of a bruised toe, the manager Marc Wilmotsconfirmed 
on Twitter <https://twitter.com/WilmotsMarc/status/520545213895680000>.

The Chelsea midfielder’s toe was “blue” at the beginning of the week, 
according to theBelgium <https://www.theguardian.com/football/belgium> manager, 
but his absence is understood to be a precautionary measure.

Belgium travel to Bosnia-Herzegovina on Monday, where Hazard is expected to 
return.
