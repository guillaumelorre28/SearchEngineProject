
Andrew Thomson is responsible for Cleantech Group's research and analysis of 
global cleantech markets, spanning thousands of companies, technologies and 
financial transactions. He was previously at Library House, a UK-based provider 
of intelligence on fast-growth, innovation-led private companies across Europe. 
As head of data and information services he was responsible for the content 
that underpinned the company's products and services. Andrew has a PhD in 
Chemistry from Cambridge University and a BSc in the same subject from 
Edinburgh University.
