
Gawky synth-lovers not long out of school, Essex's Depeche Mode 
<https://www.theguardian.com/music/depeche-mode> didn't seem much like 
potential megastars when they agreed to record their first single, Dreaming of 
Me, for Mute. Even their initial chart success seemed lightweight. But after 
Vince Clarke left to form Yazoo and, later, Erasure, his old bandmates 
gradually proceeded to blow synthpop's horizons wide open. By the close of the 
decade, they were filling stadiums across the globe.
