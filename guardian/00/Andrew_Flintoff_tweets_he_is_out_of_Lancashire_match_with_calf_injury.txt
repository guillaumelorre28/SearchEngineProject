
Andrew Flintoff <https://www.theguardian.com/sport/andrew-flintoff> has 
tweeted that he is out of Lancashire’s T20 match against Derbyshire at Old 
Trafford on Friday evening with a calf injury.

“Devastated I’m not playing tonight, gone in the calf! Onwards and upwards I 
will be back though. No one said it would be easy,” the 36-year-old said.

The former England all-rounder had made his comeback a fortnight ago, taking 
two wickets as Lancashire crushed Worcestershire by 50 runs 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2014/jul/06/andrew-flintoff-lancashire-worcestershire-t20-blast>
.

After that game he had tweeted: “Even better 2nd time round and a touch 
harder!” followed by: “It’s a bit like sex always better 2nd time!”

He was also in the team who beat Leicestershire by 18 runs last Friday at 
Grace Road where he made only one run but took three wickets.Lancashire 
<https://www.theguardian.com/sport/lancashire> top the North Group ahead of 
their traditional rivals Yorkshire.
